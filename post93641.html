Lakshmi Hayagrivar

by indhiranis on Wed Aug 24, 2005 3:30 pm

In Thiruvanthi puram (Thiruvahindipuram) near Kadalur,
Panrutti, the main God is Lakshmi Hayagrivar. God for
Education, knowledge. White lotus offered to Lakshmi
Hayagrivar.

Lakshmi Hayagrivar temple is in a small hillock, which
is believed tobe a part of Sanjeevi Malai. No plants.
it looks in a different colour.

Devanatha Swamy is also there. If we are not able to
visit Tirupathi, we can pay our venduthals here and
he will accept.

In Thiruvanthi puram (Thiruvahindipuram) near Kadalur,
Panrutti, the main God is Lakshmi Hayagrivar. God for
Education, knowledge. White lotus offered to Lakshmi
Hayagrivar.

Lakshmi Hayagrivar temple is in a small hillock, which
is believed tobe a part of Sanjeevi Malai. No plants.
it looks in a different colour.

Devanatha Swamy is also there. If we are not able to
visit Tirupathi, we can pay our venduthals here and
he will accept.
