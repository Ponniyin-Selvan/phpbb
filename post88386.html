Common Thread in Kalki's Works

by nuburangaleni on Sat Nov 03, 2007 11:29 am

VANAKKAM,

This is my first mail to the group.interesting topic to analyse as tis seems analysing kalki himself........

"FORESTS" are highlighted in kalki's works.many interesting and some turning points in the novel takes place in forests.eg.
in PS
1.ilaiya pandian MUDISOOTU VAIBAVAM by NANDINI in THIRUPURAMBIYAM PALLIPADAI.
2.vanthiyathevan's first meeting with arulmozhivarmar happens in a forest.
3.in third part nandini meeting rakkammal ,poonguzhali and amuthan driving ravidasan into pudhaikuzhi.

in parthiban kanavu
1."vikraman in disguise as a vairavyabaari meets some unexpected dangers because of kullan"in second part .this also takes place in a forest.
2."sivanadiyaar discloses that he is nobody other than the maamalla chakravarthi himself to ponnan " in a forest.
3.neelakesi kidnaps arulmozhi rani and holds her in a forest where later sivanadiyaar goes and finds nearer to a waterfalls.

in sivahaamiyin sabatham
1.naaganandi takes away sivagami after she comes out of the surangapathai along with aayanar and enters a graveyard "
and sivahaami's home itself is forest.

in kalvanin kaadhali
1.muthaiyan after transforming into a thief lives in forest.and he is caught by the police in forest.

another hold is "YAANAI"
1.in PS , veerapadiya aabathuthavivahalin sathi towards arulmozhivarmar in the form of yaanai in thiruvaarur,aniruthar threatening vaanathi while she travels to nagai soodaamani vihaaram,in second part ponniyin selvan's most of the journey is on yaanai as yaanaipagan evan in the last when he turns the elephant violent and escapes from the gang with poonguzhali ,etc.even in kanavu poonguzhali travels over "INDIRANATHU IRAVATHAM".
2.in SS the very first scene in which paranjothi saves sivagaami.
3.in PK when paarthiban is nearing his last moments.his PATTATHU YAANAI dies witha huge trumpet.


OTHER THING TO BE NOTED IS IN EVERY HISTORICAL NOVEL THERE WILL BE A POOR FAMILY CLOSE TO THE KING'S FAMILY (KNOWING ALL THE RAJARAHASIYANGAL) AND WORKING FOR THEIR WELFARE.
eg
1.so many characters in PS ,senthan amuthaninn thaayar loyal to sembiyan maadevi,murugaiyan who later holds bakthi on ilavarasar amv,poonguzhali close to ilavarasar arul mozhi varmar, all ordinary amilies
2."PONNAN,VALLI,VEERABATHIRA AACHAARI" in paarthiban kanavu ,ordinary padhotti and kammaalan.
3."KANNAN,KAMALI,THAATHA" vey close to mahendrar and maamallar-arasar's ratha saarathi


i haven't read other books of kalki.eager to know more.........
