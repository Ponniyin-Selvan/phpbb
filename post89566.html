--- Inponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Muthu Prakash R"
wrote:
While the murder of AK is a historical fact, I think we are mixing
the
facts with the fiction of PS.
1. Nandhini is a fictious character and no reason to assume that she
existed.
2. Ravidasan is a historical character who was punished for the
murder
of AK.
3. There is no supporting evidence that the murder took place in
kadambur palace. It could've been Kanchi or even Tanjai. No proof
here.
4. Again, whether the murder weapon was a vaal(sword) or a knife is
not a concrete fact. It can be poison or treachery of some other kind.
5. Timing of the murder is also not clear. As someone mentioned,
there
are not many inscriptions for this time period and the year of murder
has a 3 year range.
