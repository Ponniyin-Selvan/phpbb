by sridhar_rathinam on Mon May 17, 2004 7:02 pm

Dear all
One of the things I find really fascnating in Kalkis naration is making his charectors so human...They are not idealogical mythological charectors..
I didnt understand this when I was a kid and have wondered how arulmozhi could dream of poonguzhali and fall in love with vanathi....or poonguzhali dream of arulmozhi and marry senthan...

arulmozhi saying he doesnt want the kingdom and then changing his tune to try to free his freind

Kanthamaaran and parthibendran changing their allegance after meeting nandhini

thats all so natural we change as the circumstances around us change.....Everything is not love or romance we think they are and only when you look back like the autograph style do yu realise hoe much of t was just infactuation and thats what kalki clearly potryas in his charectors....

its natural for an youngster to dream of a beautiful woman thats what his heros vandhiyadevan and arulmozhi do.....but when it coes to serious love they stick to their woman....

opinions welcome

Romance and Love Kalki style

by privj on Fri May 21, 2004 5:27 pm

Hi,

nalla doubt. ennai poruthavarai Arulmozhi poonguzhali than mel
anbu vaithirupadhaga thaan ennukirar.Avar vandiyadevan irukum
kappaluku padagil sellumpodhu poonguzhaliyin kangalil irundhu
kanneer varugiradhu.Appodhu avar poonguzhaliyidam "kankalai
thudaithukol yaaravadhu paarthal enna ninaiparkal? enkirar. Anbu
illai enral aval en avarai piriyum podhu kanner vida vendum?
adhanaal avar avvaru nambukirar. Aval atthaiyan nilamaiku vazhi
theda paarkiral enbadhellam arulmozhiku theriyaadhu.
Melum madakini irakum podhu aranmanaiyil ullavar ellarum sundara
cholar pizhathathu, avaral nadakamudindhadhu patri sandhosham
adaikindranar. Appodhum poonguzhali than athai ipdi poivittale enru
varuthamadaikiraal. Arulmozhi appodhu "Un athai en thanthaiyai 2
times kapatrinaal" enkirar. Appodhu dhaan arasa kulathavarin
selfishness avaluku purikindradhu. Apuram aval manathai matri kondu
vidukiral.Arasa kudumbathavaridam vaikum anbu waste enru
poonguzhaliku than atthai moolamaga therindhu vidukiradhu. Idhu
Arulmozhiku puriya vialli.Adhanaal Vandiya devanidam "Sendanai
manamuvandhu dhaan thirumanam seidhu kondaala?" enru ketkirar.

Arulmozhi thanaku kadalil payanam seithu thora desangaluku sella
vedum enra aasai irukiradhu enru poonguzhaliyidam solkirar. Appodhu
poonguzhali thanakum ammadhiri aasai irupadhaaga solgiraal.Melum
vandiyadevan, poonguzhali, arulmozhi ivarkal moovarum erkanave
serndhu payanam seidhavarkal. Andha groupil oruvar missing enral
avarum vandhaal nanrai irukum enru solvadhu pol dhaan solkirar.
idharku innoru aadharamum irukiradhu. Vasandham vandhadhu chapteril
sendhanamudhan,poonguzhali, sembiyan madeviyudan arulmozhi
kodikaraiku purapadu kirar. appodhu avarkal vandiyadevanai kooti
poga mudiyavillaye enru varuthapattukondathaaga kundavai
vandiyadevanidam solgiral. so arulmozhi poonguzhaliyai kettadhu
vandiyadevanai kettadhu maadhiri dhaan. Avarai ponra aasaigal
avalukum irupadhal apdi ketkirar.

Okva?
