by sps10142004 on Sat Oct 07, 2006 8:32 pm

Dear Friends,

In 985 AD, Rajaraja ascended the Chozha Throne. In 1012 AD, Rajendra
was also made the King. After 1014 AD, 29th yr of ascendence from 985
AD, we do not have any records of RRC..

Rajaraja Chozhan commenced construction of the Brahadeswara Temple,
Thanajvur in the year 1006 and completed it in 1010 AD.

The 1000th year of commemoration has been planned to be celebrated last
year itself and that is the reason why we created our website
"www.thebigtemple.com"

Our enthusiasitc member Kalinga Balu is planning to organise a Dance -
drama and photo-exhibition along with sale of Tamil culture-related
gift items - like Thanjavur plates / paintings / Naachiar vilakku etc in
Bhuvaneswar by end November involving Bharathi Tamil Sangam,
Bhuvaneswar.

Dear Dhivakar has shown interest to conduct a similar programe in Vizag
where he is planning to involve Vizag Tamil Sangam.

Mrs. Uma, Mumbai has almost completed arrangements for the Dance - Drama
taking guidance of the well-known Dance Artistes.

Obviously conducting such programmes needs support - including
financial support from various sources.

Those interested to support individually / collectively/ through
Sponsors are requested to give some indication through private email
to Kalingatamil Balasubramanian or Mrs. Uma.

anbudan / sps

Hi all

Like rajaraja and rajarajeshwaram there is a aaditya cholan and a
aathithEcharam.
now its called kalappaal.
thiruthuraipoondi to mannargudi( 3rd km)

this temple was built by aadityan s/o vijayalayan

but how far from cauvery the locals have to tell me.

there are three temples on the bank of mulliyaaru

a pandya kalvettu of kulasekharan says 'kalappaal udaiyaar
aathithEcharamudaiyaar'

thiruvisaipaa is there about this temple. also one of the 63
naayanmaar kuutruva naayanaar was born here.
