Thirumazhabaadi - 2 - Pillayar Madiyil

by Guest on Fri Jul 18, 2008 2:21 pm

Posted by Subramaniam Sankaran using id Subramaniam Sankaran

Hi,

The Goddess on the lap of Great Pillayar, is Vallabhai. Vallabha Ganapathi,
is one of the many forms of Lord Pillayar.

People who practice of Srividhya upasaka, will well know him, as he is the
first step into it. The dhyana Shloka, is as follows,

Bheeja pooragadekshukarmukarujaa chakrabja pasthpala,
Vrehygraswavishana rathna kalasa prodhyuth karamboruha,
Dhyoyo *vallabhaya* cha padmakarayaslishto jwalath bhooshaya,
Viswothpathi vinasa samsthithikaro vigno visishtarthadha.

The famous carnatic number, Vallabha Naayakasya, in Ragam Begada, by Great
Muthuswami Dhikshatar, is on this form of Ganapathi.

Regards
Subramaniam Sankaran
