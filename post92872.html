Dear Arun & Sri

You disagree Sivakaami as an egoist. In my mail I have
pointed out as

1.Sivakaami's words and deeds hurt Maamallaa many a
times.
2. Kalki himself could not resist describing the
discomfort felt by Maamalla by Sivakaami's behaviour.
3.Sivakaami is a heroine having some elements of
Nilaambari.

Now I want to add

1.Sivakaami wanted a mommoth war to break out and the
war to be led by Maamalla. This is not entirely bcas
of patriotism .
2. Maamalla had to prepare for the war for about nine
years with an additional stress of retriving Sivakaami
without any harm.
3.Sivakaami, at one time or other, creates problems
for the main characters - Maamallaa, Mahendraa,
Paranjothi and Aayanar.
4.Sivakaami doesn't seem to understand the regular
royal obligations, a prince will have.

I have a question about Sivakaami. Is she not a
cursed girl?

She is introduced to us in an accident. Her
Arangetram is aborted. Her lover being royal always
intangible. She had the cruel eyes of Naaganandhi on
her hosting tons of trouble. She became a hostage. At
Vaathaapi her artistic skills are mocked at. She lost
all her youth in jail - a oophing 9 years.
She rarely had happy moments in that big Novel.

But Arun & Sri, all my observations may be from a
fixed viewpoint of mine. You can give reasons for a
different stand bcas I still want to like Sivakaami's
paaththirapadaippu.

I agree with your comparison of Vaanathi and
Sivakaami. But Sivakaami is the puniest character
among all female characters of PS and anybody can be
easily be better than Vanathi. Dare anybody on earth
can compare Punguzali?

All your adjectives for Sivakaami and tons more like
that may not be sufficient for Punguzali. Ippo
