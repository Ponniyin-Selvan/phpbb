RAJENDRA HAD 3 SONS : THEY RULED BETWEEN 1044 - 1070.

Rajakesari RAJATHIRAJAN :: 1018 - 1054. Co-ruled with Rajendra from
1018 to 1044. Defeated Chalukyas in 1044-45. He died in 1054 in
KOPPAM BATTLE On the banks of Krishna River against Agavamallan.
Was also known as King Who died on the Elephant.

Parakesari RAJENDRA CHOZHA DEVAR :: 1052 - 1064. Co-ruled with
Rajadithan till he died in 1054. In the same Koppam battle Field,
he pronounced himself as the King and defeated Jayasimhan,
Pulikesin, Dasapanman & Nanni Nulamban and killed them. Agavamallan
left the battle field. He waged a war against Lanka too.

Rajakesari RAJAMAHENDRAN :: S/o aforersaid Rajendra Chozha Devar.
Was Yuvaraja 1060 - 1073.

Rajakesari VEERARAJENDRAN :: 1063 - 1069. Also known as Karikalan.

Parakesari ADHIRAJENDRAN :: 1069 - 1070 S/o Veerarajendran.
============================================
From here, Rajendra's DAUGHTER's Roots took over ::
-----------------------------------------------
KULOTHUNGA I was born to RJC's daughter AMMANGA DEVI and RAJARAJA
NARENDRAN (ruled Vengi 1018 - 1059) in 1043 - Star Poosam. He was
also named as Rajendran after RJC in conformity with Chozha clan.
ALSO WAS KNOWN AS VISHNUVARDHANAN - VII.

There was one VIJAYADITHAN - VII, born to VIMALADITHAN, thro" the
step mother of Rajaraja Narendran. He assisted RR Narendran.
Sakthivarman was s/o Vijayadithan VII. KULOTHUNGA was already
declared Yuvaraja and was in Vengi / Chakrakottam.

After Adhirajendra's death in 1070, KULOTHUNGA rushed to GANGAI
KONDA CHOZHAPURAM and BECAME CHOZHA KING.
Aforesaid Vijayaditha VII was ruling Vengi.

After this, there were various polarisation among Kings in various
parts of Deccan ..

Hi Group,
THis is a interesting discussion.
Yes , the current senior prince and Junior prince(my Brother went to
school with junior prince) in Thanjavur palace are King Sarfoji
descendents. And I think the current palace was built during marathi
kings period.
SPS Sir, do you know if there is any evidence that part of original
RRC period fort structure in existence?

Speaking of descendents of Chola Dynasty, My uncle used say that we
are descendents of Kulothunga Chola when I was a kid( He used to
brag, Nanga Kulothunga chola parambarai la vanthavanga!!!

I never got a chance to talk to my uncle about the above statement
after I grew up. But when I traced my genealogy with my father, we
were only able to trace our genealogy for past 7 or 8 generations.
All these people have lived in the places in between Madurai, Trichy
and Thanjavur.

BTW, Since I am new to this group, let me introduce myself. I am
Shirley, originaly from Thanjavur( my beloved home town)but
currently I live in US. I joined this group for 2 reasons. One, My
love for Amarar Kalki's books and Two, My love for Indian History.

Though I am Engineer, I love history. And I really appreciate the
experts knowledge in this group.

The first tamil novel I ever read was Kalki's PS. Instantly I fell
in love with his work. Then I read PK,SS, Solaimalai Ilavarasi, Alai
Osai.. etc
I'll end this by saying I really enjoy being in this group and
appreciate all your opinions.
rgs,
Shirley
