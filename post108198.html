Temple : Arulmigu Koneswarar Thirukovil, Kodavaayil

This weeks episode is about "Arulmigu Koneswarar Thirukovil, Kodavayil".

Audio Link :
http://podbazaar.castmetrix.net/download/144115188075857226/1/TamilPodcast70VaaramOruAlayamArulmiguKoneswararThirukovilKodavaayil.mp3
Audio Duration : 6m 37 sec

Location&Direction:This temple is situated in Thivarur District and located at Thivarur-kumbakonam bus route.

Current Name : Kodavaasal
MainDeity : Koneswarar
Godesses : Periyanayaki
Theertham : Amrutha Theertham
Pathigam : Thirugnanasambantha swamigal
Holy Tree : Banana

Darshan Time : Morning 8am- 12pm Evening 5.00pm-8pm


During
deluge when land was drowned by ocean water, in order to save vedhas
they were placed in a pot and was coverd with a Shiva lingam as a Lid.
After few days this pot
reached Kodamooku near kumbakonam. As
days passed by, this pot broke into three parts, the bottom part landed
in Kumbakonam, Middle part reached ThiruKalayanallur, and the top part
along with the Lid reached Kudavaayil which is now called as Kudavaasal.
