by satish_arun on Thu Feb 10, 2005 3:12 pm

Dear SPS,

We can fully understand your feelings and accept your points. What
you say is correct. But as Saravanan pointed out, due to practical
reasons all 500 members cannot participate in such movements.
First and foremost thing, many people, including me, dont know what
to do. When we come across such situations, we dont know whom to
approach and what to do. Even if, out of interest, we move forward,
an individual do not have the much needed backup. To be precise, do
you think it would have been possible for me, as an individual, to
have met and interacted with people like Rajavelu of ASI? Never. Only
through our mentors like you,Dr.KK etc. we come across such people.

What I am trying to say is, please dont get discouraged by poor
response. What you people are doing is something great, which others
cannot do. Show people the path, and I am sure there will be good
number following. If need be you can send out a mail calling for a
meeting at some place where the interested people can meet and
discuss.

Not responding at one point of time doesnt mean there is no
involvement. Due to various reason, a person might not be abel to
contribute. I take the trip we made as an example. When the plan was
told, people like me, Shankaran,Sasi,Sriram,et.al, the first timers
were immediately ready to join the group. But only after going there
we came to know that almost everyweek(or rather every month), the
varalaaru team goes for a trip. We were a BIT puzzled by this and
asked why other trips are not told to the group. We were told that,
initially they did, but since no one joined, they stopped circulating
the news. If you remember, when we decided for the first time, to
meet and discuss about small trips, at marina, last december, few of
us joined, and Shankaran and I along with Kamal made a trip then, and
again we both joined for the Tanjai trip. Only if what is being done
is spread or shared, things will happen. I may not be as dedicated as
varalaaru team, but if I know, I can join them atleast 25% of the
time. Atleast 25% participation is better that nil participation.

So, if you tell people what to be done, atleast a few will respond.
Probably the same set of people cannot contribute everytime. Each
time a new member will surely be interested. So after a long run, we
can see that all 500 of us had contributed atleast something for the
cause. Ramar palathula anil seva sencha madhiri......you people
continue to build the paalam, many 'anils' will join you.

Regards,
Satish
