Dear Friends,

Thanks to the initiative of our dear Vijay and efforts of dear Thiru,
English version of Ponniyin Selvan (first Two volumes) is now
available free for the benefit of our Group members ...and Thiru is
uploading the same for us here.

As most of you are aware, the translation is done under PROJECT
MADURAI wherein our dear Member Pavithra Srinivasan has also made
enormous contribution..

Project Madurai is the brain child of Dr. Kalyan, well renowned
Scholar in the Web... more-particularly Tamil Web.

On behalf of all the Group members, myself along with other
Moderators, place on record our sincere thanks to all involved in
Project Madurai and all connected in making this available to us.

Greetings.

sps .. for on behalf of all the Moderators.
