Stone carving Narasimha at Mangalagiri:
 
One of the finest rock-cut temple is 'Panaka Narasimha swamy Temple at Mangalagiri, Near Vijayawada. The Hill Temple was also known for 'miracle happening' that Swamy would be taking only half of Panakam what-ever measure is offered.
 
The Rock stone carving statue of Narasimha Swamy is very famous in Coastal Andhra. Just last month I was there and had a nice dharshan. Previously the steep steps to the hill temple were little harder for people to climb (previously I have used twice these route). But Government has laid a road now straight up to Temple.
 
Mangalagiri is 20 kms south from Vijayawada towards Chennai NH and 17 kms from Guntur towards North. Temple was probably 12th century temple but norecords on that period. But during Vijayanagaram period, King Chellappa won over Gajapathi Kings at Mangalagiri and donated jewels and lands, probably during 15th century end. There were some inscriptions and copper plates on this.
