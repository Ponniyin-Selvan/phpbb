Yeah.. Uthirapatheeswarar..

then Nallur Kalyanasundareswarar - Avvanname mudiyai nam thalai mel
vaithan (as indicated earlier Lord siva hath put his Lotus feet on
my Head - hymned Narukkarasar);

Chandramouleeswarar in Thiruvakkarai.

these forms should have been depicted with Local Legends.

As dear Gokul mentioned Dr. Kudavoil's THIRUVARUR is extensively
dealing with this temple. In this book Rajendra's grants
(inscriptions) for making golden roof of the temple figures. (Ref
Archieves too).

One Scholar a month is a good suggestion.

We can take up certain published materials earlier and put them for
ready reference of our members also - Scholar-wise .

Airavatham Mahadevan, Dr. Nagaswamy, Dr. Kalaikkovan, Dr. Rajavelu,
Dr. Sathiamurthy, Nadana Kasinathan, Dr. Kudavoil, Dr. TS Sridhar
and the like.

We will try to work on this also.

Today's Junior Vikadan reported that Sembian Kandiyur excavations
are bringing out "Cauvery" Nagareegam (Like Sindh Civilisation,
Cauvery Civilisation - near Mayavaram).

Pls check for more details.
