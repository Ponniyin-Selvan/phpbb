Waare wah! Sreeram, the casting- particularly the Hollywood part of it was
cool:-) My half a cent's worth- we could put in Sivakumar(when he was young)
for Arulmozhi's part.

As for Manirathnam directing PS- right from the time the story starts, from
the midnight conference in Kadambur- everything will be in darkness. All the
conversations will be in fits and starts, and characters will talk with
curious jerks. Example:

When Kundavai meets VD in the siraisaalai (3rd paagam), and swears on her
love to him:


The place is in darkness. Sounds of tigers growling, and gaurds talking in
the distance. Kundavai's face can be seen dimly, beyond the bars. VD is
staring at her out of wide eyes. Perspiration gleams on his forehead.

Kundavai: onnu theriyumaa?

VD: enna? enna?

Kundavai: maattom. nenaikka maattom.

VD (blinks): enna solringa? aang?

K: puriyalai?
(she lowers her head and suddenly grins)

K: ungalai nenaicitten. vera yaaraiyum nenaikka maatten.

VD (dumbstruck): aaahhh...

K: enna purushaa? okvaa?

VD faints.

***********

))Not that Manirathnam won't do a good job out of it. He has his good
points (alaipayuthe is one of my all-time favourites). This conversation was
just a joke, so Sreeram, please don't take offense.

Sathya ma'am...ada, 1,2,3 sonna udane vanthittengale? ungalukkuthaan 1st
prize.))

Love,
Pavithra.
