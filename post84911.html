29th - 30th Trip details

by adhitha_karikalan on Tue Jan 25, 2005 9:16 pm

Hi All,

The 29th trip starts from Kumbakonam. Though the necessary details are
conveyed to the participants, I am recaping here for those who haven't
planned yet and possible to make it at the last moment.

Saturday we all assemble at Hotel Chellas Kumbakonam and start the trip by
8:30 am. Dr.Kudavoil Balu is going to guide us on that day and possibly
visit Pazhaiyaarai, Nandhipuram, Thiruvalanjuzhi, Thirukkandiyur,
Irattaikkoyil (Pallippadai of Sundarachola) and Sendhalai. Saturday night we
will be staying in Thanjavur (Ganesh lodge).

Sunday morning the function is scheduled between 10 am to 12 noon. The venue
is the new ASI office located between Rajarajan and Keralandhakan
thiruvaayil. In the afternoon we will visit periya koyil under the guidance
of scholars. It's a rare opportunity.

Please contact me at 98842-01210 or Lavanya at 98410-09327 if you are
joining. This will help us to decide what type of vehicle to be arranged.

Thanks
Kamal
