Thank you sir, very kind of you.

But I feel Irandam yathirai is not the apt word, because there were so
many yathirais, big and small in the past years. I think the first
group who made mudhal yathirai (atleast a part of them) made further
trips before consolidating into the current varalaaru team. And later
there was one big trip to Tanjai and surrounding places and lots of
small trips by few members now and then.

So better we call it PS yathira, without number and avoid confusions.

Dear Vishwak Senan sir,

We announced two programmes.

One is MALLAI TRIP...

This is planned on the 7th Sept. Morning 0730 hrs about 14 members
confirmed so far, will leave in a Temp traveller and another small
private car - to Mallai from mandaveli Bus Terminus (via) Nandanam
Arts, Saidapet,Governor Bunglow, Adyar Bakery, Thiruvanmiyur - New
Mahabalipuram Road.

Break fast will be at Arusuvai - near Maayajal.

To reach Mallai at about 0930 hrs.

Prof Swaminathan (Retd IIT) will be guiding us.

Return journey may commence at about 4 pm.

The Second ( THIRD to be APPROPRIATE) Yaathirai will be in Oct. Date
yet to be decided.

We will definitely visit Uppliappan temple and pick u up in this trip
and drop you back after going around PS related places.

Will communicate to you in detail later ( after a fortnight or so) on
this.

best regards / sps
