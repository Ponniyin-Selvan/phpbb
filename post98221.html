PS Serial - lighter

by saranspondy on Wed Oct 05, 2005 3:34 pm

I personally pray they dont take the serial....

A few days back in Vikatan, Sujatha Rangaraajan had
written beautifully,

"Myself and Manirathnam have crafted a three hour
screenplay for ponniyin selvan.
But the problem is...
bookkai padiththa ovvoruvarukkum avaravargal
manadhil..
nandhii ippadiththaan iruppaaal
vandhiyathevan ippadithhtan irukkavendum

appadeennu oru image irukkum...
It is impossible to satisfy everybody who has read the
book... Even then, if they take a movie.... some
tamizhan living in arabia would coolly say, "padam
bookku alavakku illai...yean bookla 30% effect kooda
illai.."

I think Sujatha has written aptly...

Sujatha-manirathnam ezhuthara scriptukkey ippadeenna
TV Serialellam entha moolaikku..

I just dont want somebody to see the movie/serial
without reading the book and say... What the hell of a
story is this... !!

Heart vedichurum...
I mean it..

shankar

Dear all,

Great to see lots of things happening in the group. Feeling bad that
I am not of any use here. My work is keeping me off the mails as
well. Checking only once in 2-3 days. Anyway, goodluck to all and
hope "ramarukku anil" madhiri, I too contribute something. Lets see.

Diwakar, Your opinion that a good director can make things happen is
optimistic. But the word "GOOD" is whats going to make the
difference.

I remember Mr.Kumaravel's words during our Vikraman meet - "Show
mudincha udana kamal dressing roomukku vandhu romba paratinaru.
immediately he asked, mylaporelenrdhu mamiellam vandhirupangale.
amam sir vandhanganu sonnen. udane avar, kayila PS bookoda
vandhirupangale. avangala satisfy pannave mudiyathuppa. adhanalathan
naan drop panniten".

A giant in his arena, with such a comment, should give a second
thought to the directors.

Regards,

gave the dubbing .... mavane.. nijamaa puneet essar wud not have
been able to give that voice and modulation... ennaoru veri ..
aakrosham inthat voice.....

so there are lot of talented guys .... summaa kamal sonnaan....
sujatha sonnaan.. nnu ellam bayandhu nikkaradhu madathanam....
