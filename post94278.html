Dear PS Lovers,
Yes.Its a gud thought coz i have had practical experience of this. My family roots are from kallikudy village near thiruvarur. In that small village, there is a sivan temple.It was very old.Nobody could find when i was built.But it was in a very dilapidated state.nobody used to visit tat temple. I used to feel sad for tat. But 2 years back, my family took the initiative of giving a facelift to tat temple.Putting the burden on the lord, we started the work.Initially, the villagers said tat instead of faelifting an old temple, let us build a new mariamman temple. We did not accede to their request. Finally things started moving and we got help from all quarters(excluding the govt). And the kumbabishekham was conducted on june 9th 2003. From: then on, there is a dedicated kurukkal for this temple and he conducts 3 poojas daily(moonru kaala poojai).
Like this i have seen many such temples. I want to help them out to come back to their previous glory.

Regards,
Mouli
