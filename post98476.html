by suresh28370 on Sun Dec 28, 2003 8:32 pm

Anna Pura nanooru potrigale..

Pura naanoraiyum kurai solla venam, oruvanukku oruthiyaiyum korai
solla venaam.

namma makkal ethai venallum eppadi venallum interpret pannikkuvanga -
- to suit them.

the time of purananooru is spread over several years of tamil
history. veeram enbathu oru basic requirement of survival.
usuppethha thevai iruntha kaalam. ippa athai vechu "vizhippunarchi"
kondu vara muyarchi pannarangale -- appo are they ready to go back
to those times?
