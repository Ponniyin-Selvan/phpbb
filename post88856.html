Dear Vijay,

Kulothunga I's father was Rajaraja Narendran I of Eastern Chalukya.
Mother Ammanga Devi d/o Great Rajendra I.
Rajaraja Narendran was born to Vimaladithan, whose wife was Kundavi
D/o RRC - I.

Therefore Kulothunga I (also known as Vikramaditha VII - Birth Star
POOSAM) is GRANDSON of Rajendra I through his daughter. He was also
Grandson of Kundavi (D/o RRC) + Vimaladithan ! He was Yuvaraja-
designate of VENGI. Rajaraja Narendra ruled VENGI from 1018 - 1059.

There was one VIJAYADITHA VII.

He was son of VIMALADITHA, not through Kundavi, but another Wife.
His son was SAKTHIVARMAN. They assisted Rajaraja Narendra I- means
Kulothunga's father and positioned in VENGI.

In 1060, Narendra died. Kulothunga did not prefer to succeed the
throne. His Sithappa VIJAYADITHA VII occupied the throne.
Kulothunga invaded farther North - Sakkarakottam etc.

Chalukya Vikramaditha VI invaded Vengi. Veera Rajendran (1063 -
1070) s/o Rajendra I interfered, defeated Vikramaditha VI and
restored Vijayaditha VII.

When Veera Rajendran died in 1070, Vijayaditha VII, put Parakesari
Adhi Rajendran, s/o Veera Rajendran, in GKC Chozha throne. Adhi
Rajendra also died in 1070.

Now KULOTHUNGA returned to Gangai Konda Chozhapuram (GKC) from
Sakkarakottam and Crowned himself as Chozha King.

Vijayaditha VII continued as VENGI King.

Dr. Rasamanickanar, in his Chozhar Varalaru, whole heartedly
appreciates KAN Sastry ::

Dr. Rasamanickanar concludes:

1. Vengi inscriptions describe Kulothunga prior to becoming Chozha
king in a particular way;
2. Vikramanga Deva Saritham describes the same in another way;
3. Kalingathuparani describes in a different way.

It is Sastry, who went into every detail and arrived at the true
picture ! Ref Sastry's The Colas (Pages 338 - 358 ).

Field,

left the battle field. He waged a war against Lanka too.

-----------------------------------------------
KULOTHUNGA I was born to RJC's daughter AMMANGA DEVI and RAJARAJA
NARENDRAN (ruled Vengi 1018 - 1059) in 1043 - Star Poosam. He was
also named as Rajendran after RJC in conformity with Chozha clan.
ALSO WAS KNOWN AS VISHNUVARDHANAN - VII.
=========================================

There was one VIJAYADITHAN - VII, born to VIMALADITHAN, thro" the
step mother of Rajaraja Narendran. He assisted RR Narendran.

==============================================

Sakthivarman was s/o Vijayadithan VII. KULOTHUNGA was already
declared Yuvaraja and was in Vengi / Chakrakottam.

After Adhirajendra's death in 1070, KULOTHUNGA rushed to GANGAI
KONDA CHOZHAPURAM and BECAME CHOZHA KING.
Aforesaid Vijayaditha VII was ruling Vengi.

After this, there were various polarisation among Kings in
various
parts of Deccan ..

KULOTHUNGA CHOLA 1

Kulothunga Chola 1 is partly eastern Chalukya also known as Vengi
Chalukyas

The first ruler of the Vengi Chalukya dynasty is Kubja Vishnuvardana
(624 AD to 641AD)

The 23rd ruler of this dynasty was Vimaladitya (1011 AD to 1018AD)

This Vimaladitya married Kundavai who was the daughter of our great
Raja Raja Chola and the sister of King Rajendra Chola 1.

Vimaladitya's son was Raja Raja Narendra.So for Raja Raja Narendra
His thaatha -Raja Raja Chola
His Amma -Kundavai
His Thaai Maaman -Rajendra Chola 1

With the help of his maternal uncle Rajendra Chola 1, Raja Raja
Narendra(1018 AD to 1061AD) became the 24 th ruler of the Vengi (or
Eastern) Chalukya Dynasty.

This Raja Raja Narendra married Ammangai (his maama Ponnu) daughter
of Rajendra Chola 1.

The son of Raja Raja Narendra was Rajendra Chalukya (Kulothunga Chola
1). Rajendra Chalukya spent much of his day in his maama veedu(Gangai
Konda Cholapuram)

Rajendra Chlaukya(Kulothunga 1) married Madurantaki Devi the daughter
of Rajendra Chola 2 (1054AD to 1063AD)

Rajendra Chola 2 had a brother -Virarajendra Chola and a son Raja
Mahendra.
Raja Mahendra died earlier before Rajendra Chola 2.

Vira Rajendra Chola was crowned as the Chola king in GangaiKonda
Cholapuram.

After the death of Vira Rajendra Chola his son Adhirajendra Chola was
assasinated in 1070AD.

With the assasination of Adhirajendra Chola there were no successors
from this Chola line.So Rajendra Chalukya ascended the Chola throne
as Kulothunga Chola 1.

Kulothunga Chola 1 also witnessed the end of the eastern Chalukya
dynasty.The last ruler of vengi Chaukya dynasty was Vijayaditya 7
(stepbrother of Kulothunga Chola's father RajaRaja Narendra).

With the death of Vijayaditya 7 in 1075 AD the eastern Chalukya
dynasty came to an end and vengi became a part of the
Chola empire.

Dear Vijay,

Kulothunga I's father was Rajaraja Narendran I of Eastern
Chalukya.
Mother Ammanga Devi d/o Great Rajendra I.
Rajaraja Narendran was born to Vimaladithan, whose wife was
Kundavi
D/o RRC - I.

Therefore Kulothunga I (also known as Vikramaditha VII - Birth
Star
POOSAM) is GRANDSON of Rajendra I through his daughter. He was
also
Grandson of Kundavi (D/o RRC) + Vimaladithan ! He was Yuvaraja-
designate of VENGI. Rajaraja Narendra ruled VENGI from 1018 -
1059.

There was one VIJAYADITHA VII.

He was son of VIMALADITHA, not through Kundavi, but another
Wife.
His son was SAKTHIVARMAN. They assisted Rajaraja Narendra I-
means
Kulothunga's father and positioned in VENGI.

In 1060, Narendra died. Kulothunga did not prefer to succeed
the
throne. His Sithappa VIJAYADITHA VII occupied the throne.
Kulothunga invaded farther North - Sakkarakottam etc.

Chalukya Vikramaditha VI invaded Vengi. Veera Rajendran
(1063 -
1070) s/o Rajendra I interfered, defeated Vikramaditha VI and
restored Vijayaditha VII.

When Veera Rajendran died in 1070, Vijayaditha VII, put
Parakesari
Adhi Rajendran, s/o Veera Rajendran, in GKC Chozha throne.
Adhi
Rajendra also died in 1070.

Now KULOTHUNGA returned to Gangai Konda Chozhapuram (GKC) from
Sakkarakottam and Crowned himself as Chozha King.

Vijayaditha VII continued as VENGI King.

Dr. Rasamanickanar, in his Chozhar Varalaru, whole heartedly
appreciates KAN Sastry ::

It is Sastry, who went into every detail and arrived at the
