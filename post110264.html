TREASURE TROVE: Gold Coins that were found on a private land at a
village in Dharmapuri district. Â—

Dharmapuri: A treasure pot containing 131 stone-age gold coins found
on a private land was handed over to the Dharmapuri District
Collector, P. Amudha, here on Wednesday.

The pot was found by the land owner, Kamal, wife of Nadimadhu of
Kalkoodapatti village at Palakkode taluk in Dharmapuri district, while
digging pits for laying foundation last week. People conveyed the
information to the Palakkode tahsildar, Duke Ponnuraj, on Wednesday.
Mr. Ponnuraj asked Mrs. Kamal to hand over the same to the district
administration.
Value not known

The weight and value of the coins are not known immediately, Mr.
Ponnuraj said.
