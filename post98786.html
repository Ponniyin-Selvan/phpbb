Dear Vijay

Rajaraja did fight the western chalukyas in an attempt to keep them
out of tamilnadu and help the vengi chalukyas...I think the Chalukya
king was Satyasaraya
====================================================
Vimaladithan was installed as Chalukya king after Kundavi d/o RR was
given in marriage to him by the RR. RJ had to rush up every now and
then to keep Vmdn in being dethrowned..

In 1006 AD - writes Sastry - with about 900000 warriors - imagine the
population of Chozha Empire and compare with the figure of the
Army ! - stunning - Rajendra - as Comm-in-Chief, marched inside
Chalukya kingdom - and ransacked the same - throwing away all known
War Desciplines - killed even women, elderly and the children - and
killed all the enemies of Vmdtn. I think Chatyasreyan was killed in
this War only. Hattur (Karnataka) inscriptions talk of these (mis)
doings eloborately. What we consider as a HEROIC act by the
Conqueror is termed as Barbaric by the Conquered.

By this war several things were accomplished ..
After 1006, Rajendra succceeded RR in 1012 as the King.. in RR's life
time .. He was not the c - in -c alone anymore.
Chalukya problems solved during RR / RJ life time ..
The booty was so large and useful, it is said it funded the
construction of Big temple project - Hiranyakarbam - Thulabaram etc.,
in a very big way..

Re Rajaraja's defeat - even DREAM will fear it ..
Rajaraja and defeat are Antonyms ..

It is pertinent to quote the recently (about 15 yrs back)uncovered
Sankrit Inscriptions in Thanjavur :

" One's Sickness runs away with the Lord Shiva's Vibuthi .. Likewise,
the FEAR of the ENEMIES of Rajaraja runs away the moment they adorn
themselves with the PAADHA DHULI of Emperor Rajaraja on their body
and forehead .. and they are free to move around at will ... "

With 900000 (Nine Lakhs) Warriors marching around - and with an army
reaching Udagai (Near Trvndm ) from Thanjavur in a matter of hours -
just to take vengence for the disgrace shown to an Ambassodor of RR -
do you think any one has the guts of confronting Rajaraja ?

"Thirumagal pola peru nilachelvi thamakke yendrurimai . "Meykeerthi..
conquering the Great Kingdoms is an exclusive right of RR ...

The quiz part is very interesting ..
