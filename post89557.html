errors of kalki

by rojatraders on Tue Jul 26, 2005 3:45 am

I wouldnt call them as errors,but would call them as illogical.

1.Why should aditha karikalan commit suicide after hearing that
veerapandiyan is Nandhini's father?What's so earth shaking about this?

2.Mandakini is shown to be in deep love with sundara cholan.But
suddenly she is said to have had illicit relationship with pandya king
and said to have had two children through him.Surprisingly she follows
sundara chola even after it and considers PS as her son.She doesnt
bother about two children born to her.Not even a visit or enquiry
through poonguzali.

3.Maturanthakan is said to be a coward.But suddenly he defeates chinna
paluvettarayar and nearly kills him.Kalki justifies this by saying
that paluvetrrarayar did not fight with his full heart and hesitated
since his daughter would become a widow.But kalki earlier writes that
chinna paluvettarayar did not hesitate to try to kill his elder
brother in the court of sundara cholan,when periya paluvettarayar
confessed he killed aditha karikalan.What happened to that iron will
when maturanthakan fought with him?

4.The reasons for nandhini sparing the life of vandiya thevan is not
clear.

5.Arulmozhi is shown to be taking many unwanted risks.He goes into
fort of mahinda disguised as a mahout for seeing the artworks.This
looks like an image buildup attempt by kalki and not something which
an intelligent commander-in-chief like arulmozhi would have done.

6.Almost all the love affairs are love at first sight.Vanathi-
arulmozhi,kundavai-vanthiyathevan,manimegalai-
vanthiyathevan,paluvettarayar-nandhini,mandakini-sundaracholan...all
are variants of love at first sight.

regards
sampath

Dr.Sridhar Rathnam,

1.Why should aditha karikalan commit suicide after hearing that
veerapandiyan is Nandhini's father?What's so earth shaking about
this?

Kalki doesnt actually till the end say how AD died Pazhuvettarayar
takes the blame but he leaves it as a question mark to the end


***Aditha karikalan dies because nadhini says a secret about veera
pandiyan.She says "Avar enathu....".After knowing this secret aditha
karikalan commits suicide.What could have nandhini told?By the logic
of the story veera pandiyan could have only been father,lover or
uncle to nandhini.Aditha karikalan was under the impression that
veerapandiyan was nandhini's lover.So he wouldnt have commited
suicide hearing that.What else could she have told?

She couldnt have said anything apart from "Avar enathu
appa/lover/uncle/family friend/husband...".None of these is earth
shaking news.Already karikalan suspected veerapandiyan was
nandhini's lover,so had she said 'husband' he wouldnt have been
surprised.So I think this is one major weakness of the story.

2. Mandhakini's liason with the Pandya king was when she was in a
post traumatic stressed state and amnesic....The whole emphasis of
her love for her former lover is showered on arulmozhi....Maybe she
has no recollection of her twins at all

*********Then it means pandya king physically exploited a mentally
traumatic woman.I dont think a tamil king would have done like
that.The availaible options here are

1.mandakini tried to forget sundara chola by having a new life for
herself.

2.pandya king exploited a mentally affected woman

If first is the case,then mandahini should have remembered her
children.That did not happen.If second is the case then it means
veerapandiya's character is very bad.

3. Stress and Adrenaline will make a man do anything because it is
life or death so Madhuranthagan fighting with vigour is acceptable
and chinna Pazhuvettarayar unsheaths his sword but did not defeat
periayavar...

****mathuranthakan could have had vigour,but where did he get the
technique from?And we have to remember that kandamaran had thrown a
spear on mathuranthaka and maturanthaka was literally shown as
dead.Further maturanthakas body is shown as falling from a steep
waterfall.Kalki titles that chapter as 'maturanthakan maraivu'.

After mathranthakan was hurt like this,he must have taken a long
time to recover.And he learning swordfight and fighting with
paluvettarayar after this accident in a short time looks a BIT
doubtful.He fought with paluvettarayar on the coronation day of
uthama chola,which happened quickly after this incident.

we can say the spear hurt maturanthaka only marginally and he
escaped unhurt and learn warfare with vigour.

But kalki writes kandamaran threw the spear with full force.So we
cannot dismiss the wounds to be light.

errors of kalki

by mprakas on Tue Jul 26, 2005 8:11 pm

While the murder of AK is a historical fact, I think we are mixing the
facts with the fiction of PS.
1. Nandhini is a fictious character and no reason to assume that she
existed.
2. Ravidasan is a historical character who was punished for the murder
of AK.
3. There is no supporting evidence that the murder took place in
kadambur palace. It could've been Kanchi or even Tanjai. No proof here.
4. Again, whether the murder weapon was a vaal(sword) or a knife is
not a concrete fact. It can be poison or treachery of some other kind.
5. Timing of the murder is also not clear. As someone mentioned, there
are not many inscriptions for this time period and the year of murder
has a 3 year range.
