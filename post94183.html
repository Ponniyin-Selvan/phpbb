places to visit:-

Madurai Tour !

by rahul_pentiumiii on Mon Feb 12, 2007 1:58 pm

dont come by bus to Madurai! there is a road building proces going on near
Viluppuram and it delays buses by nearly 4-5 hours. I made a 15 hour trip to
Chennai recently! Only if you are extremely lucky somehow the traffic gets
thru otherwise you are stuck for hours at one spot. My suggestion is you
guys buy tickets in train through Tatkal. This has to be done before 5 days
of travel. It costs a BIT more than the normal ticket but is worth as u can
have a comfortable journey. The only issue here is if u cancel a Tatkal
ticket you loose nearly 95% of the amount. My suggestion is confirm the
members before 5 days, get the money, buy the tickets.

Regards
Rahul
