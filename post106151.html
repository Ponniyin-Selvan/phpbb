Shiv,

Are you are talking about another incident where the same thing
happened to Lord Venkateshwara at Tirupati?

Is it a legend, mentioned in some pasuram or somebody told you ?
All I read is that it is suyambu, At the time of Ramanjua Lord didn't
have Sangu and Chakara, so everybody was claiming that it is their
deity (Shiva, Muruga, etc.,). Ramanuja went and proved that is it
indeed Lord Venkateswara.
All community heads gathered one night, closed the doors of sanctum
and singing. Ramanuja cried and asked Lord to show the world who he
is. Next day morning, he showed Sangu and Chakkara and proved that he
is Lord Venkateshwara.

This could be legend, but I remember reading this in a book I bought
in Tirupati few years back and heard the same through a discourse.

Hope you read venkat's response. As per the deciphering, it is
Govindaraja Permumal in Chidambaram, not Venkateshwara in Tirupati.

Thiru
