but I thought the general feeling was he was killed in the battle in Koppam?
thats another thing i wanted to raise but you went ahead of me...

Though its interesting to believe Rajendra died in Kadaram...it doesnt make sense really does it?
Because the chola tradition is to name people on where they have died
like
Ponmaligai thunjiya thevar:Sundara chola
Yanaimel thunjiyathevar:rajathithan
Merkeruntharuliaya thevar: Kandirathithan

If that is the case Rajendran would have been called Kadaram thunjiya thevar

Second its hard to believe that the king will leave the country for a foriegn campaign in the presence of enemies south west and north
those days a sea voyage would have been a good few months and a campaign would have meant a few years?

What do you all think?


regards


Sri
