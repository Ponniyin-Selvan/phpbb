Very nice Cheenu... back after a long time.. !!

In Vaalikandapuram (near Perambalur), Parantaka period Siva temple,
Dr. Thiagarajan (History H of D of Ariyalur Arts college) has done
extensive work and published a hand book.

One of the tallest Balasubramanian located in the temple !

Like Thiruvellarai Vinnagaram Swastika tank (near Thuraiyur)
exquisite (water) tank is found here also.

Dr. Thiagarajan has found a couple of Eggs of Dyno in this region.
Also several sea shells etc., are found.. It is being postulated
that there was sea till Ariyalur (lakhs of years back !). We also
have a FOSSIL (wood becoming Stone over millions of years) Forest !
Thiruvakkarai (near Tindivanam) / Thiruvalngadu (near Arakonam) are
other places where we have lot of Fossils..

We can also see change of course of water-ways (rivers !) and mass-
burials in Thiruvakkarai.

Dear Venkatesh,

near Thirubuvanai (where opposite sides belong to Tamilnadu and
Pondychery at some portions), there is an inner road where one NANDHI
is found in fallen state. The Nandhi resembles the ones in Thanjavur
Big temple Vimanam (2 in each corners - 8 in all )!

Story is that while it was transported from Thiruvakkarai area, via
Sakadaperuvazhi, and the Nandhi slipped and as a part was broken, was
ignored there itself ! Nearby was a Kallukkadai (Toddy shop) when I
visited this spot 10 years back!

There were three types of roads during Royal regime : One Rajapattai -
prominence for Royal travellers / warriors etc / mass movement),
passenger road and Goods-carrier road Sakadaperuvazhi . Present
Highways mostly Rajapattais. On Cauvery belt and Kollidambelt, the
bund-roads were used for Passenger traffic and Sagadaperuvazhi!

Now we are following Lane - system. Those days, they diverted
Goods-carrier to one road, not affecting passenger / human traffic flow !

If date is finalised, I can request Rajavelu ASI to guide the group.
(subject to his convenience). Sriram also can help us here.

regards/

sps
