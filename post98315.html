maintain it as a archive site and continue with yahoo group.

resetting the password through

by thirumalaikv on Wed Mar 26, 2008 11:14 pm

When I imported the messages to the forum, I purely went by the
subject, if the subject is different then it will be different topic,
so you won't see "Sethusamudhram under subject Nandhini", but as a
side effect if there are two topics with similar it will be merged
even if we intend to keep it seperate, it has to be seperated
manually, but I believe if the topic is same then the messages should
be under same topic.

Another one is removing unncessary quoted text, sometime it is keep
increasing as everybody(Including me) just keep replying without
removing unnecessary text and it is tedious to scroll through all
those, to avoid that when you view the forum now, I by default hide
the quoted text and can be expanded manually. This can be avoided in
the forum, where when you reply, you have two options, just reply or
reply by quote.
