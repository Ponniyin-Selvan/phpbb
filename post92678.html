kalki as a writer

by abhivencat on Tue Nov 06, 2007 2:31 pm

Hi

first its worthwile examining how kalki became a writer in the first
place

3 incidents happened almost within a gap of a year ( 1920-21) in
kalki's life to tell him clearly that his chosen path was writing.


1. kalki saw rabindranath tagore in trichy. when kalki was living on
subsistence levels as a student with the funding of 20 rupees or so
every month sent( with great difficulty) by his brother from the
village , he heard that tagore was holding a meeting in trichy, and
those who paid the princely sum of 5 rupees would be allowed to see
him.
the amount was for shanthiniketan fund.
kalki had read a lot of authors but not tagore but tagore was already
a nobel laureate and mahatma gandhi himself had called him " gurudev"
so kalki decided to go penniless for a few days.
he sat by the path that tagore took and even touched the poet's white
silken robe. this was the first incident that made kalki realise the
fame a person could attain by writing.
(kalki in later years would compare mahakavi bharathi unfavourably
with rabindranath, but thats a different story)
the second incident happened in jail......

3. PS is a super-human with unmatchable qualities... hence an
ordinary

hinder the process of getting involved with the story. So, Kalki
chose

themselves better with the role and develop great interest and
