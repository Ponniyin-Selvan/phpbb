Regarding Archaeology courses

by kalinga_tamil on Fri May 11, 2007 6:42 pm

Courses Offered from utkal university : M.A. in AIHCA(traditional)100
Marks

Infact inder utkal university of culture we are trying for the
distance education for the above subjects.

process is initiated by me under our
Historical links research Foundation



Paper I -Principles & Methods of Archaeology
Paper II -Prehistory & Protohistory
Paper III -Archaeology & Applied Science
Paper IV -Political History of India upto AD 1200
Paper V -Social, Cultural & Economic History of India upto AD 1200 or
Tourism, Muscology or Legislations for Archaeology
Paper VI -istory of Indian Art & Architecture.
Paper VII-History, Society & Culture of Orissa upto 1435
Paper VIII & IX-Special papers (any one group)
Group A -Art and Architecture of Orissa
Group B-Archaeology
Group C-Religious History of Indian Iconography.
Group D-Epigraphy & Numismatics
Group E-Art & Archaeology of S.E. Asia &.SriLanka.
Paper X-Dissertation/Report, on the basis of Field
work/Exploration/Excavation.
M.Phil:in AIHCA (Traditional) 400 Marks
Paper I -Concept of History
Paper II-Ancient Indian Historiography
Paper III -Research Methodology
Paper IV-Dissertation

Archeology course designed for distance education using ICT as a
media.

course will reach to the user by courier,chating,pdf
files,documentries ,email and different media through capacity
building on the above subject.

this course will linked with
anthropology,archeology,history,lingustic,litreature,socialigy,enviro
nmental history on habitate and migration,ICT and geography.

presently we are designed this course for the orissa based NRI's
example
harward university physist Mr.B.Mishra who is 55years old doing
research for NASA but native of PURI verykeen on archeology &
historylike me .

discussions.

subject. all ready applied through my historical links research
foundation on this regards.

monica smith of USA,chris hill a famous environmental history,

help in this regards .
