by satish_arun on Thu Aug 21, 2008 8:49 pm

Good flow.

Another trivia - The order of Kaurava's senapathis, served half the
time as their predecessor.

Bheeshma - 10 days.
Dhrona - 5 days
Karna - 2 (half doesnt mean exactly half. 2.5 can be rounded off to 2
and hence we can consider 2 as nearly half of 5 )
and Salya 1 day.

Regards,
