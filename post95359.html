by dhiwakarb on Mon Aug 29, 2005 3:15 pm

satguru muruganandam avargale saranam

andha kanakkil paarthaal

Subramanian sivanoda oru simplified form enru engo paditha
gnapagam.....

neenga solraa maadhiri sivanukku pratyakshamaaga theriyum mugangal
aindhu (may be not in the same order)
1. eesavaasyam
2. Vaamadevam
3. aghoram
4. Thathupurusham
5. Sathyojatham

kannukku pulappadaadha aaraavadhu mugam .. melnokki iruppadhu..
is called Adhomukham....

aanal subrahmanyan andha aaraavadhu mugamaana Adhomukamum thannudaya
bhakthargalukku kaatchi tharumbadi simplified aaga ullaaan....

moreover, pillaiyar and murugan pathi innoru vishayam padichadhaa
gnapagam....

Pillaiyar and murugan are steps leading to sivan...

1. pillaiyar enbadhu mudhal padi... where avar ulaga aasaigalai
arulvaar
2. Murugan enbadhu irandavadhu padi ... where gnanathai arulvaan
murugan

3. sivan moonraavadhu padi.... absolute gnanam....

since pillaiyar is the mudhal padi he is may be called as first
son.. murugan second padi may be called as second son....they are
representation of Nadham and Bindu ....

arunagiriyaar goes one step further and calls murugan

"naadha bindhu kalaadhi namo nama..."

also further he calls murugan as the one who can give sivagnanam...

".. thagaimai siva gnanamukthi paragathiyum nee koduthu udhavi
puriya venum neidha vadivelaa...."

Soundarya lahari il paarthaal ambaal and sivan are in a roopam
called as "kaameshwarn / kaameshwari" roopam..( dont think Michael
madhana kamarajan movie)

Kaameshwari dhaan ellaarukkum thalaivi... Kaamakshi thaaye
ellaarukkum thalaivi enbadhu dhaan adhu....
