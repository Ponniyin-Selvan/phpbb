Poll results for ponniyinselvan

by ps-poll on Tue Nov 13, 2007 5:51 pm

The following ponniyinselvan poll is now closed. Here are the
final results:


POLL QUESTION: Member Sivaram Kannan suggested that, we should get rid of
yahoo groups and move to forum based website. Initially
I thought about the same but didn't pursue since I have no
idea how the messages are posted and skeptical to Sivaram's
idea. Just wanted to get a feel of other member's thought.

Advantages

1. Easy to edit the message already posted for correction,
update wrong information, keep the information upto date if it
is outdated. Keep certain information upto date in the first
post or common post instead of going through tons of emails

2. Can add more features (wiki, etc.,) which are not
available in yahoo groups right now.

3. Members can communicate through private messages if
required

4. Easy to moderate.

Disadvantages

1. Few members are very comfortable in checking their new
emails instead of visiting a forum and check for new
messages.

2. (Very)Few members don't have access to external websites @ work
sending emails are very easy from work email.

3. Heavy traffic forum site requires a good hosting
provider to keep the site up and running otherwise it may
be expensive to maintain.

4. Restriction to new changes, why change?

CHOICES AND RESULTS
- Etho solra, Nalla idea, forum based site try pannalaam, 9 votes, 45.00%
- Yahoo groupsukku enna korachal? Onnum matha venam, 6 votes, 30.00%
- Archive site pothum, innoru site vera ethukku?, 2 votes, 10.00%
- Nee yarappa ithellam seiyarathukku,vera vela illaya?, 0 votes, 0.00%
