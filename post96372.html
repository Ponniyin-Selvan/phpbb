On Palm leaves...

by vkasirajan on Wed Aug 23, 2006 11:19 pm

Hi,

I wanted to ask one question here. The dramas of
Mahendra Pallavan - in which language they were
composed ( Tamil / Sanskrit ) - do we have all of the
dramas intact ?

In the link section of Varalaaru.com, there is a link
that says that the authors of "Kalithogai" was found
in a palm leaf manuscript...Our current assumption was
it was sung by a Pandian King.

So the palm leaves identified in southern districts in
a recent govt census might contain some precious
historical news / lost literature as well.

Our group can contribute something on these things,
before they are lost into oblivion.

Some initiatives please. We talk a lot, but nothing
happens beyond that.

On Palm leaves...

by satish_arun on Fri Aug 25, 2006 2:54 am

VK,

In the deemed university of Kanchipuram (Sri Chandrasekharendhra Saraswathi
Viswa maha vidhyalaya,remember we even met its regitrar during our RK school
meeting) has a department called Manuscripts dept. which works with the
central govt. to digitize and catalogue all the palm leaves. They have an
extensive collection and most of them are unpublished work. I have
personally seen that dept. and thrlled, because what is available there is
only a portion and was shocked to realize how much we would have lost.

My friend is working in this dept. Can try arranging a visit, if needed.

Regards,
Satish
