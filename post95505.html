Hi All,

I think the haters of vanathy have totally
misunderstood her. I request those to go thru the book
again. Actually she is potrayed as being very
practical unlike Poonguzhali who always dreams of so
many things and accomplishes only one or two in the
end and that too not in the way she dreamt of.

In case of vanathy she has only two goals/ambitions or
whatever you call, in her life. One is to beget a son
who could avenge her father's defeat before she met
arulmozhi. And after meeting arulmozhi, marrying him
was the another one. Kalki has mentioned very clearly
that at first she had only this thought of begetting a
son who is very brave. She often dreams about her son
coming to her after emerging as victorious in all the
battles he fought and thus making her proud. At this
stage, she is not really bothered about her husband to
be. He can be any X or Y.

Once she met arulmozhi she thinks that she could not
accept anyone but arulmozhi as her husband. So her
thought of begetting a brave son becomes complex cause
now it has to be thru arulmozhi only. And now this
second thought must be fullfilled , then only she can
think about her first ambition. And there were many
pblms in marrying arulmozhi. It was not just easy
though she was a princess. Now her objective was to
get arulmozhi first and then beget a son thru him. She
is not sure of getting arulmozhi. So only after
meeting arulmozhi she gets this fainting habit.
Remember kundavai telling arulmozhi that vanathy was
very much carefree and talkative when she first came
to pazhaiyaarai from kodumbaalur.

Actually she is mentally very strong and sternly
follows her mind till the end. Also the sabatham which
she does is not just for poonkuzhali or anyone. It is
actually for arulmozhi. Because though arulmozhi knows
that vanathy loves him, he has an iota of thought that
may be she loves him in hope of becoming a queen. This
thought came to arulmozhi's mind because of kundavai,
Periya velar etc. She makes the sabatham so that
arulmozhi can understand her true love for him. When
kundavai asks her why has she made such a terrible
vow, she replies "Rombo naatkalaaga en manathil
theermanitha vishayathai dhaan ippodhu palar
ariyumbadi koorinen akka". So one can see that she is
not influenced by anyone and she can never be. Her
sabatham is for clearing away the stumbling blocks in
her pathway to accomplish what she wants. It is after
this episode that arulmozhi just makes explicit his
hidden love for her.

She is very stubborn also.She pays a deaf ear to
kundavai when she wants to go to nagai for arulmozhi.
She asks for VD's help but when he is not willing she
decides to go all alone. These incidents are examples
of how undeterred her mind is. Actually she proves
her true love for arulmozhi over poonguzhali or
anyone. Manimekalai fails to do this to VD.

Even when arulmozhi teases her so many times she just
remains quiet. She never gets angry , she never
argues, which means that she is sure of herself. She
is undisturbed. She accomplishes her goals in a very
quiet and calm manner.

One more point to be noted is that after knowing her
mind fully, arulmozhi immediately agrees to marry her.
Kundavai doesn't come in the picture at all. Vanathy
tells him that he should marry her before he goes to
nagai for political reasons for which he says okay.
And finally he does that also. That's why they didn't
wait for VD and others to come for their wedding.

So arulmozhi and vanathy marry, influenced by their
own minds. Kundavai had nothing to do here.
Arulmozhi's teasing of vanathy is because of his love
for her. He never does this to any other person.

We all know that when a person has no problems in his
personal front, he can concentrate on his carrer fully
and reach greater heights. This is one reason why
arulmozhi chose vanathy to be his bride. He tells her
that he has so many ambitions and he feels afraid that
she may become hinderous in achieving his goals.
Vanathy clears that saying that she will never be a
stumbling block for him. And arulmozhi understands
that vanathy knows what she wants and what arulmozhi
wants. And so he decides to marry her.

In case of poonkuzhali, her mind wavers. She is not
sure of what she wants actually. She keeps changing
her thoughts every now and then. Nandhini also does
this but is totally unpredictable. Poonguzhali accepts
amudhan only when she comes to know fully that she
cannot get arulmozhi. I like poonguzhali's character
also.

There is a saying " nam kai 5 viralkalum onna
irukaadhu". All characters by kalki are like this.
Each one has a different mind set, behaviour etc. But
all are potrayed as mere humans. This is the
highlight.

Priya.

Remember kundavai telling arulmozhi that vanathy was
very much carefree and talkative when she first came
to pazhaiyaarai from kodumbaalur.

Actually she is mentally very strong and sternly
follows her mind till the end. Also the sabatham which
she does is not just for poonkuzhali or anyone. It is
actually for arulmozhi. Because though arulmozhi knows
that vanathy loves him, he has an iota of thought that
may be she loves him in hope of becoming a queen. This
thought came to arulmozhi's mind because of kundavai,
Periya velar etc. She makes the sabatham so that
arulmozhi can understand her true love for him. When
kundavai asks her why has she made such a terrible
vow, she replies "Rombo naatkalaaga en manathil
theermanitha vishayathai dhaan ippodhu palar
ariyumbadi koorinen akka". So one can see that she is
not influenced by anyone and she can never be. Her
sabatham is for clearing away the stumbling blocks in
her pathway to accomplish what she wants. It is after
this episode that arulmozhi just mak es explicit his
hidden love for her.

She is very stubborn also.She pays a deaf ear to
kundavai when she wants to go to nagai for arulmozhi.
She asks for VD's help but when he is not willing she
decides to go all alone. These incidents are examples
of how undeterred her mind is. Actually she proves
her true love for arulmozhi over poonguzhali or
anyone. Manimekalai fails to do this to VD.

Even when arulmozhi teases her so many times she just
remains quiet. She never gets angry , she never
argues, which means that she is sure of herself. She
is undisturbed. She accomplishes her goals in a very
quiet and calm manner.

One more point to be noted is that after knowing her
mind fully, arulmozhi immediately agrees to marry her.
Kundavai doesn't come in the picture at all. Vanathy
tells him that he should marry her before he goes to
nagai for political reasons for which he says okay.
And finally he does that also. That's why they didn't
wait for VD and others to come for their wedding.

So arulmozhi and vanathy marry, influenced by their
own minds. Kundavai had nothing to do here.
Arulmozhi's teasing of vanathy is because of his love
for her. He never does this to any other person.

We all know that when a person has no problems in his
personal front, he can concentrate on his carrer fully
and reach greater heights. This is one reason why
arulmozhi chose vanathy to be his bride. He tells her
that he has so many ambitions and he feels afraid that
she may become hinderous in achieving his goals.
Vanathy clears that saying that she will never be a
stumbling block for him. And arulmozhi understands
that vanathy knows what she wants and what arulmozhi
wants. And so he decides to marry her.

In case of poonkuzhali, her mind wavers. She is not
sure of what she wants actually. She keeps changing
her thoughts every now and then. Nandhini also does
this but is totally unpredictable. Poonguzhali accepts
amudhan only when she comes to know fully that she
cannot get arulmozhi. I like poonguzhali's character
also.

There is a saying " nam kai 5 viralkalum onna
irukaadhu". All characters by kalki are like this.
Each one has a different mind set, behaviour etc. But
all are potrayed as mere humans. This is the
highlight.

Priya.
