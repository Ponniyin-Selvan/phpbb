I was missing this group for a very long time. I had a relocation to
UK. I am currently in Pune, flying to Chennai next week and back to
UK with my loving Family.

I request something from this group. Can someone help me in my
project of Tamil Comics. It is awaiting a start from scratch. Needs
help in your views of Software, Images, etc.

Sps, Krupa, Arun, Kamal, Swetha, Gokul and other I request your

Don't ask me about Animation movie. I am extremely sorry. The
investment, manual work, copyright issues and other issues are
really tough to manage, at least at this stage.

thier national languages as, Tamil is not read or written by many
Tamilians. And is only spoken. There is a good population in Vietnam
also. Not to speak about people from Eelam, they are also every
where. My intention is to start this project, and if successful,
then we hav pushed the result atleast an inch towards us.

RJC!
