We went from Tiruvarur via Tirukkannamangai. This according to legend is one of the 3 sculptures that the Poradacheri Shirpi sculpted, the other 2 being Sikkal & Ettukudi - though the 1st one is in Poradacheri itself & not at Sikkal.

Swami name: Brahmapureeswarar
Ambal : Brihan Nayagi

I have no info about kalvettuss, but Arunagirinathar has sung at this temple, so must have exisisted from then.
