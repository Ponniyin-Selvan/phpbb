When I prepare rasam my mother's normal lecture is something like this

1. I should be able to judge the amount of Tamarind required for the
preparation of rasam. Soak that required amount in hot water.
2. After sometime when its soft extract the juice of the tamarind and add
rasam powder and salt that will be required for the rasam.
3. Add Asafoetida and allow that to boil.
4. When the rasam powder smell is gone add dhal water to that and keep it
in sim
5. After sometime the rasam will form thick foam that's the time to add
finely chopped coriander leaves and switch off the burner.
6. Talichi kottudhal simply mustard will be done after this.
