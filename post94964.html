Most Disturbing Death? & mudhal yaathirai info

by lavy_78 on Wed Jan 29, 2003 2:51 am

Hi all,

Here is BIT of my personal information. I am a
chartered accountant, working as a team leader in one
US based company, working long hrs, as sridhar told
tired eyes etc., etc., I read sivagamiyin sabhatham
when I was about 10 yrs old and I have the habit of
reading one book or the other always. I am an ardent
reader. I have read SS so many times I can't count. I
have PS for 5 or 6 times.

Here are my views about the most disturbing death.

Adithya Karikalan: His whole life seemed to be filled
with sorrow. Since we anticipated his death, it
wouldn?t give a shock. But then his whole life had
been a tragedy (can we term his success as a war hero,
a success in his life??). His love brought him nothing
but agony. Sure enough his death relieved him of his
pain, but then readers would feel a tinge of sorrow. I
felt a lot unhappy after reading his death part.

Pazhuvetarayar: He had been portrayed as a villain
from the first. Not directly but he was under the
spell of Nandhini. Since in the later stage he
realized his mistake, his death would create sympathy
in the minds of the readers.

Manimegalai: She was in a quiet love with
Vandiyathevan. All her wants were simple but even
those were not fulfilled. Hers is a true love. Even
kundavai had some motives for loving VD. Finally
Manimegalai had to meet an end like that. Her death
actually came as a shock for me when I read that part,
the first time. It is definitely a tragedy.

Oomai Rani Mandhagini: Her death simply portrays her
unconditional love for Sundara Chozar and all his
family members. Her?s is a deiviga love. Her death
although anticipated, would give one an unhappy
feeling. I used to think why kalki should have killed
her. He could have completed as though she returns to
Srilanka again and spends the rest of her life time in
the forests there, peacefully (now that she has saved
her beloved she would not have anything to worry). I
liked Oomai Rani a lot.

And as for Pinakapani, it is better to die than lead a
life of lunatic. I don?t think his death would have
any great impact.

Overall I think, Manimegalai?s is a real tragedy. Then
next to it is Oomai rani?s and Adithya Karikalan?s.

And here are some info about proceedings on mudhal
yathirai.

Yesterday Myself, Kamal and my sister met Mr. devamani
rafael referred by Mrs. seetha ravi. He is basically a
temple researcher and he has given us one book he has
wrote on various temples incl. Darasuram,
Rajarajeeshwaram etc., I am yet to read the book. He
also gave us some info abt the places. He gave us the
names and contact no.s of some others who have good
knowledge abt the places we propose to visit. One of
the persons referred writer Balakumaran. Kamal met
Balakumaran and B recorded their chat and has given
the cassette to kamal. B referred one Mr. Sundar
Bharathwaj. Kamal and my sis met him today. My sis was
telling me that i missed the important meeting. He has
given lots of info abt the places. He has volunteered
to type down everything neatly and also give us some
photos and more info abt the places. I feel really
excited abt the trip and feel very enthu.

Kamal is making arrangements for Travel and Stay.

Regards,
Lavi
