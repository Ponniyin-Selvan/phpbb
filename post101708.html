Burindi sultan?.........

Cholas were not thalayaati pommais. They respected
their close friends and people. They took necessary
advice from them.

Karikal Cholan took advice from his uncle and escaped
from Uraiyur. He made great history.

Rajaraja Cholan took advice from Kunadavai made him
emperor.

Cholas respected their friends, royal relatives,
commanders, soldiers and utmost the people. That's why
their empire withstand for 450 years.

Thanjavur dolls nothing to do with the Cholas.

If you want to make show, buy some electric bulbs and
do some serious wiring in front of your house.

Make sure all focused on you and switch on. Definitely
it will make you happy. By the by, you can be like a
Thanjavur doll make worthy your show.

Never ever try to make another history(your dolls
show) on magnificent Cholas.

Hi all
the problem here is that kalki was so realistic

we neither have info and I seriously doubt that in reality kundavai
would have been so dominant. or vanathi so passive. we were still
living in a male chauvinistic era.
but one thing we have to remember that when we talk about kundavi or
vanathi we are mostly talking about the novel.
no novel has been torn apart like this as PS and that was out of love
for it.
we have analysed what happened before vanthiyathevan came to vira
narayana eri and finaly what happened after manimekalai died on its
banks.
we have visited places mentioned in the novel without ever knowing
whether their equivalent real historical charecters had set their
foot on them. for example knowledgeble people in the group tell us
the sambuvarayars had little to do with kadambur.
so opinions on these charecters should be welcomed rather than
opposed.
because it should be treated as a literary review.
thanks all
