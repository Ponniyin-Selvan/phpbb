This week's Podcast is about
"Arulmigu SivaSuryanarayanamoorthy
Alayam,Sooriyanarkovil ". This temple is close to Aduthurai
and 15 km from Kumbakonam.This temple is also situated 3 km west of kanchanur
(where sukran sthalam is located).Bus facilities are available from
kumbakonam,Aduthurai and mayiladuthurai.






Among the planets, Sun
occupies the prime position. That is why the first day of the week is named
Sunday. The seven planets including Saturn form the seven days of the week.


Suriyanar (Sun) Temple tower s 15.5 meter high. It has three tiers. At the
top of the tower there are five kalasam. Outside the Rajagopuram (main tower)
in the north side there is a holy tank called Surya Pushkarni where one should
take a bath, and those who are not in a position to bath should sprinkle water
on their head.






Presiding Deity : Sivasuriyan


Goddess :
Ushadevi & chayadevi


SthalaViruksham : Vellerukku


Theertham : Surya Pushkarani






As you enter the Temple on the right side there is Kol Theertha Vinayakar
(Ganesh) Sannidhi is there and in the northern corridor one will find Thejas
Chandikeswarar Sannidhi.


Suryanar Koil is the only temple in the Dakshinapatha, exclusively dedicated to
Sun god with Usha Devi and Pratyusha Devi --- his consorts along with the other
eight grahas, each in a separate shrine around the main one. After Surya
Baghawan one should go to Sabanayakar Mandapam and offer prayers to Lord
Nataraja, the Navagraha, and at Sthabana Mandapam worship Sri Kasi Viswanathar
and Sri Visalakshi Ammai.


The main mandir enshrining
the Sun God facing west is surrounded by separate shrines for Rahu, Ketu,
Chandra, Angaraka, Budha, Sani, Sukra and Brihaspathi.




Temple is open from 6 A.M.
to 12.30
P.M. and 4 P.M. to 8 P.M.
