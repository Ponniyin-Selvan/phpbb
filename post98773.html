Sivaji's RRC is good for the audience who dont know history. I would
say a decently made movie to project RRC in the right sense. Sivaji
overacting in the movie is a different topic altogether.

Why, Indian moviedom, especially Tamil cine field, didnt come up
with a magnum opus on any of the historical stories we have. I think
we have a lot more history than any other country in the world.
While seeing movies like Braveheart, 300, Troy, Gladiator etc, it
gives a sick feeling why Indian movies run around only trees and
waterfalls and duets and hero fighting hundred men at a time.

Atleast in Hindi there was an attempt on Ashoka (though the movie
has nothing to do with real History and would say RRC was much
better than Ashoka). Heard another movie on Ashoka is on cards, Ajay
Devgan is doing Ashoka.

I think Telugu has fair amount of history films. When it took so
many years to attemp PS atleast as a serial, I think we have miles
to go.

Vineeth will play the title role in a movie titled Uli Osai, which is
based on a story by Chief Minister M Karunanidhi.

Karunanidhi, who has penned the story, script and dialogues for
several Tamil films in the past five decades, had written a short
story titled Uli Osai. It is based on the life of a dancer.

Now director Ila Venil will direct a film which is based on
Karunanidhi's story and playing the lead role would be Vineeth.
Interestingly Vineeth was also part of Karunanidhi's last film Paasa
Kiligal.

The shooting for the movie would commence soon.
