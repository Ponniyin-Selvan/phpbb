Hi All Fans of PS and Kalki,

I found out about this yahoo group just today and i joined right
away. My father introduced me to the wonderful and magical world of
PS when i was in 6th standard. It was the summer holidays and i had
nothing to do, was bored. when i complained, he gave me the first 4
volumes of PS and asked me to read it.

I still remember the nonchalant way i started reading PS, something
to do to pass time....then how i got hooked on to it, how the
characters became part of my life and imagination, my utter adoration
and fascination with the narrator, Vandhiya Thevan.

at that time, the 5th volume was just coming out in Kalki Weekly.
from then on, i just waited with bated breath for Kalki every week
and savored every word of that 4 to 5 pages of story. hmmmm....those
were indeed magical days.

From: this group, i understand that many have experienced the same joy
and passion for Kalki's masterpiece. in fact, many have such vast
knowledge about the history and background of the book. i am right
now in San Diego, CA., unfortunately dont have access to a copy of
PS, so anyone discussing anything about PS is greatly welcome.

Regards,
Indu
