by suganya_ml on Fri Nov 19, 2004 3:05 pm

Hi Uma,

As Arun said, 'Kadal Pura' and 'YavanaRani' of Sandilyan will be good
choice. It deals with chola emperors.

If u r interested to know about later pandyas read 'Raja Muthirai'.
Also, u can read 'JalaDheepam' to know about Maratha rulers who
succeeded Chathrapathi Shivaji. Both are by Sandilyan.

To know abt, Rajendra Chola read 'Vengaiyin Maindhan' by Akilan and
'Mannan Magal' by Sandilyan.

To know the complete history of Mogul Empire, get Madhan's
'Vandhaargal Vendraargal' from Vikatan pathipagam. Ur heart will
definitely cry while reading this book, b'cos u will come to know the
number of plights Indians have faced.
