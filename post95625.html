HI veerapandian - was a name many kings took - so were there two veera
pandian - one the father of nandhini and the other her
lover....because in the start kalki's clearly says that when AK hunted
down pandian to the hut, nandhini asks him to spare her lover. this
infuriates him to behead the wounded and unarmed pandia much against
yuddha darma..also AK laments and repents on hearing who veerapandia
was just before he is assasinated (??) killed / comitts suicide.


pandya's seem to crop up every where during that time. same way who
was the small boy who was crowned in the jungle as pandya king ?

so much for fiction, now some facts for discussion:

incidentally i was with Kudavoil Balu sir and he explained to me that
the initial reading of the Udayarkudi inscription was wrong and this
led to whole line of wrongful inferences ( incl KAN)...as per him the
udayarkudi inscription - is as follows:

a resident of south arcot wishes to create an endowment for the local
temple. in this regard he seeks a small portion of land and the
village authorities look for an eligible piece - the land identified
is a smallpart of the lands confisticated after the assasination of AK
by the traitors ravidasan/soman sambavan who ran away /banished /
punihsed ( earlier???) - since this is a high profile case and inroder
to clear the title of the land - RRC who was ruling at that time sends
his high officer to ensure that the endowment is done properly. so as
per him the inscription is not noting the date of punishment ( 986 ad)
but the date of the endowment. So there is no proof that ravidasan and
gang held high posts in uttama rule ( if anyone does have any such -
please do let me know), they could have run away after comitting the
crime, justice was not delayed for 16 years.

some more points for discussion -

Ravidasan was a high officer during sundara time. anyone controdicting
this

his close relative was a high officer in pandya kingdom same time

Both of them were trained in the chera academy ( kandhalur salai)

they both plot to assasinate AK as his above unfair treatment of a
fallen foe ( exhibiting his head on a pole)

due to this reason RRC's first assignment or claim to fame is
destruction of kandalur salai...
