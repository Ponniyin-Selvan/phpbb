Dr. Kalaikkovan

by adhitha_karikalan on Mon Apr 07, 2003 9:09 pm

Hi Friends,

I would like to introduce Dr. Kalaikkovan to you all. Some of you
might have heard about him. This is for the people who do not and
want to know about him and his expeditions.

Dr. Kalaikkovan is the son of the well known tamil scholar Dr. Maa.
Rasamanickanar. He is an eye specialist by profession. He is the
director of Dr.Rajamanickanar Centre for Historical Research, Trichy
and conducting various historical researches with help of the
scholars in his centre.

Here are some of his activities and achievements.

- He runs a half yearly journal called 'VaralaaRu' (history).
- He researched the vimaanam of thanjavur temple and found that it is
not a single stone.
- See one of the articles about his researches in hindu
http://www.hindu.com/2001/06/22/stories/04222236.htm
- See Mr. Kannan's (Germany) mail in e-suvadihttp://www.tamil-
heritage.org/tpmail/ma27_2_2.html
- See Mr. Jaybee's discussion in
http://www.forumhub.com/tnhistory/7121. ... 14.15.html
- See the article about his research on pandiyas inscriptions
http://www.eelavar.com/eelam/pageview.p ... 6&SID=1044
- Helped Mr. Devamani Rafeal with lot of information to write the
book 'Thamizh naattu kalai koilkal'
- Helped us a lot to frame the itinerary for our Mudhal yathirai.

On behalf of our group, we can help Dr. Kalaikkovan either by
participating in the field works or supporting by donations or both.
I hope this interaction with him will keep our group very active and
will get wide view of the historical events and facts.

As part of participating in the field works, we are going to visit
his establishment during our irandaam yaathirai and discuss with him
about the feasibility of participation.

As part of supporting by donations, we are planning to sell the VCD
of mudhal yaathirai for Rs 400 and donate the entire collection to
him. It is a kind of two-in-one offer. You can watch the places
featured in PS as well as donate for his valuable researches. The
process of getting tax exemption for all the donations towards his
organization is also in progress. And subscribing to his journal
Varalaaru will also help him.

So, please share your views and opinions on this.

Thanks
Kamal
