Regarding Tour

by satish_arun on Mon Nov 08, 2004 6:16 pm

Dear Friends,

There was a thread regarding our next tour and tentatively it was
fixed for May2005. Yes indeed we need to go for a grand tour to learn
our history. But I have a suggestion. Why dont we make short trips in
the mean time. I am not sure how many of us are in Chennai? But
people belonging to one locality can make a half day or one day trip
to temples and historical places nearby. There are hundreds of
historical temples around chennai itself. So I think it will be
fruitful for the new comers like me to visit and get an intial
outlook on the historical aspect. Say for ex. the temple in
Madambakkam near Selayur, Temple near Poonamalli,etc. Senior members
can take us and explain the temple architecture etc. We can even
start in the morning and return by evening. It can be a one full day
or just half day trips.

This is my suggestion and like to know your views. Can we make it?

Regards,
Satish
