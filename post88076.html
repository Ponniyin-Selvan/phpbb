only Vijaynagar empire stepped in, by defeating the Chera successors.

by rajendhracholan on Fri Nov 19, 2004 4:07 pm

A correction...

The Cheiftain who claimed to be the descendants of Chola, was 'Veerashekaran' and ruled Kambam, he was defeated by Naagama Naayakar, the father of Vishwanaatha nayakar.

There was another Chola descendant, who ruled Kambam after Veerashekaran and I have not got his name. He was defeated and killed to Ariya naaga mudhali, a associate of Vishwanaatha Naayakar. This war holds importance as Vishwanaatha Naayakar, after this war waged war against Pancha - Paandiyas individually and put a stop to Pandya Dynasty. the place where this war took place is the famous 'Paanchaalankurichchi'.


Rajendhra Cholan!

Chola Pandya branches..

by shed_naresh200 on Sun Feb 03, 2008 7:46 pm

I have the list of Pandiyans who ruled starting from 1357AD.Before
going through the list lets see the nature of the Pandiyans.The
Pandiyans differ from Cholas in one aspect.The Pandiyans were known
to quarrel for the throne.We hear of the 2 Pandiyans quarrel during
the later chola rule.Then between Sundara Pandiya and Veera Pandiya
before the Muslim invasion.If the pandiyans have ruled peacefully
then they might have ruled for long.But they ruined themselves.Soon
this was followed by Vijayanagara
invasion which made them very weak.The Pandiyans learnt a good
lesson.But it was too late.Pandiyans
lost control of Madurai and fled to Tirunelveli and started ruling
from there.How sad is to learn the Pandiyans loosing Madurai!!.The
Pandiyans though not very powerful always had a dream of getting
hold of Madurai.One Pandiyan named Sadayavarman Kulasekara Pandiyan
tried to conquer Madurai in 1479AD
but was defeated by Narasa Nayaka.

1.Sadayavarman Parakrama Pandiya-1357AD :Its said that he ruled for
23 years

2.Parakrama Pandiya : Started ruling from 1384 and ruled till 1415 AD

3.Sadayavarman Kulasekaran-1395 AD

4.We hear another one Pandiyan ruler who ruled during the same
period.He is Sadayavarman
Vikrama Pandiyan.He ruled from 1401 AD to 1422 AD

5.Sadayavarman Parakrama Pandiyan (1422 AD to 1463 AD): He ruled
during the reign of Devaraya 2nd.
He died in 1463AD

6.Sadayavarman Kulasekaran (1430 AD to 1474 AD)

From 1468 AD his son Parakrama Veera Pandiya started to rule and thus
shared the rule with his father.

7.Sadayavarman Kulasekara Pandiyan (1480 AD to 1507 AD)
This Pandiyan was defeated by Narasa Nayaka when he tried to conquer
Madurai in 1479.

Till 1479 AD the Pandiyans were ruling as independent rulers.After
1479 (Guess after the defeat in 1479)
the Pandiyans were reduced to feudatory rulers under Vijayangara
rulers.

8.In 1502 the portion ruled by the Pandiyans came to be known as
Chandrasekara Pandiya Mandalam
We hear of ChandraSekara Pandiya who was the ruler during Krishna
Devaraya period.

Through Pudukottai plates we hear of 2 Pandiyans Abirama Parakrama
Pandiyan and AGAVARAMAN

9.Two Pandiyans
a.Agavaraman's (mentioned above) son Sadayavarman
Srivallaba Pandiyan ruled the areas of
Shenkottai,Sankaran Kovil,Sri Villiputhur with THENKASI as the
capital

b.Vettum perumal Raja who ruled the some areas of
Tirunelveli,Kovilpatti and Sri Vaikundam regions with KAYATHAR as his
capital

Both of them are relatives but lacked understanding.

In a battle Vettum Perumal Raja was defeated by Sadayavarman
Srivallaba Pandiya(Thenaksi Pandiya)

During Srivallaba rule Achutha Raya became the Vijayanagara king and
he apponted Vishwanatha Nayak as his mandala mudali.

10.Again we hear of 2 Pandiyans.they were the sons of Abirama
Parakrama Pandiya.They
were
a.Sadayavarman Parakrama Kulasekara Pandiyan(1543AD)

b.Nelveli Maran (1552AD to 1564AD)

11.Sadayavarman Adhiveerarama Pandiyar (1564AD to 1604 AD)

His son -Sokkar His brother - Vallabar

His periyappa's son -Varathungarama Pandiyar

12.Varathunga Rama Pandiyar:(1588AD)Started ruling some regions with
Karivalam vantha Nallur

13.Varaguna Rama Kulasekara Pandiyan(1613 AD):He did a yaga in 1615AD
and came to be known as
Kulasekara Somasriyar.

14.Adigal Perumal Vela -Varaguna Rama Kulasekaradevar:

He is the last ruler of the Pandiyan dynasty.He ruled till
the saka period 1675 which is equivalent to 1753AD.

There is one way to trace the Pandiyan descendents.I will discuss
this in my next post.

Chola Pandya branches..

by shed_naresh200 on Mon Feb 04, 2008 4:52 am

Lets start from the period of Vishwanatha Nayak (1529 to 1564AD).he
is the founder of Poliga system and he divided the southern region
into 72 Palayams.He brought the PANDIYANS OF THENKASI & KAYATHAR
under the Poligar system.

The 2 Pandiyans whom we identify are
1.Sadayavarman srivallaba Pandiyan(THENKASI)
2.Vettum Perumal Raja (KAYATHAR)

Thus its clear that these 2 Pandiyans attained the status of
Poligars.So they must be one among the 72 Poligars.The possibility is
that these 2 Pandiyans might have ruled much larger territory than
the rest of the
poligars.
But the difficulty is to know the names of the chiefs of all the 72
Palayams formed.Anyway we have the list
of Pandiyans who ruled till 1753 AD.Now the big question is what
happened to them after 1753 AD?
We can try in one way.Its by going through the initial records of the
72 poligars and also through the prabandhams sung in praise of the
Poligars.My idea is those poligars who were not the Pandiyas will be
mentioned by their own caste name or caste title but not with a
Pandiyan family name or the title Pandiyan.
I heard that most of the Poligars started using the title Pandiyan at
a much later stage.I came to know that there is a temple somewhere in
Tirunelveli where there is a statue of Adhiveerarama Pandiyar.Also i
heard of a family in Tirunelveli who do shiratham for Adhiveera rama
Pandiyar(1552-1564AD). I don't know what shiratham is.Does this mean
Thivasam?
