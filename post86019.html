RespectedSir/Madam,

It is a great pleasure for us to announce that the 3rd Annual
Conference of the Society will be held under the auspices of the
Allahabad Museum at Allahabad (U.P) from 30th to 31st October 2007.

The aim of the Society is to provide a common forum for interaction
among Scientists, Archaeologists, Historians and others with varied
disciplinary interests.

A window has been opened to bring forth the researches conducted by
the scholars engaged in the field of Archaeology, Epigraphy,
History, Art and Architecture, Numismatics, Ancient Mathematics,
Physical, Biological and Metallurgical Sciences, Ancient Economics
and Commerce, Computer Analysis of Ancient data, Sciences in Indian
languages and Literature, Scientific Compendiums , History and
Philosophy of Science, Onomastics, etc.

This Conference is planned to provide a large canvass of ancient and
traditional sciences in comparison with recent scientific
discoveries.

Besides Science from Vedic literatures, this meet will explore the
various aspects of Indian scientific heritage , History of Science,
Science and Technology shrouded in Itihasas, Puranas , Smritis,
Sutras, Samhitas , Dharmasastras and others in various languages.
You are requested to bring out the ancient Scientific and
Technological Knowledge of Ancient Indians in your Presentations.

Former Rashbehary Ghose Professor of Mathematics, University of
Kolkata and former Vice-Chancellor , Visva Bharati University
Professor.Dr.Dilipkumar Sinha, has kindly consented to inaugurate
this Conference. Professor.Dr. Smt. Vibha Tripathi, Professor and
former Head of the Dept. of Ancient Indian History Culture and
Archaeology, Banaras Hindu university, Varanasi will be the General
President of the Conference.

The 2nd volume of the Society's Journal will be released on this
occasion by the eminent Indologist and Honorary Fellow of our
Society Professor .Dr.Samaresh Bandyopadhyay, who will also preside
over the Theme Session on (i) "History and Archaeology of the
Allahabad Region" and(ii) "Science in the Rural Life of Ancient
India".

Besides these themes, there will be open sessions on different
disciplines where scholars can present papers on topics of their
special interest.

Delegates are requested to send the Abstracts not exceeding 250
words and the papers with C.D. to Dr.Smt.N.Pankaja, Joint Secretary
of the Society before 15th October, 2007. Abstract should be typed
in a single page, double space on one side of A4 size. Full paper
should be on one side of A4 size, double space not exceeding 8 to 10
pages.
