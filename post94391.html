Dear Swetha and SPS

The Rathna haaram and manimudi were brought back and there is still
a river and town called Mudikondan

SPS yu have to read this book if at all anything this is a true
sequel to PS and no wonder Akilan got the Sahithya academy award for
it...
The narration and charectorisation is very similar to PS

Rohini is a BIT like Nandhini multiple personalities
Arulmozhinangai reminds you of Kundavai
Veeramallan of Kandhamaran

The Hero Ilango is a mixture

Our Main hero VD is the Samanthanagayar CinC of all the chola
armies and Akilan preserves the charector of Kalki though he is now
in his 60s or 70s
It starts with the Eelam expedition and finishes with completion of
GKC with the Kadaram campaign as a three page Mudivurai

You must read it

Regards
Sri

Kodumbalur is located on the Pudukkottai - Kudumiyamalai -
Manapparai main road about 35 kilometres from Pudukkottai. And it
lies 5 kilometres away from the Tiruchi - Madurai highway. Bus
facility is available from Viralimalai and Manapparai.
=================================
THERE IS A MOOVENDAR KOIL - REPORTED CONSTRUCTED BY ALL THE THREE
KINGS. INCIDENTALLY, THE IS ALSO THE CLOSEST BORDER TOWN OF ERST

FAMOUS "PONNAN SANKAR" PLACE IS ALSO NEARBY.

the 'manimudi'

period?

Rajasimma Pandiyan the second lost miserably to Paranthaka Chozhan.
He took the traditional manimudi of the pandiyas and the raththina
haram, which is believed to have been given by Lord Indiran to the
pandiyas, and left them in Sri Lanka in the custody of the Lankan
Emperor(Mahinda III?).

Chozhas waged war after war with the Lankans, to bring this back
into the Tamil land. They finally succeeded in Rajendra's period.
This is what has been described in the lines you have quoted.
