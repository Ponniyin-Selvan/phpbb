Karthikeyan's mother.

wishes.

Rgds
G
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"kalavathy karthikeyan"

sir,
i am karthikeyan's mother. do you remember him.i guess u will know
him by the name ponniyinselvan [vijayanagaram blog].
he met wiyh a fatal accident on 26 August 2005.
i am that most unfortunate mother.
[i know Tamil well,and i can write poems . but i do not know typing.]
so i continue in english.
it is after a very great effort,i could access to net and contact you.
i want to talk a lot about my son and want to continue his place.
all that a mad mother could do .
will you please contact me?
a very sad mother,
karthik amma
