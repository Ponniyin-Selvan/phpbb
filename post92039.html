interesting warfare before BC!!

by rahul_pentiumiii on Tue Mar 13, 2007 7:43 pm

Im sure most of the members familiar with computer games would have played
or heard of the game called Age of Empires (there are 3-4 parts of this
game) by Microsoft Corporation. Its quite exhaustive and brilliant in the
portrayal of warfare before BC. The detail is just stunning. The use of
trebuchets, battering rams, etc are all intricately portrayed. There are
explanatory ntoes about each weapon, each civilisation is superbly detailed.
The architecture is wonderfully researched even though there might be few
errors its a good crash course on ancient weaponry while playing!!
