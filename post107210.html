This weeks episode is about "Arulmigu Muktheeswarar Thirukkovil,Thilatharpanapuri".

Audio Link :
http://podbazaar.castmetrix.net/download/144115188075857177/1/TamilPodcast62VaaramOruAlayamArulmiguMuktheeswararThirukkovilThilatharpanapuri.mp3

Audio Duration: 5m 50 sec

Location&Direction:
One should take the Mayiladuthurai-Thiruvarur bus route and get down at
Poondhottam village.From there Take Eravancheri bus route for 2 km
where this temple is situated at west

Current Name : Thilatharpanapuri (thilakaipathi)
MainDeity : Muktheeswarar,Mantharavaneswarar

Godesses : sornavalli (porkodi)
Theertham : Brahma theertham
Holytree : Manthara tree

There
are 7 sthalams for performing the rituals (Pindam,Tharpanam) for
dead(pithru sthalam).They are Kasi,Rameswaram,Sri
Vanjiyam,Thiruvenkadu,gaya,Thiriveni sangamam and the seventh
one being Thilatharpanapuri. In Ramayan, When Ravana took Sita, Jatayu
tried stopping him but finally killed by Ravana.During the return from
Lanka, Rama and Lakshmana did the rituals for their Father and beloved Jatayu as well.

To hear more about this temple and sthalapuranam click below

Poonthottam Dakshinamurthy :

Dakshinamurthy @ Aalamarselvan sits on a small mound wherein all the

Dakshinamurthy earlier - Alangudi, Thenkudithittai, Omampuliyur,

Venkat has written earlier on this temple in detail). RRC period.
Excellent sculptures. Famous for Rathasapthami !

Thiruchengottangudi :: Pillaikkari (recently held this year).
Seeralan - Siruthondar - Bhairva episode. (Pls refer Archieves)

Thiruveezhimizhalai :: RRC II - golden roofed this temple. Presiding
deity Rajarajeswaran ! (Pls ref Archieves)

Thirunallar - Darbaranyeswarar (Saneeswaran) nearby

Between Mayavaram - Thiruvarur there are Veerattanams like
Thiruppariyalur, Thiruvirkudi !

Among the Pithru Sthalams (Ref Nataraj) - thiruvengadu is considered
even above Kasi (kasikku veesam (1/16) adhigam !)

Reason :: Here one can conduct such ceremonies for Friends, Servants
and even pet animals !!

Vaaram Oru Alayam #62 Arulmigu Muktheeswarar Thirukkovil,Thilatharpanapuri

by rajendhracholan on Mon Jun 09, 2008 3:50 pm

We had a divine grace of cisiting this temple.

Before entering the temple there is a small shrine for Aadhi
Vinayakar (as mentioned), the mighty pillayar with human face. At
the vicinity, there are Saptha Kannikas, on the open ground.

This place is now called 'Sethalapathi', against 'Thilagaipathi'
or 'Thilatharpanana puri'. Alongwside Lord Rama, there is also a
King Narchodhi, associated with this temple.

At the back of Great Lord Shiva Alais Muktheeshwara, Lord Rama and
and King Narchodhi, will stand worshipping Great Lord Shiva, as
Lingam. It is said, Lord Rama, did the tharpanam and the Pindams
turned Lingam. The Great Lord Muktheeshwara, thus grating Moksha for
Jatayu and departed King Dasaratha.

RJC!
