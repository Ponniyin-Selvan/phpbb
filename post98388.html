Dear Sri,

In single mail you are achieving !

After "Krupa's Wedding?!" - there are again activities now. Nice..

Two view points have surfaced so far :

1. VD's night stay at Kadambur & listening to Sadhialosanai.
2. AK's Murder

Strictly speaking I read & re-read PS several years back. I remember
the story as a whole - but not event by event.

VD is sent from Kanchi by AK to assess Thanjavur events. He takes the
route of Viluppuram (Thirukkovilur via) - Sethiathope -
Veeranarayanapuram (Veeranam) - Kadambur. Sambuvarayar's son Kandhan
Maran requests him to stay that night thinking VD may qualify for his
sister Manimekalai. He fumbles on this gathering : Pazhuvettarayars -
Madurantakan (bad) - Sambuvarayars & co., By the end there is a
Kuralan yelling there is going to be a loss to the Throne.

I did not consider this as a turning point because AK already
suspected the involvement of those assembled in Kadambur. If one
recalls -AK is the only affected person because there was an uprise
against him - who was already an Yuvaraja (Crown Prince).

Those assembled there did not want him to become the King when
Sundara dies.Instead they wanted Maduranataka to become a puppet
King. (What a lovely story .. the land offerings to Ravidasan & co
by Madurantaka subsequently proves this line of thinking very well)

These people did not AIR their views when AK was made the Yuvaraja in
the presence of Sembianmadei - ignoring Madurantaka - by Sundara
Chola. But Siriyapazhuvettarayar had the foresight and gave his
daughter in wedding to Maduranatakan. (All supported by Historical
facts)

Pazhuvettarayars could not sit tight but to instal Madurantakan.

AK was the only obstruction to all of them assembled over there.

The only agenda was to design getting rid of him.

This is the KNOT for the Story.

(HAD ANYBODY ANTICIPATED IN THE GATHERING THAT ARUNMOZHI WOULD EMERGE
AS CHAKRAVARTHY RAJARAJA ONE DAY, THEY WOULD HAVE ELIMINATED HIM
ALSO. HOW CLEVER ARUNMOZHI WAS .. EXACTLY KUNDAVAI FEAR THIS AGAIN &
AGAIN.. KALKI HAS RANSACKED CHOLA HISTORY AND DEVELOPED THE STORY
LINE.. I AM SURE HE WOULD HAVE DISCUSSED IT WITH SASTRY & PANDARATHAR
& OTHERS SEVERAL TIMES )

Aditya's murder - Madurantaka's ascendency - Ravidasan's role in AK's
murder - is the story of Ponniyinselvan.

Arunmozhi finding this and becoming Rajaraja ...A story yet to be
told..

VD's experience was only a reinforcement to AK's suspecion..

Hence I did not consider this as a turning point..

ADITHA KARIKALAN'S MURDER WAS THE TRUE TURNING POINT OF NOT ONLY
CHOLA HISTORY BUT ALSO SOUTH INDIA HISTORY ...

Fondly, SB
