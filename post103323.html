THIRUIDAICHURAM - near Tirukkazhukkundram - is of MARAGATHA LINGAM !

SIRUVAPURI - near RED HILLS / Chennai - is of MARAGATHA MURUGAN !

UTHIRAKOSA MANGAI / Near Ramnad - Tutikorin (Those who attend
Palliyezhuchi in Sivatemples can recall Manickavasagar's
Thiruperundurai (alias Avudayar koil - Lingam without Avuday)
Thiruppalliyezhuchi hymns. Likewise during Arthajaamam,
Uthirakosamangai hymns are sung by Vodhuvars) - NATARAJA - is of
MARAGATHA NATARAJA !

The Temple (under Ramnad HH Rani as Trustee) allows the Sandal paste
to be uncovered on ARUDHRA Day morning and again applies in the
NIGHT to be KEPT IN TACT TILL NEXT ARUDHARA !

The maragadha Nataraja is THUS VISIBLE only on Arudhra Day !

Uthirakosa Mangai is also known for KAAGA PUJANDAR (Siddhar)
SAMADHI !

-Hi
idaisuram is a fantastic temple, nestled between a few hills
but I doubt if it is so close to thiruporur.
it definitely must be a longer distance.
i remember it was closer to chingelpet. 5 km or so.

the temple was renovated by the saravana bavan group and is crowded
during pradoshams. on other days the gurukal lives close by.

actually he has a murai in mylapore kapali temple too and comes here
for 15 days.

the lingam was supposed to be a dazzling green in those days

Iidaisuram meviya ivar vannam ennE? asks sambandar/

however when the deepam is shown we can still see the green between
the vibhuthi lines.


chennai' ites who want to visit this temple can do a half a day tour.

start with kachur opposite ford.
go to idaisuram
then to thiru kalu kundram
3 paadal petra sthalams.

venketesh
