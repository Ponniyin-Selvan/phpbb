Nandhini - The Gujarathi conection

by dhiwakarb on Thu Oct 13, 2005 2:03 am

Nanbargale....

There is a great connection between Nandhini and Gujarath.

as usual i was roaming in Bukavu(congo) market with my bloody
salesman Bag.... everything was usual...

aana rendu naal vegetarian saappaadu kidaikkaama mandai kaanjen.. was
surviving on Loads of Banana ( vayithai kalakidichu ) .....

veruthu poi... i attended a business meeting (atlast with an Indian)
and at the end of the meeting, i asked him where i wud get vegetarian
food as i had not eaten for the past two days..

udane andha punniyavaan told me that there is a person working in his
company who is a gujarathi and told me that he is a pure vegetarian
and introduced to me.....

Nijamaa solren.. the person to whom i got introduced is a
punniyavaan... uyir kodutha deivam to me.... he took me to his house
and gave dosai, sambar, chapathi, and more importantly Thayir saadham
and casually showed his family album...

He has just had a kid and the mother and child were in India.....when
asked about the name of the child ( wife ai pathi kekka mudiyumaaa)
he told the name as what you know???

"Nandhini" .. yes the name of the child is "nandhini".. i was
astonished to see the name in a Gujrathi family , and asked whether
the wife is a south Indian for which he replied in the Negative.. and
said Nandhini is common in Gujarat....

avvalavu dhaan andha aalukku naan PS kadhaiya muzhusaa 2 hours
ukaandhu sonnen ... starting with "khem cho " , hindi and
English..... and he was astonished the way Nandhini's character has
been portrayed In PS.....

I gave an aura on Nandhini and explained him fully.. her patriotism ,
her beauty and of course her vily nature .....

after all the discussion he was very happy that he gave his daughter
the name and you know waht wants an english translation of PS to be
sent to him.....

aaga indha African trip la uruppadiya ennoda companykku business
aachcho illaiyo..... PS pathi oru Gujarathi kku sonnen.....

Vaazhkayila oru uruppadiyaana kaariyam pannirukken inikku dhaan......

Anyway, Mr. Krishnamurthy nandhini ngra perai edhanaala
vaichaar....???

ORU VELAI YAARAAVADHU "NANDHINI" NGRA PERAI KONDA GUJJU PONNAI PAATHU
MAYANGI VAICHIRUPAARO????
