by suresh28370 on Sun Dec 21, 2003 7:41 pm

ayya thozhargale..

Nandhini yum Naganandhiyum incomparables. I will tell kuraindha
batcha aaru vithiyasangal:

1. Ponnu Vs Anu
2. Naganandhi had a right to the throne -- nandhini never had
3. naganandhi was a villain because of love -- nandhini used love
(loves?) to show villathanam
4. Naganandhi at least had some soft corner to paranjothi -- even
though nandini at times show some soft corner to vandhiyathevan, it
is only a show.
5.naganandhi was a part of a victorious war -- nandini starts her
vengence after a defeated war.
6. naganandhi had a backup of salukiya army -- nandini had nothing
else than a handful of black magic-ers.

podhuma innum venuma -- aaru enna nooru sollalam.. the only
similarity is both are brilliant people with a goal opposite to the
hero characters.

sari annakkale.
