Dear Mr. Ramesh,

Sometimes we all do feel that way.

To cite an example / two : Please check the mails posted by one
Srihari from Srilanka about 2 years back. About 8 to 10 of them.
All gems. Most related to various dynasties and contemporaries - not
only in South India - but in India and Overseas related to period
800 to 1000 AD minimum. Great aset for every History lover.

I still remember the very interesting one :

He came out with inscriptional evidences on one Sattan Pazhiyili
that it was this Mutharaya who was conquored by Vijayalayan in 850
AD at Thanjai - ( members recall PS Novel ) - based on Pudukottai Dt
inscription publications. Please remember he lives in Srilanka and
was not happy with another Forum. He showered informations.

Some of us went to Narthamalai - checked Pazhiyileeswaram - found
that Vijayala Chozheeswaram mention was a later period mention - and
thought this information needed further check.

We went to another School of thought and checked Senthalai
inscriptions near Nemam / Tirukandiyur.

By this process, which took nearly 2 months, we gained first hand
knowledge that there were atleast 2 sets of Mutharayas prior to 850
AD - when Vijayala Chozha conquored Thanjai - one from Senthalai &
Thanjai region - in commotion and in contact with the Chozhas - who
were then in Pazhayarai and other from Narthamalai / Pudukottai -
who bordered with Pandyas - who were in commotion and in contact
with the Pandyas and posted these details in the Group.

With such exchanges and enthusiasm Member Srihari posted other
mails. Response level was inadequate. I have not seen his mails
for the past 2 years.

Similarly ( vijayalakshmi) Rangarajan is a Scholar / Professor in
IIT, Madras. Took nice thread on Banas - Rashtrakutas. Initiated PS
audio version ! With inadequate response level his mails also
stopped.

Dr. Swaminathan's mails also did not get deserved responses ..

But some persons from the Group have to carry on this task dear
Ramesh... as you are very much aware ... Please come out of shell..

I am aware of Ramesh - who headed ASI Mysore .. Are we mixing
identities.. !

Please do share your views ..

We have been not responding to mails which affected fellow
members .. Otherwise there are so many enthusiasts among us ..

Re-request you to post more.. Thanks for re-introducing ..

Best regards / sps
