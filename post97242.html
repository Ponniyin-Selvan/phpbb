Dear SB,

There is no question if RRC/RJC were incapable of putting gold. No
doubt they can build N number of temples with gold plates.

We are not against golden plating and not that we dont like big temple
vimaanam having golden plates. We will also be happy if pon vimaanam
concept is proven beyond any doubt. Why cant we wait till we find
solid proof for this instead of coming to a conclusion with less
fragile evidences. Pon vimaanamaaga illaa vittaalum Raajaraajeswaram
great thaane!!

Discussing among ourselves is fine. But when this point is submitted
as a paper in any conference or as an article in any research magazine
/ book, again the same questions will arise from the scholars and they
have to be answered. After all the Kunnaandaar koil paper itself had
to face many questions in thanjai conference though it was a KCA
(Kalaikkovan Certified Article). So, extra caution is necessary before
arriving such conclusions.

If we announce now that it was pon vimaanam and later if it is
disproved, it will affect our other findings as well. They will also
be looked with less credibility. So, lot of patience is required
before deciding anything.

These are all our concerns and nothing personal.

Thanks
Kamal

pon vimanam existing even today in burma

The Shwedagon Paya is a 98 meter gilded stupa located in Yangon,
Myanmar. The Paya is west of the Royal Lake on Singuttara Hill. The
Shwedagon Paya is the most sacred Buddhist site for the Burmese with
several Buddha relics.
Legend has it that the Shwedagon Paya is 2500 years old.
Archeologists believe the stupa was actually built sometime between
the 6th and 10th centuries

The stupa was rebuilt several times and reached it's current height
of 98 meters in the 15th century.

The base of the stupa is made of bricks covered with gold plates.
Above the base are terraces that only monks can access. Next is the
bell-shaped part of the stupa. Above that is the lotus and then the
crown. The crown or umbrella is tipped with 5,448 diamonds and 2,317
rubies. The very top is tipped with a 76-carat diamond.
