Kaviri mainthan, nandhi purathu naayagi and vanthiyathevan vaal -
are all set just as PS ends - say 969 AD onwards till 985 AD.

you can read all about KM in our archives. if i remember right, it
picks up 5 years after ending of PS...so 975 AD.

vikraman's works NPN and VV - are a BIT of a strain to read -
because they work on the premise ( following the recap provided by
kalki that VT and Kundhavai get married after facing lot of
hardship ) -

NPN - he actually takes Parthibendra Pallavan as villain and that VT
is jailed for 10 years and kundhavai waits for him - AMV sails off
somewhere.....ie story starts in 969 and runs to 979

i couldnt get to complete both

Akhilan's Vengaiyin Mainthan - is post RRC demise - just as RJC
prepares his assault on lanka ..as per mahavamsa accounts this took
place in the 36th year of Mahinda's ( srilankan king) reign, i.e.
A.D. 1017, our beloved VT is there however but as a old man but
with all this ` charm'

am sure you can get the books in major books shops in chennai.
