Where can I buy in Chennai?

by abhivencat on Wed Jun 18, 2008 9:52 pm

He wants the old bounded version of Ponniyin Selvan which is
available
only in lending libraries or used book stores. The recent publishing
my big publishers are without any paintings.


Hi
there are about 5 different sets of ponniyin selvan which came in
kalki during different ages. the advertisements are so revealing
about the periods.
we should do a tabulation of the years and the artists, one of these
days.
the original which ran till 1954 april or so is the most valuable. we
all chanced to see one set with an active member of our tours in
kumbakonam.
in fact some members like swetha didnt even want to leave kumbakonam
wanting to spend some time with those bound books though it was time
to leave.
i am lucky to have a original set of 'sivakamiyin sabatham'. i am
told its very valuable.
to me its priceless. and invaluable.
venketesh

HI
almost a year back i had an access to a treasure trove. a cupboard
which was unlocked nearly after 40 50 years in a remote village in
chettinaad.
a trove of books, some of them a century old were given to me.
a lot of our friends received their shares. the most benefitted was
plastics chandra who directly made a visit to devakottai itself. his
life long mission of collecting books by his grand uncle suddananada
barathi was helped as he got some very rare books of his in the
cupboard. it helped that suddananda barathi had worked as a teacher
in devakottai itself.
