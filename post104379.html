It was

the eri.
I don't know whether the temple existed even before and parantaka
simply
renovated it.
Parantaka also built several tanks across the country for the purpose
of

The great king's last days were marred by more than one sad news:
- He lost his first son rajadittha in thakkolam
- He lost another son uttama seeli to veera pandiaya( who was later

- He lost some territories to rashtrakutas during the last days.

It was only after Sundara chola that the empire gained the strength
it lost.

I always pity parantaka for not having a peaceful time during his
last days.
What a king!
