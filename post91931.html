4
hrs

Tvlngud CP etc., & Historical values :: 6 hrs

on
tour : one day.

participants.

head.

If we can show 100 enrolement, we can look at
Rs.
1200 per head.

Below 20, no Classes.

We may have to interact with other groups and
bring
in other
participants.

40 hrs needed. 20 sittings of 2 hrs each.
Absentees
need to be
covered with a special sitting. Details to be
worked out.

But this is exclusively tailor-made for our
Group.
