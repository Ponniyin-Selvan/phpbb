e-PONNIYINSELVAN

by vj_episteme on Sat Nov 03, 2007 2:24 pm

Hi

Project madurai is a voluntary intitiative undertaken by Dr. K.
Kalyanasundaram.
http://www.tamil.net/projectmadurai/

Apart for PS you will find PK and SS also in the site along with
hundreds of other tamil works - incl the tirukural, purananuru,
agananuru, auvaiyar's works, nalaiyra diva prabandam...bible...

http://www.tamil.net/projectmadurai/pmfinish.html

Project Madurai is an open and voluntary initiative to collect and
publish free electronic editions of ancient tamil literary classics.
This means either typing-in or scanning old books and archiving the
text in one of the most readily accessible formats ("ETEXTS") for
use on all popular computer platforms. All etexts will be
distributed in both web/html and PDF formats.- Distributed through
the World Wide Web servers , anyone located anywhere may download a
copy for personal use or read what we publish on the internet, free
of charge.

....meaning these have been painstakingly keyed in tamil by
volunteers all over the world ( from digitized/ scanned
copies)...capturing these works for eternity and distributing it
over the net for free ( honoring copyright of course)

the objective of nationalisation of kalki's texts by the government

his best writings were held under copyright by anandavikadan and
vasan refused to release them because he was upset with kalki baiting
him frequently after leaving vikadan.
