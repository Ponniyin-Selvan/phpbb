--- Inponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "tilotamma"
wrote:
so does anyone think arulmozhi would have insisted on marrying her
is she chose to go back to Kodikarai as opposed to marrying her
cousin.
or was he a coward to be manipulated by his sister?

Both of them are incorrect...

Before falling ill, an incident happened in Kodiakkarai. Kalki
somehow did not want to include it in PS. What actually happened
was.......

Poonguzhali: "Ilavarasey, naan ungakitta oru vishayam sollanum..."

Arulmozhi: " appadiya? naan kooda unkitta onnu sollanumnu
nenachchane..."

Poonguzhali: "idhu vandhu..konjam, konjam personal vishayam.."

Arulmozhi: "naanum personal-aadhaan sollanumnudhaan nenachchane.
neeyae yedhukkum modhalla sollu."

Poonguzhali: "Ilavarasey...neengadhaan modhalla sollanum. adhudhaan
murai."

Arulmozhi: "No no...itz impossible. You have to tell."

Poonguzhali: "Nope...you gonna tell..."

(Saendhan Amudhan arrives in the scene)

Saendhan Amudhan: "Ah....Arulmozhi varmar. neengalum ingadhaan
irukkeengala...okay, okay. naanum Poonguzhaliyum konjam personal-a
paesanum. Ungalai Vandhiya Devan padiththuraikku kooptaar.
kalambunga..."

Arulmozhi: "Illa Amudhan..vandhu...poonguzhalikitta oru vishayam..."

Saendhan Amudhan: "ayyo..seekkaram poanga...Vandhiyadevan is waiting
for you...yedho mukyamaana vishayamaam."

(Vandhiyadevan, seeing these three conversing, shouts from a distance)

Vandhiyadevan: "Ilavaraey, seekkaram, seekkaram. oru mukyamaana
vishayam. Paandiyan, paandiyan"

(hearing the word Paandiyan, with blood pressure in the 150s,
Arulmozhi rushes to go away from the duo and goes near Vandhiyadevan.)

Arulmozhi: "Yes...yenna maettar?"

Vandhiyadevan: "aah...vaanga vaanga ilavarasey. Abhiraami theatre-la
Paandiyan padam 2 ticket kadaichchu irukku...poagalaama?"

(Arulmozhi idhai kaettu thalaila adichchukkaraar...)

Arulmozhi: "Onakku naeram kaalamey kadayaadhu..chey"

(solli vittu, viraivaaga Poonguzhaliyidam varugiraar)

But alas, too late...Saendhan Amudhan has proposed in the
meantime...I don't know how did he convince her.
