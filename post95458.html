After visiting Kailasanathar temple Kanchi, Sundararaja - Vaikunta

varalaaru issue) also accompanied them. Sankaran & a couple of
new members joined us. Dr. Rajavelu & Dr.Thirumurthy, who co-
authored Archlogical details of South India, were also present to
inagurate the Peravai. Mr. TKV Rajan - media specialist also
attended the meet as a special invitee.

With formal introduction of all those present, Rajavelu &
Thirumoorthy gave some guidelines for functioning of such peravais.
Kamal explained the formation of varalaaru.com group, introduced
Lavanya, Krupa & Ram to all those present. Dr.Kaikkovan was very
much precise and advised everyone to SPELL OUT CLEAR TRUE VARALAARU
(Detail of History) - without getting influenced by others' views
and check and counter check details before presenting in media for
everyone's view.

The meet was organised (1) to recognise and record appreciation of
one year achievments of varalaaru.com (2) particularly the services
rendered by Kamal on the eve of his leaving India for some time (3)
and formal inaguration of P S V P.

The objective of PSVP is to create more awareness in knowing True
the History of our Native Land, bringing it out to appropriate
levels for recognition and sustain the heritage and culture of
which we are all so much proud of.

Dr. Kalaikkovan & Dr. Nalini - well recognised Scholars - have
assured their support to young and energetic Neo-History-
Apprentices;

ASI Rajavelu and Tirumurthy have shown readiness in involving us in
certain projects.

The meeting was concluded around 6 pm with all the members
following the MEYKEERTHI Of Rajaraja - " Thirumagal Pola " sung by
Dr. Nalini and National Anthem.
