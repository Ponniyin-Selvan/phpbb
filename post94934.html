Oops.. interesting tamil history thread got hijacked by Periyar
bashing...hmmm..

My 2 cents..
------------

There is a lot of blame to go around.. Instead of dwelling in the bad stuff
about the past why can't we move forward, think about how to improve the
present and future not repeating mistakes that we learned from the past..

Having said that let me try to defend Periyar...( Interestingly, I've just
defended Periyar in another thread in another group.. Maybe i should get
compensated by the DK folks.. )

Periyar's self-respect movement is ALL about instilling self-confidence
and self-respect in the historically under-privileged people who have been
taught from birth that they are good for nothings.. have no intellect.. fit
for just physical stuff etc..etc...

People at that time were believing that god made them to be born into lower
caste families because of their bad deeds in previous generations and if
they perform their duty according to the dictated rules (such as don't
mingle with higher castes/ don't drink water from the same well/ don't
touch etc.. etc..) they might have a chance of being born into a higher
caste in their next generation.. As ridiculous as it may sound, that was
the scenario (it's unfortunate that even now ppl believe in this crap..) If
they fail to live according to the caste rules, god will punish those
folks..

I believe that's the reason why Periyar attacked Hindu gods just to show to
the ppl, nothing will happen to them if they go against the rules and went
on to live happily till the ripe age of 96..

I personally think some of his actions/comments are to the extreme, but his
intentions were noble.. The unfortunate result is that the non-brahmin
upper castes hijacked this self-respect movement and made it an anti-
brahmin movement..even though they've became the oppressing castes now..

I really feel sad to discuss this again and again..
Caste system is an outdated system and the sooner we get rid of this, the
better..


Cheers,
Naren.

minorities.

out the truth, instead of sticking to the old history told by the
westerners? If we think talking the truth will create hatered, then
the country will never live in peace.

everynow and then, the holocaust is remembered and reverberated

Germans remember the holocaust every now and then, because they
repent for what has been done and wanted to remind the world that
such things should not happen again. They have a sense of guilt which
they repent for. Jews and German are not fighting and killing each
other after the Holocaust.

But what happens in India..The Hindu Holocaust is much more higher
than the Jews holocaust. Then why no exhibition is conducted to

sidelining them saying that in older days the uppercaste suppressed

History is the reflection of our culture. Its just like a mirror.
