-Hi chandra

fantastic recreation of your trip

3 things.

it is maravarman sundara pandya. not maya varman/ mayavaram either
pandya kings have the titles jada varman maravarman etc.

the fort in tranquebar was built by danish not dutch

there are several temples to shiva seeing the sea. its just that
sometimes the sea embraces and swallows the temple. old timers of
chennai remember kasiviswanathar on the ennore highway before it fell
into the sea.

even the seaside temple in mahabalipuram has a shiva lingam inside.
