Efforts never fail

by kalinga_tamil on Sun Sep 24, 2006 11:22 pm

Dear All

I am forwarding this letter with the hope and belief
of getting your support for the cause of compilation
of comprehensive directory of the Rulers of south - east India and
vassals in allegiance to British/ Indian Princely
States e. g. Zamindars , by whatever name called in different parts
of Orissa.

This is aimed to encompass the basic data of the
State/ Estate (like situation, extent, population,
revenue, major achievements, palaces & forts, etc.), a
concise history of its establishment and development,
present encumbents and their clan affiliations and
relations.


This in turn requires the compilation of list, history
and genealogical tables of Rulers/ Jagirdars from the
respective Princely States, Land Revenue Records and
enactments like Oudh Estate Act 1869, etc.

I am in a process to approach all leading members of different
raja/zamindhar families to provide me with this information.

I am seeking your help to get me as many details/
contact information in respect of above Raja/zamindhar
families either by providing me the proper links of
the families or referring to the records.

since last 10 months , involving with Talcher pricely state Raja to
renovate their palace 1880 builted in Talcher.And also collected
details of History
and genealogical tables of Talcher Rulers cultural heritage and
history of Talcher.

To promote the palace allready started with summer camp at heritage
palace premises on 27th and 28th of this month with warm support from
the citizen of Talcher. Talcher going to be a international tourist
spot very soon.Talcher family even having marriage relations with
chennai.

Mr.Premkumar current cheif secretary of maharastra married to sneha
of Talcher.

I hope to get your support for the project

Thanks

S.Balasubramani B+ Bhubaneswar Orissa
