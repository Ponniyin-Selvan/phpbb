When I say atma is free, it is free from everything..let it be karmic
bondage or will to act on its own. As the upanishads say, two birds on
the tree, one eating the fruit and other seeing it (adam eve story is
said to be based on this), the bird watching the other bird eating
fruit is the atma and the bird that is eating the fruit is the 'I'.
