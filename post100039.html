Dear Dhivakar,

Appar hymns at Thiruvalanchuzhi :

" Malangu pay vayal soozhntha valanchuzhi (Thiru- Valanchuzhi Surrounded
by irrigated fields)
Valam kolvar adi en thalai melave (who ever is taking round and seek
the blessings of the Lord siva over here, their Lotus Feet shall be
kept on my Head " !

He is thrilled to hymn "Naam paravum Thiruvadi En thalai mel vaithar
Nallur Emberumanar .. "

Nallur Emberumanar - Siva - has kept his Lotus feet on my Head !

RRC completes some of his inscriptions : " Iththarmam kaappaar Shree
paathama en thalai melena " who ever is protecting this dharma, their
Lotus feet on my Head "

Kulothunga II - ascended to throne in 1133 AD.

In his Meykeerthi he described : Himself with his Crown Queen
Bhuvanamuzhuthum udayal adorned the Throne like siva and Parvathi
adorning the Golden peaks of Kailash !

His Minister was Sekkzihar.

Kulothunga modified his meykeerthi after Sekkizhar's Periyapuranam :

With the Blessings of siva and Parvathi adorning the Golden peaks of
Kailash , me and my wife adorn Chozha throne !

After visiting Thiruvarur temple, he again modified his meykeerthi :

" Anabayan (Kulothunga II) is the bee at the Lotus feet of Lord
Nataraja who dances in thillai "

And in Thiruvalangadu (near Kumbakonam) temple there is a stone
sculpture showing Kulothunga III bearing two " Padukas" on his Head !

best regards / sps

Dear SPS and Venkatesh,
Yes. I know the link between Srisailam and TVMarudur. But where is Where is Thiruppudai Maruthur situated? Please let me know and I will visit the place.

Srisailam : As Venkatesh said, Sundarar might not have visited Sri Sailam. But his descriptions on Peacocks and Deers dancing on the way to Hill shrine were beutiful. Sundarar has not described Paruppatha Nathar (Mallikaarjuna Swamy) but Apparpiraan did. All his (Sundarar's) ten poems on Srisailam on its natural beuty only. (It is a surprise one, because he would not leave the place without describing his Friend).

Srisailam is also one of eighteen Sakthi Peetam and it is said that here Shiva started the life of human being by creating Sakthi. SHE is behind HIM. Also it is said that Arjuna got pasupaathaasthra here from HIM after a fight. Sanscrit Poet Bharavi wrote Kiritaarjuniam, a great epic work here, it is said. 'Kanchipuram was mentioned in his work as best of learned cities. (PurusEshu Vishnu, NagarEshu Kaanchi..........)

Kumara Gupta's (one of last kings of Great Guptas) daughter stayed here long time and like our AndaaL, she surrended herself to Malligaarjunswamy and got married to Him. There is also a great connection with western ghat (after Chickmagalur in Karnataka) tribes to Swamy here. They treat Him as their son-in-law and every year for Mahasivaraathri, thousands of people throng Sivasailam and stay there for a night. Swamy, as said by SPS, is jyotirlingam and every one can go inside and put their head on Him. Absolutely there is no restriction between Him and His adiyaars.

Anbudan
Dhivakar.

You have performed AbhishEkam. Great. Actually AbishEkam waters are being brought out from PaataaLa Ganga, that is Krishna River in down streams. Even today, the Lingayats wear around their necks, the Linga stones obtained from the bed of the Pathalaganga at Srisailam, preserving them in little caskets made of wood or silver.

Adhi Sankara stayed here for some time and described Mallikarjunaswamy in his 'Sivaananda Lahari' and also in 'Dwadasa Linga stothra'. He also sang Goddess Brahmaraambika in his Brahmarambika Ashtaka.

I visited Sri Sailam many times
