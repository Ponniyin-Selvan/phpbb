by seshadrigokul on Thu Mar 27, 2008 12:01 am

Dear Thiru

Pl see if the following makes sense to you:

Step 1 - Let an automated web program select first 500 or 1000
messages. It will group all messages appearing in one topic into one
article. Format will be:

TITLE OF THE ARTICLE (ORIGINAL MESSAGE THREAD)
: - Box 1
: - Box 2

Each of these messages will appear in their boxes and there will be
one page holding all the boxes. (if the subject heading has changed
during discussion it will be listed under different heading)

Step 2 - A first level editor (one of the members who nominates and
volunteers for this effort) will review this boxes and the whole
article. he will rearrange boxes, remove irrelevant messages etc. NO
CONTENT EDITING - JUST ARRANGEMENT OF BOXES. tHIS IS THE ZEROTH DRAFT

Step 3 - Out of first 500 messages, we will examine if some of the
messages of this article have gone into other articles (due to subject
change). Such boxes will be "IMPORTED" using import function and will
be deleted from original articles

Step 4 - One the article is in decent shape, ALL BOXES WILL BE
COLLAPSED AND WE WILL TITLE IT UNDER A SINGLE ARTICLE. Now a second
level editor reviews, edits content and we publish - FIRST DRAFT

Step 5 - members read it and comment and this article may be refined

Looks like a complicated project - but is the approach workable?

Thanks
Gokul.S
