Hi sps

actually many archeologists feel finding stone age or iron age
settlements are not that rare.

especially gift siromoney used to give a list of tips to find
ancient
settlements. he suggests look for hills next to lakes. and then
look
around for caves or rock over hangs on the hills.
using a compass which responds to magnetism he said we could find
iron smelting sites pretty fast. the slag ( by product of melting)
still remains.

remember he found cavemen settlements next to gst road in
guduvancher
and a host of them on hills in vandalur kelambakkam area.

we are finding quite a few burial urns these days. surprisngly
some
were found inside thirukadayur temple itself.
venketesh
