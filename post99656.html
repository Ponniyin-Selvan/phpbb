Dear Friends,

AYYANAR was also known as SAATHAN. " Saathanai Maganaga Kondar" says
Appar.
There were TWO CELESTIAL WHITE ELEPHANTS. 1. Airavatham of Indira. 2.
Airavanam of Siva. Saathanar @ Ayyanar was the Mauth of Airavanam -
Siva's white elephant.

when Siva wanted to bring Sundara to Kailash, he sent Airavanam with
Saathanar ..to Vanchi - Chera's capital where Sundara was with
Cheraman Perumal.
Seeing that Cheraman uttered " Namasivaya" in the ears of his Horse
which also reached Kailasam with Airavanam where Cheraman sung
Thirukkayilaya Ula in front of Lord Siva.

This was brought to the Earth by MaaSaathan and sung at Thiruppattur @
Thiruppidavur - between Samayapuram and Perambalur.

Even today there is Ayyanar temple in Thiruppattur bearing
inscriptions of RAJENDRA CHOZHA and subsequent rulers !


sps

=========================================
Earlier sps wrote :...

RRCs ambition to reach the abode of Siva is also explicit from the
portraits of Cheramanperumal - Sundarar !

Therefore Emperor Rajraja - like the Great Ashoka after Kalinga War
renouncing Power - also would have become to be known as a
"siva-devotee" ... and in this process Easana Siva Pandithar would
have played some inportant role... as Sarva siva pandithar would have
played important role in Rajendra calling himself Siva Sarana Sekaran !

MUSH ETC.

horses in front of Ayyanar .. Our Ayyanar is son of Siva !

thogai by Sekkizhar in Periyapuranam.

siva) temple - " YOUR FATE LINE CAN BE CHANGED " is Today's
Slogan !) is Thevarasthalam is to be ascertained.
