The Udayarkudi inscriptions read some thing like : " Veerapandiayan thalai
konda Aadhitha Karikalanai kondra DUROHIGALANA Irumudi Chozha
Brahmadhirayanana Iravidasanum, Ivan thambi Parameswaranum ....
kiramavithanum ... " etc., etc.,

Irumudi Chozha Brahmadhirayar has to be a Minister as the Throne had gifted
him,
his brothers,
family members,
his- in -laws,
his sisters' in-laws,
Sambandhis (Vettagathar) & others

- fertile lands on the Banks of Veeranam - the premium lands in the Chozha
Empire !

If one reads the Schedules of the lands, most of them are adjoining
a 'Vaykkal " (Like Ganapthy Vaykkal - Veeman Kundavi Vaaykkal etc) - means
direct irrigation from Veeranam was provisioned !

No doubt he was a Minister! And an Influential one! And was well - rewarded
during Uthama's period - all of them were Auctioned in Public and the
interest on the proceeds were utilised for feeding 12 Siva devotees daily
basis.. at Anantheeswara temple of Veeranarayana Chaturvedi Mangalam!

by sps10142004 on Thu Dec 09, 2004 10:39 pm

--- Inponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Sridhar Rathinam"
wrote:
Dear Sri,
Nice to see your mail.

Orumudi :: Original Chozha nadu

Irumudi :: Extended within India;

Mummudi :: Extended overseas

This explanation was given by Dr.Rajavelu ASI when we both, with a couple
of friends, read the Udayarkudi inscriptions about 6 yrs back.

Kanthalur Chaalai is again an inspiring matter to write about. It figures
from RR's 3rd Regnl Yr whereas Udayarkudi inscriptions were of 2nd Regnl
Yr.

In the First Rgnl Yr of any King, inscriptions were never recorded.

Obviously after Udyarkudi, Kandalur chalai was the priority for RR! As
earlier mentioned in the Group, Rajendra Chozha Devar was the Maadanda
nayakan of this Great War !

Probably Ealam was already under Chozha's rule. Hence RR called himself
Mummudi Chozhan.

Looking from another plane : An obscured Arumozhi till 984 - shares the
Chozha throne in 985 with Uthama, declares himself CHAKRAVARTHY and Sri
Rajaraja Devar in 987 after punishing the culprits responsible for AK's
murder - obviously after eliminating Uthama, defeats Chera & Pandya -
calling himself MUMMUDI CHOZHA !

This event in particular and the general definition of Mummudi Chozha
mandalam (Zones - created for Administrative reasons) may have looked at
different perspectives, I personally think.

I also would like to speculate that after expulsion from Veeranarayana
Chaturvedi mangalam, Ravidasan with all his relatives, might have been
offered asylum in Kanthalur chaalai by the Cheras. There might be a
possibility that they were KC - trained (like 36 Shaolin chambers!) prior
to AK's murder! When they were called Durohins, it is clear that they
deceived the popular perspection.. During the course of trials, RR might
not have wanted to award them capital punishment for several reasons ... He
might have hunted them down in Kandalur chaalai and had the satisfaction of
eliminating them enmass in War with fair opportunity to show their strength!

There must be very strong reasons for Arumozhi for calling himself -
RAJARAJAN - CHAKRAVARTHY & MUMMUDI CHOZHAN within just 3 yrs of ascendancy
to the Chozha Throne! Even he could not have dreamt that he was heading to
make the Best innings in next 27 years - which the World talking about for
next thousand years or more !

Re pazhuvettarayars of Chera origin - which clan was so powerful 150 yrs
prior to RR, lost all its power and ceased to be mentioned - save the 7th
Regnl yr inscription of Rajendra - means next 37 years! That too was
pertaining to a mention of a servant maid!

fondly, sps
