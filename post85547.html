Dear Sri,
now that you reminded me , alwarkadiyan has a big fight witht the
shaivates and is brought before the prime minister.
and i think he reprimands the pm for having deserted the vaishnavite
faith.
and kalki in the words of adiyan asks the pm are you so and so's
grandson, are you not so and so's son
the actual words i remember came from the anbil plates.

well i dont have a copy of PS near me and i depend on your
photographic memory

is the naval voyage of adiyan described? d
oes kalki talk in detail about the adams bridge ?
i just dont remember seeing anything like that?
for kalki was very vocal in describing the kodikarai to lanka trip.

regards
