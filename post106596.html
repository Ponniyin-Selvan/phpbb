SapthaVidanga temples

by dmaloo on Sun May 11, 2008 10:44 pm

What I know about this - Vaalan endru oru arakkan who was harassing
Devas. So Indra took the help of Muchukunda Chozha (whether there was
really a king like this I don't know). Anyways Muchukunda killed the
rakshasa and helped Indra, Indra asked him for what he wanted in
return, being a shiva devotee he wanted a non linga, moorthy image of
Shiva at Tiruvarur. Since Shiva could not take human form due to a
curse by Bhrigu, Indra was in a dilemma - and decided to test
Muchukunda by showing him 7 identical idols of Shiva, hoping he may
not identify the right one to install at Tiruvarur. Muchukunda
identifes the original with Shiva's grace and his bhakthi removes the
shadow on Shiva too that he cannot be present in idol form.

7 idols were installed Thiruvarur , Thirukkuvalai , Thiruvaimur ,
Thirukkaravasal , ThiruNallar , Vedaranyam and Nagappattinam . These
temples are called SapthaVidanga Temples.

by shobhainsf on Mon May 12, 2008 7:29 am

Hi Malathi,
The legend I know goes like this.
When Musukunda killed the rakshasa Indra told him to ask for anything that he had other than Indrani. Indra had a lingam (maragatha lingam) which he was worshipping & Musukunda asked for it. Now Indra was in a dilemma & he said he will get it the next day & made 6 more asking the King to choose the right one. Siva who was annoyed 'cos Indra considered Indrani more precious identified himself to the king helping him choose the correct one. Indra gave away all 7 & the king kept them in the 7 temples you mentioned. They are called Vidangars because they were made without the Uli (i.e. not sculpted ).
The maragatha lingams are the Vidangars not the human form idols. (Group Correct me if I am wrong)
Regards
