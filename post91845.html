Hi all ,
Can I make a suggestion?
Why not conduct a e-workshop on PS?This could help focus on all
issues of relevance-literary,historical,geographical,cultural and so
on.It can even be a rolling one in which as and when some one has
arrived at a significant contribution he can share it with others.On
the literary side I have in mind critical analyses of the kind that
have been made on Shakespeare's plays,including possible scripts for
theatrical presentations;on the geographical side it can be
travellogues and identification of spots,rivers etc;the historical
side seems well charted out and needs hardly any elucidation;on the
cultural/political side gender issues,monarchy and imperialism
etc.Contemporary issues such as renaissance in Tamil literary forms
such as historical novel,stylistic issues etc constitute another
fascinating area.This way the diverse talents and energies of the
group can be channelised towards greater participation,awareness and
integration.Perhaps it is a somewhat heavy itinerary but nothing
worthwhile is achieved thro just casual and informal remarks but
only thro sustained, enthusiastic and systematic efforts which should
be well documented.
GR
