Dear Visitor/Member


Welcome to Ponniyin Selvan Varalaatru Pervai !

If you are interested in tamil History - Chola History in particular and also Ancient Tamil Sangam period to contemporary in general, then you are among the like minded ones !

Join us in taking a walk down the memory lane to the glorious golden days of imperial Cholas. A prosperous period in indian history and heritage. Share your views and enjoy yourself through discussions, debates and meet the scholars.

If your thoughts and passion are more than just admiring tamizh literary work, if you share our thirst in restoring and preserving tamizh language and heritage, please join us.

We have members from all walks of life, including Doctors, Researchers, Writers, Software Engineers, Media persons, retired officials, students and the like, from all over the World in all the age groups.

Would request you to spend some time and read the article below. Thought is is bit lengthy, you may find lot of information on what is available to you and how you can use it.

Etiquette

As a group we have members including Doctors, Researchers, Writers, Software Engineers, Media persons, retired officials, students and the like, from all over the World in all the age groups and we SERIOUSLY respect their privacy, time and DO NOT encourage the following

  • SPAM Mails
  • Promotion emails not relevant
  • Subject not at all relevant to the group
  • Bashing individual member(s) - Please do this offline

** You have been warned **

Our group will be very active (usually active) some time and you may get tons of emails per day. If you do not want to receive so many emails, then don't worry, we have options for you. Please go through each options mentioned below, see how it will suit your need and use it.

1. Sign up for GMail (Seriously)

Yes. GMail allows you to keep messages posted in a thread as a single email (which contains all the messages, refer the image below). But still you will as many emails as many topics/thread posted.



2. Digest only

This option will send only one email every day with the messages sent previous day.


3. Opt for Web Only

You have to manually log into yahoo group everyday and check the messages yourself.


4. RSS

If you are tech savvy and wants to read the messages along with your other RSS feeds. Then subscribe to the RSS feed using url http://feeds.ponniyinselvan.in/PonniyinSelvan

5. Instant Messenger

If you are impatient that you are not getting email notification immediately and want to be alerted immediately, then signup @ http://www.imfeeds.com, add the RSS url http://feeds.ponniyinselvan.in/PonniyinSelvan, configure your instant messenger account.

6. Twitter

Again, if you are tech savvy and like/use twitter, you can read the messages at http://www.twitter.com/ponniyinselvan
