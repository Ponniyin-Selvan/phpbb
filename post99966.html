Dear Mr. S.AN, Thank you very much.
I just attended a meet in the Chennai Press Club on a subject of interest
to our Group. The dignitary addressed the Press was Dr. Proko - former Vice
Chancellor, Madras University. The gentleman organised the meet out of his
own funds is Mr. TKV Rajan.

Dear Mr. VK, Sorry I did not see your mail earlier. That's why did not
respond. I could not see anyone other than the press persons. Have missed
each other there?

Re details : The meet commenced at 1615 hrs.

I had an opportunity to have an informal chat with Dr. Proko and promptly
projected the nature of activities and interests of our Group. He was
astonished to realise the involvement of Tamils, particularly those living
abroad on matters of such interest. Mr. TKV Rajan and Dr.Porko mentioned
high of PS activities during course of the meet.

Basically they declared that as per carbon dating the Script found on the
Adhichanallur urn is dated about 1500 BC means about 3500 yr old. The
earliest script known to Indian History is that of Ashoka dated about 300
BC means about 2300 yr old.

Dr. Porko vouched that the earliest Book (script) is Tholkappiam and no
other Indian language is having script comparable being such ancient;

.. Earliest Tamil inscription is 500 BC of Sangam age, prior to Ashoka's.
Though, these facts are not properly projected in the Educational system or
through the media all these years;

We should shed our shy and come forward to proclaim the ancient nature of
our language, script, culture and heritage. We should not leave any effort
in creating more awareness on this among the International community.

DD news, Jaya TV,Dinathanthi, Dinamalar are among the leading media
personnel present.

Essentially it is an attempt to light the first candle .. much more should
follow..

Hope the press reports the meet more in detail..

fondly, sps
