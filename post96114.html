The poll query can be aptly answered by the following Thiru Kural:

Piniyinmai selvam vilaivinbam yemam
Aniyenba naatirkkiv vainthu.

Which ruler is having those traits to provide these five things,
he/she is considered to be a great king/queen. And those traits are
the greatest.
