Hi Malathi,

Whoa.. Louisville eh?:) I was there in February this year for an interview..
got the job but finally decided to move back to India instead

Anyway, I understand the need to conserve and be environmentally friendly. I
am that way too. I also completely agree with our need to reduce dependence
on pesticides etc. The GM crops that are being researched into are mainly
for that.. how to reduce pesticides.. how to reduce infection by pests.. how
to boost yield/acre.. so unlike what is being made out.. agri-biotech firms
are not creating franken-foods.. they are trying to solve a very real
problem.

Like I said, organic food is not something everyone can afford.. and with
the population exploding, we need to find ways to improve our yield.. there
are different techniques being tried including molecular breeding as well as
biotech food.
