I have a 'kavithai cum Kurunovel' called "Thaneer Desam" by Kavignar
Vairamuthu in PDF format.

For those who do not know about the book :
-------------------------------------------

It is a master piece of Vairamuthu about 'KADAL'(Sea). Yes...It is a
small novel but in poetic acsent with lot statistical as well as
geographical information about the sea and very interesting. It
contains all the different flavors of a novel like science, suspense,
terror, romance, humor and so on.

Padikka aarambicha, mudikkira varaikkum book-ai keezhe vaikavey manasu
varadhu..avalo interesting-a irrukkum.

If any of u are interested to read the book, just ask me.
