Conference in Vellore by Tamilnadu Archeological Society

by adhitha_karikalan on Sun Jun 29, 2003 1:04 am

Hi Friends,

Tamilnadu Archeological Society, Chennai is conducting a two days
conference in Vellore on 19th and 20th of July. Lot of researchers
are going to participate and many research papers are going to be
submitted. Every year they are conducting in various places of
tamilnadu and this year it is in Vellore fort. Approximately 125
people are going to attend.

Mr. Rajavelu of ASI is organising the function this year and
contacted me last week. He asked for some contribution or compliments
for the conference on behalf of our PS eGroup and i said that i would
get back to him after discussing with the group members.

Myself and poonguzhali have discussed about this and we propose the
idea of giving scribbling pad and ball point pen to the viewers. And
thought of preparing a rubber seal with our group name and url and
can stamp in the scribbling pad.

What do you say?

Thanks
Kamal

Conference in Vellore by Tamilnadu Archeological Society

by adhitha_karikalan on Wed Jul 02, 2003 12:25 am

Hi Friends,

I spoke to Mr. Rajavelu and came to know that already
another party has committed to contribute pen/notepad
set. So, it's ruled out. Instead, he suggested that we
can give the cash itself whatever the pen/notepad set
costs as per our budget. That will be used for food
expenses.

Another alternate idea is, Mr. Sundar Bharadwaj is
contributing a partial amount for preparing
complimentary hand bags. We can add our money also to
that amount and ask them to print our group url on the
bag. What do you say?

And i will check with Mr. Rajavelu about the banners /
flyers also.

This is not just to promote our group. If we show our
presence and acheivements, we will get noticed by the
scholars who can provide support and guidance for our
future activities.

This conference is not conducted by ASI. There is a
private organization called 'Tamilnadu Archeological
Society' run by the employees of ASI and other
historians. They only conducted the Iravatham
Mahadevan's Nool veliyeettu vizha two months back. It
was started 10 years ago and Dr. Kalaikkovan was
heading the group then. Now, Dr. Se. Irasu ( Retd. HOD
of Inscriptions dept, Tamil university Tanjore ) is
the vice-president. It is more or less similar to our
group when it was started. We can also become members
of this society by paying some nominal subscription
amount. I will provide the details of joining fees
next week.

Please let me know your opinion on complimentary hand
bags.

Thanks
Kamal
