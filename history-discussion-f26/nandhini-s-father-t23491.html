work provided by Kalki ...(mail 188)

Excellent work ... Not only PS is to be re-serialised.. Even the PS
mails also!

Views are :
1. NANDHINI was daughter of Veerapandian
2. Nandhini was daughter of Sundara
3. Nandhini was daughter of Karuthiruman.

Possibility (3) is eliminated by Gokul clearly.

Permit me to make an attempt within the frame work provided by Kalki:

VOL III : Chapter 45 : Vaanathikku Abaayam :

Arumozhi expresses to Kundavai (in Nagai after the ship mishap) of
having seen the "Cauverythai" in Lanka. " That Ommaithai is our
Periamma. Our mother is now sitting in throne which she should have
occupied. Inspite of it she has been showering affection on me"
Kundavai narrates Sundara's confession. Vandhiyathevan tells
Arumozhi that she resembled Nandhini. Kundavai accepted her as her
sister willing to apologise for her misfortunes..

Four main characters confirm the husband-wife relationship between
Mandakini & Sundara.

Vol IV : Ch 29 : Rajadarisanam
Mandakini sees Sundara after several decades .. spontaneous flow of
love - rather reflow!

Ch: 32 : ENNAI YAEN VADHAIKKIRAY?
Sundara - after seeing Mandakini - thinks that he has again seen her
ghost image (which was earlier enacted by Nandhini) and thows a
lamp.Kindavai rushes in and clafies to him that she is not the ghost -
but her PERIAMMA.
Ch: 33 : Chozhar Kula Deyvam :
Empress Vanavanmadevi confirms that Mandakini saved Arumozhi in the
Cauvery as she has personally witnessed the event. Aniruddhar - with
whom Sundara made a confession in early ages - confirms Mandakini -
Sundara nexus. It is also revealed that Mandakini was Kodikkarai
Thyagavidangar's sister (and Karayar Magal !) Poonguzhali was also
present in the scene.
Ch: 35 ::Chakravarthiyin Kobam : ANIRUDHAN tells Sundara ;
"... please do not talk about punishing Nandhini .. she is not only
the Ilayarani of Pazhuvettarayar.. but also THE DAUGHTER OF
SUNDARACHOZHA...." Sundara calls him made but did not refute...

I fail to see where Kundavai has told anyone that Nandhini was born
two years after Sundara departed ...

It is not possible to imagine another MAN in Mandakini's life other
than Sundara. Nandhi's resemblence to Mandakini is not only
confirmed by all the main characters, but also by the passers-by of
Mandakini when she was brought to Thanjai by Poonguzhali...Her
dumbness was mistaken as the arrogance of (Pazhavoor) Nandhini...

Then where is the confusion?

Fondly, SB

THANK YOU PAVITHRA. I THINK I HASTENED UP & MISSED SOME IMPORTANT
PARTS OF THE NOVEL EARLIER... I READ VOL V - 52 TILL THE END LAST
NIGHT :

It is stated that Madurantakan and Nandhini are twins;
Both are born to Mandhakini - near Thanjai - when she was with Vani;
Semibianmadevi's new born son was mistaken as dead and was about to
be buried by deaf & dumb Vani, but Karuthiruman saved the child - who
is Sendan Amudhan.
The girl baby Nandhini was taken care by Thirumalai and went to
Lanka. Nandhini was Veerapandiyan's daughter.

To maintain the consistancy with respect to Vol III, IV & V, it can
be summarised that :

Nandhini & Madurantakan were born to Mandakini & Sundara -
Mandakini became insane ...
Nandhini was nurtured by Veerapandian in Lanka and tuned to become
Chozha revenger - (Like We see Villain Nambiar tuning a Police
Officer's son as a Thief in Twin MGR Story Kudiyirundhakoil - What a
wonderful Title! for Mother) and thus Nandhini was born to Sundara,
but her VALARPPU THANDHAI was Veerapandiya ...

Otherside, Madurantaka - inspite of growing in Chola heartland was
tuned to become Chozhakulanasini ... Atleast Nandhini would not have
approved her brother to bear the name Madurantaka - if they were VP's
children...

If Nandhini was not Sundara's daughter, it could have been easier for
her to accept AK's love & sit with him in Chozha throne. That itself
is a revenge & run the Kingdom as Pandya-cholas!

If she was Sundara's daughter alone without VP's influence, even
Kundavai & Anirudh were willing to reconcile with her...

The combo - born to Sundara & brought up by Veerapandia - did not
pave her any way in a specific direction ...

Out of the two options of (1) born to & brought up by Veerapandia
(2) born to Sundara & brought up by Veerapandia, I favour the Second
option. Third option, born to some one else and brought up by
Veerapandia - appears to be a diluted version ....

BY MAKING SUNDARA'S CHILDREN REVOLT AGAINST THEIR FATHER,
VEERAPANDIAN ACHIEVED HIS BEST AND IN THE PROCESS ELIMINATED ADITHA
KARIKALAN ALSO.

If that Madurantakan occupied the thone, it would have been PANDIYA
CHOLAR's Rule in Thanjai .... Very much cunning ..

In Ponniyinselvan, we have seen several similarities to
Mahabaratha .. Nandhini version is similar to Karna ...

But the reading provided other interesting materials :

1. Kalki talks so much about Marghazhi - Thiruvadhirai (Birth star of
Rajendra)..
2. When Kundavai & Vanathi see Arumozhi & Vandhiathevan near
Thirivaiyaru, Kundavai complains that Arumozhi has fallen love with
Iruniulamadnthai ... (This is the beginning of the Meykeerthi of
Rajendra ..)
3. K & V see Kailasakatchi in Thiruvaiyaru (In Thiruvaiyaru South
Kailasam was built by Sembianmadevi and (Vada)North Kailsam by
Rajendra:: Here RR's provided asylum to Vimladitham first who later
became his daughter Kundavi's husband)
4. Kaliasam is the key word in RR's mind which made him
construct "Dakshina Meru" - Big Temple in Thanjai...

It is clear the last few Chapters were fore-runners for Rajaraja &
Rajendra's Successful Stories ... Very unfortunate that Kalki could
not live long and accomplish these ...

Dear Sri,

Re Nandhini's father, I developed the line of thinking based on the
fact that the Novel was written in 1950, when woman dignity was more
protected on the story telling. Even in present days, like in
Sindhubairavi, Bairavi characterisation is subtly done by KB without
affecting the dignity of her or her mother.

Same way, it would have been difficult for Kalki to explain that
Mandakini bore the twins for VP when she was sane and was already in
deep love -which did not get extinguished for nearly 25 years - (as
per PS) - with Sundara. If VP chose to have an affair with an insane
woman, it is more inapporpriate.

Hence as all the important characters concurred and accepted Nandhini
as Sundara's daughter - to explain Veerapandian discrepancy - I
suggested she was born to one and brought up by other.

Other views are most welcome and as you rightly do - pave way for
more healthy mails in the group.

Re Nandhipurathu nayakai, there is another sequel as Vandhiathevan
vaall (Sword!) - serialised in Amudhasurabi in the 80s.

Can't touch with 24 foot pole!

Fondly, SB
