100 year old books

by abhivencat on Tue Apr 17, 2007 8:26 am

Hi
I had a trip to devakottai in chettinaad on 15-16 th.
as a result some 150 books printed between 1897-1940 have come into
my possession.
some rare editions of jeevaka sinthamani( 1890) auvudaiyar koil
puranam ( 1903) included.

also one cookery book printed in 1895.some of them are in excellent
condition.
dear chandra and Uma- 3 books of suddhananda barathi also were in
that trove.

dear V.K I need your help to assess the usefulness of the books.

venketesh

100 year old books

by abhivencat on Tue Apr 17, 2007 11:30 am

Hi Vk
sure we should meet.

i was in particular fascinated by the temple puranams.
almost all major temples had a full puranam written in verse. we have
very few in hand. mainly major temples like madurai, thiruvannamalai
etc,
in this set i got the puranams of auvudaiyar koil, thirupunavasal(
the biggest lingam among thevaram shrines ) and even uppur. uppur
veyil ukantha vinayakar temple was established by Rama in legend on
the way to lanka. now its a minor temple.
I have asked people to search in specific for more temple puranams.

venketesh
