3)is mamallapuram a finished work?
No - unfinished in many places.

You should remember two things.

- Many monuments have been left unfinished inspite of political
stability. Classic example raajarajeswaram unfinished in 50 and odd
places

- When one kartha initiates a work, another normally does not take it
up even if it is left unfinished. Rajendra could have easily completed
the 108 karanas of raajarajeswaram if he wanted to. But he did not
