I am following the below mentioned podcast of SS. Its a good effort for which I am really grateful to Sashi Vaidyanathan. I sincerely hope that either she or others who can do it as well as her, do so with other Tamil classics by Sandilyan etc. Currently my wife who knows Tamil well, reads Kaviri Mainthan to me.

However, I think, for the world as a whole, if we dont bring out and show the world our treasures and our merits, no body else will do it for us. The translations of PS and Cilapathikaram have been exceptional. I hope these authors or authors of similar merit take up other works and do it.

Best regards
Sandeep
