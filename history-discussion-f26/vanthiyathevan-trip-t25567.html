vanthiyathevan trip

by abhivencat on Tue Apr 03, 2007 5:46 pm

Hi

the following is what swetha has compiled.
if people are willing to cut one monday this month end ( may 1 is a
tuesday)it can be a comfy 4 day trip and no night travel.


Here is the itinerary she put together. See if it is viable in the
first place and if she has covered all the places.

Day 1 : Chennai - ECR - Chidambaram - veeraanam -Kadambur -
kudanthai- Thanjavur - Night Halt.
Day 2 : Thanjavur - Thiruvaiyaru - pazhuvur -Thirumazhapadi -
Thanjavur - Night Halt.
Day 3 : Thanjavur - Thiruvarur - Nagappattinam - Vedaranyam -
Kodikkarai - Karaikkal - Night Halt
Day 4 : Karaikkal - Tharangampadi - Thirukkadaiyur - Thiruvenkadu -
Chidambaram - ECR - Chennai

Day 3 is cramped. We should wind up quickly on Day 2 so that we start
early on Day 3, complete Thiruvarur and Nagai, take lunch in Nagai
and then proceed to Vedaranyam.

chidambaram, tanjore and karaikal we have people to get us good
lodging.logistics may not be tough.

regards
venketesh
