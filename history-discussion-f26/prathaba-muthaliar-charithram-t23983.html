Prathaba muthaliar charithram

by rojatraders on Tue Aug 23, 2005 1:04 am

This is the first novel in tamil.But it can be read even today.Too
hilarious.The beginning part where prathaba muthali is a kid is too
hilarious to be described.

Prathabamuthali does great deeds.He is born to a zamindar
family,marries his fiancee jnamba after many twists and turns,gets
seperated from her and reunites with her,gets lost and becomes king
of a funny kingdom,gnanmba comes there,saves him .....subam

In the first tamil novel,heroine is given equal importance and is
often shown as being more intelligent than prathabamuthali.She even
argues her case with british and saves hero and her family from
ending up in prison.She disguises herself as a male to save her
husband,becomes a king and rules the kingdom well.

New editions of this book are availaible in the market.Makes a very
good reading.

regards
sampath
