SUN TV Interview- Shot & Canned

by pavithranivas on Sat Mar 29, 2003 10:35 pm

Dear all...


It took an hour and a half to wrap it, but it was done. The ponniyinselvan
egroup got itself on TV.


The shoot was to be at Dreams Net, a browsing centre in West Mambalam, at
11.30 AM. Eventually though, the camera crew and the producer (Mr. Arul Raj
and Mr. Jabez) arrived only by 12.30 PM- which was a good thing in a way,
'cause we wanted to get our thoughts in order.


Kamalakannan, I, Lavanya, Anuradha and Krupashankar assembled at Dreams Net
(Phone calls and emails to the other Trippers wasn't fruitful- they couldn't
make it for various reasons). The first thing, of course, was to decide what
we must talk about- and how(because the actual news item is only gonna be 10
minutes- so we had to say a lot of things in short order).

Eventually, we decided that I would do the Introduction part of it, Kamal
would talk about how to enrol as a member to our group and how to access its
facilities, and Lavanya would describe our PS trip. (Krupa and Anuradha
steadfastly refused to appear before the camera:-)

I suppose we were all jittery (I know I was:-)- but we decided to
extemporize, for all that ( notes have a way of failing you when you badly
need info).

The too-bright arclights blazed into existence (dazzling all of us), Mr.
Arul adjusted the camera and instructed me about how to pin the
mike-thingie, the browsing centre people crowded around us (with shushings
to everyone to 'be silent')- and the shoot started.

It all went without a hitch. I said my piece (all about how PS started and
what its reach was,and), Kamal instructed everyone about how to log on to PS
(with details about how to access everything from files to Photos), and
Lavanya spoke about the trip (with acknowledgements to the people who helped
us and the places we visited).

In spite of it all, they shot Anuradha looking thru PS messages on the
Net:-).

When Kamal flicked thru the photo section, the cameraman wanted us to click
on an image of 'Sivagami' (we explained to him that it wasn't 'Sivagami',
but 'Nandhini').

He nodded. "Oho. Appa Naaganandhi padam pOdungga."

It took us some time to explain that Naganandhi wasn't on the photos section
yet, and this was all about PS only.

At 2 PM, and numerous camera angles later, it came to an end. (

After taking some photos, PS groupukku oru periya 'O' pottappuram, we
marched out of the browsing centre, feeling victorious.

The piece on PS is gonna be telecast on Monday (31st March)- at 5 PM (Indian
time)in SUN TV, as a part of the programme 'Chennai Times'. So get ready to
watch it.



Love,
Pavithra.
