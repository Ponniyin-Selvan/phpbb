-----Re: SRIRANGAM :: KULOTHUNGA I & II - GRANTS
SPS WROTE ON 6.9.2007 ::

Dear Vijay,

following references, posted a year back in our Group (SPS wrote on
2.9.2006 ):!

ASI has devoted exclusively Vol XXIV - more than 600 pages in (full
scape) to this Volume, published in 1982. In the Introduction it
mentions "Ramanujacharya, the great apostle of the Vasistadvaitham
Srivaishnavism spent as many as 60 yrs of his long life as
Aministrative head of the temple and effected many reforms .." ( He
lived during 1017 for 120 years - 1136).
No.1 is AR 69 of 1892 attributed to Aditya I (AD 871 - 907) to No:
189 is Chozha period .. upto post Kulothinga I.

ASI adds "To Kulotunga I belong the bulk of the inscriptions 26 to
108 -( 81 inscriptions ( !) and the majority of them are confirned
3rd parakaram range date from 10th to the 48th year .. reclamation
of land. donor purchasing land from temple authorities for periodic
supply of grain, flowers etc. "

Kulothunga I ruled from 1070 to 1122 AD with Vikramachozhan
overlapping 1118 to 1135 AD. Vikrama's 13th year is AD 1130. There
were grants almost every year and in 1140 AD there was a grant by
Kulothunga II.as well.

It is evidenced that during the 120 years of lifetime of Sri
Ramanujar uninterruptedly Chozha dynasty has given grants to
Srirangam temple as could be seen from more 120 inscriptions ..
===============================

SPS WROTE (13499 dt: 01.09.2006)

PRANAMS TO SAINT SRI RAMANUJAR.
====================================================
ENNAYIRAM : Our members are familiar with this place. On our trip
to Thanjavur, we got down about 10 Km after Tindivanam and found a
recent stone pointing the direction of Ennayiram :: Kavi
Kalamegam's birth place. There are stories that 8000 Jains were
put on Kazhu.
Another story that Ashta Saharsra Brahmins hail from this place.
however as desired by SARVA SIVA PNDITHAR - GURU OF RAJENDRA I - a
Siva temple was constructed .. nearby place is Esalam where copper
plates confirming that Aditha Karikalan put Veerpandian's head at
the Fort Entrance of Thanjai was found about 20 years back...
-------------------------
" Pillailogam Jeeyar" in his "Ramanujarya Divya Satidhai" mentioned
that Udayavar (Sri Ramanujar)has visited Thirumalai thrice and each
time he did not fail to visit Thirukkaoilore "Idaikkazhi
Naayanar".

ENNAYIRATHU ECHCHAN a devotee of Udayavar was ordered certain
disciplines : Washing THIRUPPARIYATTAM - clothings of Sree
Viahsnavite devotees (like Meykandar - Sivanadiayar of Nallur! -
Actor Nagesh did this role well in Thiruvilayadal movie). From
Thirukkoilore he went to Ennayiram and Blessed Ennayirathu Echchan.

During Sri Ramanurjar's 120 yr life time (1017 - 1136) Rajendra I (
1012 - 1044) Rajendra II (1052 - 1064) Rajamahendran (1059 - 1062)
veerarajendran (1063 - 1070), Adhirajendran (1068 - 1071)
Kulothubnga I (1070 - 1122), Vikrama chozhan (1118 - 1135) and
Kulothunga II (1133 to 1150) - EIGHT Chozha Kings were in Power.

RRC (985 - 1014) established RAJARAJA CHARUTVEDI MANGALAM which
included Ennayiram. In Ennayiram, AZHAGIYA NARASIMHA PERUMAL
(VINNAGARM) was constructed.

1. ARE 335 of 1917 Rajendra I - 25th yr : the income of the temple
lands should be shared by (1) Moolasthanam udayar temple (2)
Rajaraja Vinnagara Azhwar @ NARASIMHA PERUMAL VINNAGARAM (3)
KUNDAVAI VINNAGARAM (Recall Ponniyinselvan !) @ DADAPURAM
KARIVARADHA PERUMAL TEMPLE and (4) SUNDARA CHOZHA VINNAGARAM. Also
daily poojas of Surya .. sapthamatrika .. saastha.. Gramadevathas
etc., need to be performed ..

2. ARE 341 of 1917: Rajendra I - 30th yr : Aippasi Sadhayam and
Maasi Punarvasu (?) Vizha..

3. ARE 333 of 1917 : Rajendra I - 45 Veli grant for practicising
Thiruvaymozhi, Vedas, Vyakranas, Vedantha and Meemasa sastras..
Four persons appointed to hymn Thiruvaymozhi daily.. 3 kuruni nel
25 Shree vaishanas fed daily.. Aani Anusham Seven day festival.
60 kalam nel. 3 kazhanju gold for 1000 Shree Vaishnavites. Beggers
given food and clothes. There was a Vedapadasala in Gangaikonda
Chozhan mandapam where 270 Brahmacharis studied and were given 6
naazhi nel daily basis. Teachers given 10506 kalam nel.. 8 Kazhanju
for those who taught 8 sargas and 12 for those who taught 12 sargas.

4. ARE 347 of 1917 : On Sreejayanthi (Lord Krishna's birthday ) -
Convocation - each successful student was awarded Gold Ring and
Golden Lotus! Long lists of grants and receivables;

5. ARE 330 of 1917 - Rajadirajan I - Kadaitharam - Least taxing..

6. ARE 340 of 1947 - KULOTHUNGA I - 7th year - Grant of Cows for
burning perpetual lamps.

7. ARE 344 of 1947 - KULOTHUNGA I - 39th year - 50th day :: Under
the auspicious of Puliyur (Our Kodambakkam) Verkkadu Kulothunga
Moovenda velalan PERFORMED TEMPLE RENOVATION, TANK FORMED AND
SAMPROKSHANAM !

ARE 333 of 1917 mentions of similar Samprokshnam by Rajendra I
earlier.

IT IS KULOTHINGA's inscription in his 41st year which is VERY
IMPORTANT :
THRIBUVANACHAKRAVARTHIYIN K-U-L-A-D-E-Y-V-A-M-A-N-A AZHAGIYA
NARASIMHA PERUMAL THIRUKKOIYILIL DEYVA PRADISTAIYUM,
JALAPRADISTAIYUM SEYDARULINAR. HE performed this on the eve of
commencing war against Kalingam !

There are grants from RAJRAJA II (326 OF 1917) and VEERARAJENDRAN
(345 OF 1917).

(Ennayiram) Narasimha Perumal was considered KULADEYVAM of
KULOTHUNGA I... as seen from the above inscriptions ..

We may have to reconsider several things in the light of the above.

sps

DEAR VENKAT / DHIVAKAR,
