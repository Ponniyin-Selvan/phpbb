Our next step -- Meeting with the experts

by adhitha_karikalan on Fri Apr 04, 2003 12:21 am

Hi Friends,

Before the Pudukottai trip, let us have a mind blowing session with
the experts.

We are planning to arrange a conference on Rajaraja chozhan and
Chozha kingdom. We are going to invite the experts ( pazham thindru
kottai pottavargal ) in these subjects who give lectures, discuss on
some controversies raised on the above topics and a question and
answer session. The following are the guests to be invited.

1. Mr. Sundar Bharadwaj

The person who helped us a lot for making the itinerary for the
Mudhal yaathirai and for this conference also.

2. Mr. Kalki Rajendran

Son of Kalki.

3. Mr. Balakumaran

The famous writer who wrote the book Udaiyar on Rajaraja chozhan.

4. Mr. Vairamuthu

The poet, who is interested in preserving the tamil culture and
heritage.

5. Dr. Kalaikkovan

A doctor in Trichy and the founder of Dr. Rasamanickanaar
Kalaikkoodam for carrying out the research on ancient temples and
inscriptions. He only researched and identified that the vimaanam of
thanjavur temple is not a single stone and many stones were merged
like the pieces of an orange fruit.

6. Mr. Devamani Rafeal

A researcher who wrote the book 'Thamizh naattu kalaik koilkal' and
researched many ancient temples in tamil nadu for more than 40 years.

7. Mr. Kudavayil Balu

A researcher in thanjavur Saraswathi mahal library and researching on
olai suvadi and kalvettukal.

8. Mr. Thiyagarajan

A history professor from Ariyalur, who is very much interested in
researching the tamil kingdoms.

9. Mr. Rajavelu

He is working in the Archeology Survey of India department and helped
a lot to preserve the heritage of the old temples.

10. Mr. K.P. Vidhyadharan

An astrologer by profession, who is interested in tamil history.

The date for the conference will be 20-April-2003 Sunday. The
programme will start at 4 PM and go for about 3 hrs.

The venue is to be decided depending upon the no of people attending.
So, i have posted a poll in our group to register for the event.
Please rush your entries before 12-April-2003. It will help us a lot
to arrange a better and comfortable place for the seminar.

The following items will be sold at the conference hall.

1. The VCD of Mudhal Yaathirai
2. The book Udaiyaar by Balakumaran

An exhibition of amazing dolls representing the characters in
Ponniyin selvan created by Mrs. Chitkala will also be there. You can
see some of the dolls in our group's photos section.

Before that,

We feel that if we have an informal meet of our group members who are
all in chennai, it would be better to coordinate well and discuss on
any enhancement to the programme.

We all can meet on 14-April-2003 Monday ( Thamizh varuda pirappu ) at
4:30 PM. The assembling place will be Dr. Natesan's park in
Venkatanarayana road T.Nagar, which is opposite to the corporation
ground.

I hope there won't be any problem in identifying us as you have seen
us in the photos and sun tv.

So, please vote for the registration as soon as possible.

Thanks
Kamal
