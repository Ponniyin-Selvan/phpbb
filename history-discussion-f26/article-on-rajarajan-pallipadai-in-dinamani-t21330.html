Article on Rajarajan Pallipadai in Dinamani

by ratnasabapathy_srihari on Mon Jun 07, 2004 4:34 am

Anbulla Gokul, Sruthi - irruvarkum vanakkam.

I give below the contents of the Brahmadesam Inscription from the
former North Arcot region, of the 26th year, day 120, of Rajadhiraja
Chola - 1 (260 of 1915)which is as follows:

"The assembly which met under a tamarind tree sold land for a water-
pandal for quenching the thirst of the king Sri Udaiyar Rajendra
Choladeva and queen Viramahadeviyar who is said to have entered the
supreme feet of Brahma in the very same tomb in which the body of
king Rajendra Chola was interred. The gift was by senapati
Madhurantakan alias Parakesari Velar who was the brother of the queen"

----
From: the above we note the interred remains of the body of Rajendra
Chola & Viramadevi (who performed udankattai eral), were preserved
and a tomb was built over same. Whether subsequently a pallipadai
Temple was built over same is yet to be known.
