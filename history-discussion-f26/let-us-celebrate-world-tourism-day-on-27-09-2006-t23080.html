TOURISM ENRICHES

A Global Communications Campaign for Tourism
"Tourism enriches individuals, families, communities and all the
world."


Special plans for World Tourism Day
Source Indian Express Bhubaneswar Edition

Wednesday September 20 2006 10:18 IST

BHUBANESWAR: If the Tourism Department has its way, entry to the
Nandankanan Zoological Park, the State museum and tourist places all
over the State will be made free on the World Tourism Day on
September 27.

The Tourism Ministry is contemplating to do this and much more in a
bid to make the occasion eventful.

Letters have been sent to the tourism department, travel and tour
operators, hotel management institutes, hotel industries and all the
people associated with tourism to hold road shows, carnivals,
awareness walk and other unique programmes in sync with the theme of
this year's celebration - `Tourism enriches individuals, families,
communities and all the world.'

The Ministry has suggested displaying banners carrying this year's
theme in front of offices and in busy vantage points. The Tourism
Department may also hold a state-level function with the Chief
Minister gracing the occasion, it stated.

India tourism has decided to accord traditional welcome and present
small mementos to the tourists arriving at Bhubaneswar Airport by
the first flight on the day.

Travel and tour operators may give special reception to the visitors
and gift souvenirs to them.

Besides traditional welcome with complimentary soft drinks could be
offered to guests checking in and out of hotels and restaurants, it
suggested.
