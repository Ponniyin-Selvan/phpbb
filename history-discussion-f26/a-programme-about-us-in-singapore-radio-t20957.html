A Programme about us in Singapore Radio!

by seshadrigokul on Fri Apr 25, 2003 11:26 pm

Dear Members
One more happy news. Today I got an opportutiny to speak about our
group
in Singapore Tamil Radio station Oli 96.8 - for around 5 minutes. I'd
sent
a proposal long back - and they suddenly called me yesterday, recorded
a program and immediately it was on radio! Obviously I had no time to
prepare and what ever I spoke came out spontaneously.... I realise
that my
voice looks funny to myself since I was speaking through a cell phone.

Since it was all too sudden, I could not even inform singapore
members
about the programme.

Our thanks are to Mr.Azhagiya Pandiyan and Mrs. Meenakshi sabapathy
of
Mediacorp's Oli 96.8. Mrs. Sabapathy is a well known figure among the
tamil
speaking populace in Singapore Malaysia and she said she will also
join our
group - after hearing about us.

She has also promised a detailed interview later - since this was too
short.

I've converted the interview to mp3 and uploaded in the following
location :
http://s.gokul.tripod.com/psgroup/Oli1.zip

(Since the file is large, I've posted it here - Krupa avargala -
thayavu
seidhu oru transcript edukka mudiyuma - adhuvum ennai kindal adhigam
seiyamal ?)

This is the first part. If you like it and are able to download it
without
issues, I shall post the second part too.

I think Amarar Kalki is blessing us from heavens - may he guide us
further
in our noble pursuits.

Anbudan
