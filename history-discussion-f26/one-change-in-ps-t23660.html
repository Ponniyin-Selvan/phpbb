I would have made Vanthiyathevan prevent Aditha Karikalan reaching Kadambur.
That would have turned the entire history upside down. Howz that???

I was reading God Father sometime back. Somehow, I kept thinking of the
synergies between PS and this.

Arumozhi, the youngest of the three children of Sundara CHozhar took over
the reins.
Similarly Michael, the youngest of the Three, took over the reins of his
father.

Adithya Karikalan was very rash and hasty in his approach. Doesn't seem to
have a composed mind.
Same is the case with the eldest son of the God Father. (Forgot his name)

Arumozhi spends a few years in a far away land.
Similarly is the case with Michael who stays away from his father during his
studies.

Well, there is no Vanthiyathevan in God Father. The period of the stories
are completely different./ But still I couldn't stop wondering about these
synergies.

LOL. Sounds like the AU (Alternate Universe) fiction I and other fanfiction
writers often attempt. Let me count the ways...
I think I might - I **might** let Adhitha Karikalan live. Takes away the
plot twist - but I think I would make it an assasination *attempt* , instead
of outright murder. I can't reconcile myself to a Chola primce dying, yet.
And I would make Adhitha Karikalan come to his senses, and announce that
Arulmozhi is to be the next Emperor, thus paving the way for Rajaraja,
without bloodshed.
VD goes with Kundhavai. Nothing less for our free spirited knight. They
*belong* together.
And oh.........I would not, not, *not* marry off Vanathi, with her die-away
airs, to our magnificent Arulmozhi. Gah. The Kodumbalur Princess she might
be, but a more droopy heroine I've never seen. Give him a Poonghuzhali, now.
Why would he marry 13 other wives, then?? She'd rip him apart if he did.
{grin}
Although that mightn't be good for politics...
But hey, this is an Alternate Universe.
My 2 paise, folks.
Cheers,
Self-appointed Queen of AU,
