Dear Chandramouli,

Kind of soul-searching .. Nothing is wrong decision., I know a
very
scholarly History professor looking for openings to get into civil
engineering and persue construction .. to earn money ..

At the peak level, Acharyas advise : acquire knowledge to realise
when to renounce the knowledge also ..

But there are openings available in History which can be explored
without disturbing your present system much .. so that you can
revert back fast to the original setting when needed ..

Without resigining or affecting your present system give a try :

Enrole for MA History in any (South) Indian University (age no
problem): Would suggest Annamalai or Bharathidasan (Trichy)
Universities .. 2 year course by Correspondence with about One
Week
contact class per year in the major towns of Tamilnadu.. Get
associated with a local History professor .. it will make study
easier.. If you are able to get through with 55% aggregatge, you
can
directly enrol for Ph D.

Otherwise go for M Phil - History.

One year Correspondence .. another 6 months to submit your thesis
(called desertation). University will furnish you list of guides
from whom you have to chose one .. and prepare your thesis in
coordination..

While doing so, you can get associated with TN State Archeology
Department and ASI (fm where PS Sriraman hails - Central
Archeology).. and enrol as research Scholar using the letter
issued
by your University .. Such letter also will gain you access to
rare
Libraries in your locality.. in Chennai Libraries of ASI, State
Arch, Archives / Egmore.. etc.

And there are Private Research Institutions like Tholliyal
Kazhagam / Thanjai, Dr. Rasamanickanar's.. Trichy, who will be
very
glad to guide you to make you decide your goals and help you to
accomplish ..
