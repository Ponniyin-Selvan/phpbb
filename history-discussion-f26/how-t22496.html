why is this happenning.... enakku theriyala....
Dear Shankar & Seenu,

I think the answer is simple. Contemporary creative literature
Tamil-la romba kammi. Most of them are debates and arguments on fellow
writers and religion, caste, etc. which is of not much interest to the
present generation/no use to the comman men.

Ennadhaan shakesphere, Milton-nu irundhaalum Harry Potter, Lord of the
Rings ellaam varaatti verum academic studies-oda English-ai naamum
neruthi iruppome, illayaa?

In general, debating over an something is easier than writing a new
one. "vivaadhangaldhaan kandupidipugalukku vazhi vagukkum"nu oru
kuruttu nambikkai vera. The knowledge people gain by
debating/critizing is only a byproduct.

So unless there are enough reading material (which should outweigh the
critisisms/fights), no one will be interested in Tamil. Ethanai
naalaikkudhaan Ponniyin Selvan, kamba ramayanam ellaam kaamichu mattum
thamizh-la interest varavazhaikka mudiyum?

Krupa
