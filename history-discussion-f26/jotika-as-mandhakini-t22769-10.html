Guys,

Actually they dont meet each other. But Poonguzali
does see Nandhini in Pachaimalai when Nandhini is
about to run away.(Remember, that after Aditya's
murder they stay in a cave) Initially Poonguzali is
stunned to see Nandhini. She thinks that it is
Mandhakini and then immediately remembers that her
aunt is already dead and ralises that the lady she is
looking at is Pazhuvoor rani.

Please refer to Volume 5 (Thiyaga Chikgaram) in
Chapter 46 (Aazhvanuku aabathu).

Just remembered this incident, then searched for the
exact chapter in the book and thought of sharing it.
