Dear all PS members,

I am also one of the numerous silent mebers! After your repeated requests, it would not be fair if I don't reveal myself!

My name is Atputhananthan. Originally from Yaalpaanam, Sri Lanka. Currently living in UK. Profession: Accountancy. I read PS in the seventies when it came with Vinu's artwork, and enjoyed both the story and the art. My interest in Tamil History stems from reading many novels of Kalki and Saandilyan. Other interests include Religion. So these are common with many members in this group.

I don't participate in discussions primarily because I don't have the deep knowledge that you all have, which I respect. As Tharumi says in Thiruvilaiyaadal எனக்கு கேட்கத்தான் தெரியும்! (Enakku Keakathaan Theriyum!)

I am glad and grateful that through the internet we are able to share the interests and expertise with each other, which was not possible previously. I would love to be in Tamil Nadu and join you on the tours that some of you go on like Vanthiyathevan Trail. One thing I read recently with great interest is the exchanges about Mathan's Answers !

I wonder whether it is possible to buy a copy of PS with Vinu's art ?

I know there is a version available with Maniam's art. We used to have in our home in Urumpirai (in Yaalpaanam), the bound volumes of PS and numerous other novels that were serialised in Kaki in the late sixtes and seventies. They have all been reduced to ashes when our house was bombed together with many others 10 yeras ago.

If anyone of you can find such a bound volume I would be extremely grateful.

Kind Regards,
Atpu.
