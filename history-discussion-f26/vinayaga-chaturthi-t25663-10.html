Dear Venkatesh Krishnan,

Uchipillayar @ Rockfort Temple in Trichy is very special..

The siva (cave) temple is created by Mahdnra. The temple was named as
Lalithangusa Pallaveswara Graham. We have discussed this extensively
in the group and also these informations reported widely in
http://www.varalaaru.com.recent issue as well.

Mahdnra ruled between 600 -630 AD.
Pillayarpatti is preceding this. Pls refer Gokul's mail.
Thiruchengottangudi - Pillayar is reportedly brought from Vaathapi -
after Paranjothi conquored Pulikesin . Pls refer Sivagaminyin Sabadam
by Amarar Kalki. Paranjoti was commaner-in-chief of Maamalla @
Narasimhavarman I - who was son of Mahendra. Therefore
Thiruchengotangudi is after Mahdnra - Uchipillayar. Gokul disputes
that the pillayar does not appear to be from Chalukya soil! I too agree.

Means, there is no other Pillayar found in Tamilnadu so far -
predating Pillayar patti ! Uchipillyar is second in the race - or
running parallely with Panayapuram. Pls read Mahendra caves bvy Dr.
Kalaikovan.

Mahdnra was till then Gunbaran, following Jain faith. Thiruvadhigai
(Veerattanam) near Panruti - constructed by Mahendra is named as
Gunabareswaram.!
