What a coincidence!!!

Tomorrow is the day 2-August in 2003. Me, Lavanya, Ram, Krupa and
Gokul met each other for the first time and went to Dr.Kalaikkovan's
home for a discussion. Appointment was between 2-4 pm. But since
krupa's train from chennai was late, we went to his home at 3:30 pm
only. Dr.Nalini and Dr.Akila were also there. He had to leave for
clinic at 5. But Q&A session was very interesting and he called
hospital to inform that he would be 1 hr late. Even after 6pm, the
same continued and again informed about another 1 hr late. After
that the call came from 'Seyal puyal' SPS and doctor asked his
assistant to announce holiday for the clinic. The discussions were
over by 10:30 only. My God!! 7 hours continously talking about
history. Never felt bored for even a minute. The same is continuing
till date.

For all these things, we are very much grateful to SPS only. Mudhal
yaathirai happened in Feb 2003. If he was not there, we would have
just stopped with 2 or 3 trips alone and started concentrating on
our personal works. SPS is the one who kept the fire alive among us.
If he hadn't taken us to Iravadham Mahadevan's book release
function, we wouldn't have planned to meet Dr.Kalaikkovan during 2nd
yaathirai and no trips, no history discussions, no varalaaru.com. I
mentioned this in my Sun TV interview also.

Enna dhavam seydhom!

Kamal
