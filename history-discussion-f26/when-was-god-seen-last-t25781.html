pamban swamigal got a darshan of muruga on a dancing peacock in Gneral
hospital itself.I forget the year but its considered a momentus
occasion in 20th century for muruga followers.

vainagaram nagappa chettiar of amaravadhi pudur( who is responsible
for 20th and 19th century completion of madurai meenakshi temple and
those glorious carved pillars opposite the main sanctums) saw a small
girl who gave him the temple prsasd of meenakshi temple, while
stranded on the road well outside madurai. he was thus inspired to do
this great work.


during serfoji period abirama battar saw abirami . the serfogi king on
a copper plate describes that he saw a full moon on a ammavasya day.
