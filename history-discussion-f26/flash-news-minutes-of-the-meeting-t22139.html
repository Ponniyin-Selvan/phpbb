Flash News!!! (Minutes of the Meeting)

by pavithranivas on Sun Jan 26, 2003 11:45 pm

Dear all...


The meeting of those who wanted to go on the PS trip took place at roughly
4.30 PM today (sunday, 26th Jan.) at Marina Beach.

What we didn't expect was the turn-out for the meeting. Dhanya's article
generated a lot of interest. Kamalakkannan, his wife Vijayapriya, Dhanya,
Lavanya, Anuradha I and my mom met right beside the Gandhi Silai landmark.
There was quite a large group of people waiting under a shaamiyaanaa. We
wondered who they were, until one of them came and informed us that they
were all waiting for the PS trip organizers!

There were two people from Indian Express, the editor of Kalki magazine, Ms.
Seethaa Ravi, the historical writer, "Vishwaksenan", and others were
present. Kamalakannan was asked to start the proceedings. Our route map was
read out by one of the people present, after which discussions started in
earnest. Everybody was enthusiastic. Suggestions about the places to visit,
accomodations and transport flew to and fro. (One enthusiast suggested
journeying to Viranam Eri and counting all the 72 mathagus). A lot of people
wanted to join in, so we can expect a surge in our numbers.

Ms. Seetha Ravi gave certains valuable suggestions about the trip . As per
what she said, "Vishwaksenan" will be accompanying those of us who're going
on the trip- so I guess the question of coverage is taken care of.

The next point was the coverage by Vikatan- who grouped us all in and took
some photos. Some of the cast of the "Magic Lantern" were there too- the
scriptwriters of 'Ponniyin Selvan' drama staged in YMCA two or three years
ago.

After that everyone began to drift off. We stayed talking for sometime after
that. I, Lavanya, Anuradha, Kamal and Vijaya, a lawyer called Dhakshaayani
and one Mr. Seethaaraama Sharma sat down to discuss the trip in earnest.
Here's the summary of what happened:

1. Kamal is to be entrusted with the organization part of it (booking rooms,
transport, etc.) with help from all of us. He and Lavanya will probably meet
an expert on the places we're going to visit - a Mr. Rafael. (Again
suggested by Kalki's editor)

2. The next meeting for the PS trip people is going to be at the Kalki
magazine's registered office, at Ekkaaduthaanggal, at 4 PM on sunday (Feb.
2nd). This was suggested by Ms. Seethaa Ravi. So everyone who wants to come,
please be there.


Krupa, Ravi et al...what happenend to you guys? We could have done with your
presence.

Kamal will be getting back to us with his own reports.(He did quite a great
job at the meeting:-)

So that's what happenend this evening.
Regards, Pavithra.







The next meeting place for the PS trip people was decided at next Sunday
(Feb 2nd) at 4 PM at the Kalki magazine's registered office.
