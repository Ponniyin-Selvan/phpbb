Hi;
We are discussing his
poems as are many others in India and abroad (the NRI's are one of our
greatest assets). The name Bharathi invokes respect (among quite a few
people), there are statues, places named after the great poet. School books
have ensured his immortality making sure almost every kid learns his poems
during the all important childhood even though they are not too inclined to
teach the meanings of those beautiful lines. Im sure i have left out a few
more things.

Aren't these accolades? What else do you think we ought to do for a poet?
There are instances where somebody has unintentionally been disrespectful of
this great poet but so are they of Gandhi. It is in our hands to ensure that

generations of patriots and lovers.

dreadfully bad husband. Do you think his family would have admired his
poetry when the man of the house was supposed to be earning? I know his
priorities lay with the Indian freedom movement but then why did he marry?!
Try to think in a filmi way...if his wife and kids had commited suicide and
Bharathi had been hung for that we wouldn't have been discussin him!! I
think it is his wife who has not been getting the accolades she deserves!!!
