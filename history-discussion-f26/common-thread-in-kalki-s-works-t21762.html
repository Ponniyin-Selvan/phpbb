Common Thread in Kalki's Works

by satish_arun on Thu Nov 01, 2007 3:01 pm

Venkatesh,

Good Topic to discuss. As Thiru has suggested, lets start a fresh mail
for each thread instead of replying to the old one or changing the
subject of the old one. Hence this new mail.

In almost all his stories, the hero will be hunted for by someone or
some group and the hero will be hiding or running to save himself.

PS - VT runs most of the time, either from Pazhuvetarayar or the
abathuthavigal.
Magudapathi - He is a freedom fighter and hence is in hideout
throughout the story. Also, the vilains chase him to kill him
Solaimalai ilavarasi - Same as above. Freedom Fighter and hence is in
hideout.
Kalvanin Kadhali - kekave venam. Being a thief the hero is in hideout.
Alai Osai - Again Freedom struggle, so hideout.

Another common thing is - all stories will have a water body being
descibed. Either a stream or river or kulam or odai.

There will be surely a scene related to food, eating.

Even in Kalvanin Kadhali, Kalyani's athai is (partially) deaf.

Regards,
Satish
