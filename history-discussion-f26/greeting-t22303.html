Hello all,

My name is Sibi and I am a new member of the group. I am doing my Phd at the
Univ of Texas at Austin. Nice to know about this group.

I started reading PS only last summer and was immediately immersed into it.
Because of research, I could only complete all the 5 volumes last december.
I was totally impressed by the writing of Kalki - his imagination, his
narration of feud between saivites and vaishnavites, romance in many places
and various other aspects. It's also impressive to note that the female
characters have been given a lot of importance showing the greatness of
ancient tamil culture. I'd think Nandini moves the entire plot
single-handedly, for example....

I was also interested in reading other works of Kalki. I'm currently reading
SS and going to read PK. I was also interested in South Indian history.
Googling for various aspects led me to this group.

I should say I'm glad to see such an active group interested in south indian
history and am glad to join you all.

I wish you all a very happy pongal.

Warm regards,
G.Sibi
