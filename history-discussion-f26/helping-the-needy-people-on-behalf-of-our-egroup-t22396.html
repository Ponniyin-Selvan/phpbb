by adhitha_karikalan on Thu Feb 13, 2003 8:07 pm

Nanbargale,

Vanakkam. Dhayavu seithu siramam paaraamal thamizhil
padikkavum.

Nam kuzhuvin moolam, thanjai thariniyil kashtapadum
vivasaya nanbargalin thuyarai oralavu thudaikka
murpadum oru chinna muyarchi.

Nammil palarukku ithai patri therindhu irukkum ena
ninaikkiren. Vikatan niruvanam thanneer illathadhal
vivasayam seyya mudiamal kashta padum vivasayigal
adhigam vaazhum gramangalai adutha bogam (aadi madham)
varai thathu eduthu avargalai kaapatrum or muyarchiyil
irangiyullathu. Amarar S.S.Vasan pirantha oorana
'Narimanan' gramathai mudhal ooraga thatheduthullathu.
Vaasagarkal alikkum nankodaiyin moolam, melum pala
gramangalai thathedukkavum mudiveduthu ullathu.
Vaasagarkalin udhaviyal, melum 3 gramangalai yerkanave
thatheduthu vittadhu. Vibarangalukku
http://66.92.103.10/vikatan/donations/donate.asp -i
paarkkavum.

Enave, naamum nam eGroup moolam nammal mudintha alavu
(udhaaranamaaga, oru naal sambalam allathu avaravar
viruppa padi) nidhiyai thiratti oru gramathai
thatheduthaal enna?

Idhai patri ungal karuthukkalai anuppavum. Piragu, oru
poruthamaana nalla vazhiyai therndheduthu, idhai vetri
karamaaga seyal paduthuvom.

Nandri
Kamal
