More than Deserving ... CR Baskar from Makkal TV is also getting
falicitated..
Dr. Thyaga Sathiyamurthy's contribution in Indus valley contributions
through Adhichanallur finds are remarkable.. he has accomplished a lot
during his tenure as Suptt Archeologist in ASI, Chennai till last year.
Some of the few I know of :

1. Much preserved .. kept out of sight .. famous cherishable Pride our
Ancient Glory Thanjavur Big temple paintings have been made Public ..
even for commoner to see, enjoy and feel pride of our Heritage .. (There
used to be general criticism that Carnatic Music - Rich paintings or
such Arts always belonged to Jarigai Angavasthram / Pattuppudavai /
Viarmookkuthi - the upper class aristocrats only). Dr. Thyaga
Sathyamurthy by making this Public has paved way for every one take
interst in such Arts ..

2. Seven Pagodas : Much talked about subject Mamallapuram - whether
there 7 temples resembling Tibetian type Pagodas .. In post Tsunami
period, ASI has conducted extensive work and identified most of them
(all? - Sriram may help)

3. Aadhichanallur : In about 1920, the British conducted some excavation
and brought out several details to light.. There appears to have been
further works but not much remarkable. Under the guidance of Dr. TSM
this remarkable work was conducted and the finds make everyone of us
joyous... (i) Whether Asoka's Brahmi scripts were formed from Tamil
Brahmi (2) Whether Adichanallur is comparable with Harappa /
Mohanjadero (3) Whether Tamilians were more informed & cultured then
rest of their counterparts in the sub-continent since 3500 BC. Former
CM and Present CM Dr. Kalignar - immediately on taking over - have shown
keen interest in this subject..

4. Sangam period Muruga Temple :: A unique temple in Mamallapuram shore
- with two tier brick Foundation of vastly varying period is being
identified as Sangam period Muruga Temple ! (Before 300 AD atleast ! )
Scientific methods (C14) are being worked to arrive at precise dates..

We are very much honoured to have Dr. Thyaga Sathiamurthy among us here
in PS Group. With his untiring efforts, REACH foundation is taking
shape - contentrating on Preserving and Protecting Cultural Properties
in Tamilnadu.

Vaazhtha vayadillai, Vanagugirom, Sir.

Thank you Dear Madan, for bringing this to our attention.. especially
with your Wedding Reception on the Cards ..

We will definitely take part in the Function ..

best regards / sps
