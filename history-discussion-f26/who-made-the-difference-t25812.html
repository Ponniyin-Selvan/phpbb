who made the difference?

by abhivencat on Tue Aug 09, 2005 7:15 pm

Dear all,

when I read swethas mail i was reminded of how much importance
kalki gave to the minor charecters.
lets take mr and mrs arul mozhi, mr and mrs senthan amuthan, and mr
and mrs vanthiyathevan as the main charecters.
considering all others as the side charecters can we analyse who made
most of the difference in the story
the charecters
kanda maran, mandakini, the pazuvettaraiyars, karuthimaan etc

the question who made the most contribution to the story poniyin
selvan and the history behind.
( we are talking only about the novel)
regards
venketesh
