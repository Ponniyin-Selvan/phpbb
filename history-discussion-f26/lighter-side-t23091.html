LIGHTER SIDE

by sps10142004 on Fri Aug 26, 2005 2:22 pm

Dear Friends,

Very glad to see the hectic activity and steady flow of mails. This
is happening after younger generation takes more and more interest.

The content of the mails are most of the times TOPIC RELATED, but
they are on the lighter side too.

Apart from the enthus, we have dyons like Dr.Sridhar- active
professional Doctor - Dr. Kalaikkovan - observes - Dr. Jaybee - whom
we all respect as Don Bradman - another US based Lady Lion - who
organised Vikraman meet, PS Drama Video show within a short one week
trip to India.

Such REVERED members do see the mails topic wise.

This is one of the reasons, I feel, why Dr. Sridhar put a mail to
change the TOPIC / SUBJECT when sending mails.

By doing this, we can SEGREGATE TOPIC (SUBJECT) WISE - INCLUDING
LIGHTER SIDE - like Alwarkadian getting married or Maran in
Boothatheevu etc/.

Also, there is a chance that INTERESTING / DENSE SUBJECTS getting
missed among the LIGHTER SIDE ones ..

A SIMPLE REQUEST TO CHANGE SUBJECT :: LIGHTER SIDE if the mail is NOT
related to VEERANAM or SRICHAKRAM!

My suggestion for such SUBJECT IS :: LIGHTER SIDE.

Mikka anbudan / sps
