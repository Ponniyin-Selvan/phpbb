Minutes of Natesan's Park

by pavithranivas on Tue Apr 15, 2003 11:04 am

Dear all...

The meeting for the PS egroup was scheduled at 4.30 PM a Natesan's Park last
evening- and we met accordingly for it. Kamal and Krupashankar arrived
first, while I And Sumeetha (Who does the translation of PS) arrived next.
After that came Lavanya.


Talk came to the Symposium that's going to be held this month- which has
been potponed from 20th April to 27th April. So now the Symposium will be
held at Hotel Karpagam, Mylapore, on 27th April, at 4 PM. As of now, Raj TV
and Ananda Vikatan will be covering the Symposium- Mr. Sundar Bharadwaj has
numerous contacts.

MakkalE...I 'm sure we all have dozens of questions to ask the experts
who'll be present there- and who will be expecting a good Q & A session from
us. So here's something for all of us to participate in:

Kindly write up your questions- anything concerning PS's history, the kings
of TN. etc...do write to the group about it, and we'll make a list of it-
and ask the experts about them. We need as many as you can supply us with-
the experts will expect a good deal:-)

The next thing that came under discussion was the fact that we didn't have a
working group of members locally- people who could pitch in with the group
work in Chennai and thereabouts. This would help when events and trips are
organized with Chennai as the base. Members of this 'physically present'
group will be a 'kuzhu' in their own right. Naturally- the numbers will
change whenever an overseas member comes home:-)- so- those who are
localites, or even in any place within Tamil Nadu- kindly contact Kamal and
sign up with him.

Five of us is much too small a number (those of us who were present
yesterday), you see. We need more.

After that, we spent some time socializing. Sumeetha was new to all of us-
so we got better acqauinted with her. She works in http://www.sulekha.com- and she
promised us a good deal of help from that quarter, should we need her help.

After that, talk turned to general topics. Apparently Dr. Kalaikovan was
arriving in Chennai today (April 15th) for a book release- and Kamal wanted
to know who would like to meet him. Dr. Kalaikovan had also informed him
that he'd like volunteers for his expeditions. It looks like our group will
have plenty to do in the following months.


By this time, evening had turned into night, and the watchman actually
started hounding us out of our benches- so we had to leave- reluctantly.

And that brings to an end the PS egroup meeting.


Here's to hoping that everything turns out well.

Cheers,
Pavithra.

I have this question that's been burning me up

Did the incident behind the title ponniyin selvan really happen or was
it Kalki's creativity?

(I mean where Arulmozhi almost drowns and is rescued when he's little).
If I had to guess, I'd guess Kalki's imagination. If this has been
addressed in a previous facts/fictions type email, my apologies and
point me to it.



Krupa, the whole Aryan invasion theory, I'd assumed was fact all this
time. I don't give too much credit to the "sivappa irunda Aryan,
karuppa irunda Dravidian" theory. I think proximity to the sun (of
where you live) does play a part in one's pigmentation/lack of it,
illaya? As to "Aryans-naa yaar, Dravidians-naa yaaru" I have always
believed in this rather simplistic answer: Dravidians were the group of
people living in the Indus valley region, and Aryans came over from the
west, northwest, to conquer them.

Ippo nadakkira pore-ellam partha, I wonder what's the point to
everything? Why can't people get along with other people - let everyone
practice their own religion and live in peace. Why force anything on
anyone? Irukka porathu konja naal, athai happiya, ippadi ponniyin selvan
mathiri books padichittu irukkaama, ethukku sandai ellam?

I think I'll shut up now and stop rambling.
