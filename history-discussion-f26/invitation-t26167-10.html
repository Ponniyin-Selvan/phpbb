Hi madan

a year back sathish, venkateshwaran kasirajan, nadarajan srinivasan,
sivaram and myself - we went on a one day tour.
chola, pallava, sangam age, arab, all in one day.
from chennai to marakkanam where sathish this time discovered a RRC
inscription( last time in kachoor murugnandam and i beat him to it-
the trick is to look for 2 consequtive 'ja' s)
the first on the list was the tiffen at mayajal. fully fortified we
went on to thiruvidanthai.( one of the 108 thirupathis) we advertised
that as a chola temple of RRC age with no later modifications.
sivaram was surprised at the crowd inside and wondered if so much
interest was being developed for chola architecture
it was then that we told sivaram( the only bachelor in the crowd)
" unakku theriyaathaa? inga vanthaa kalyaanam fix aagum"
poor sivaram did try to flee the temple but was ably restrained. we
saw varaha moorthy as nithya kalyana perumal and within the calender
year sivaram was engaged.

of course madan there are others able enough to fix marriages for
themselves without the gentle push from nithya kalyana perumaal but
that is another story isnt it?

venketesh
