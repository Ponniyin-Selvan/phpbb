Greatest ruler of Tamilnadu

by rojatraders on Thu Mar 03, 2005 6:11 am

RR is a good choice.My choice is as follows

Military achievements:
1.Kambana
2.Rajendra cholan.
3.Narashima varma pallava
4.Rajaraja cholan.

Welfare of people
1.Kamaraj
2.chola kings who built canals and dams(rajathithya,karikalan etc)
3.nayakkar dynasty of madurai

patronizing literature:
1.nandhivarma pallava
2.vallal kings(athiyaman,paari)

Philosopher kings:
1.Mahendravarma pallava

Devotee kings:
1.ko-chenganan
2.Thirumangai mannan
3.Kulasekaran

Justful rulers:
1.Sibi
2.Manu neethi
3.Pandya nedunchezhian

Greatest tragedies:
1.Rajathithya
2.Aditha karikalan
3.maruthanayagam

charismatic rulers:
1.Rajendra
2.RajaRaja(based on PS--else MGR)

Greatest chief ministers
1.chanakya
2.MGR(both were invincible till their death)

Greatest dreamers:
1.Vijayalaya and aditha chola.
2.RR and Rajendra duo.

Greatest dynasty:
1.cholas
2.pallavas
3.pandyas

If I have to pick one among these I will pick kambana.Next will be
kamaraj.

sampath

by rojatraders on Sun Mar 06, 2005 11:15 pm

Dear Dr.Rathnam,

RR and Rajendra are landmarks in history of TN.They are our reply to
alexander and napolean.They stand there with the very best in Indian
history along with asoka,krishnadevaraya,kanishka,harsha and
pulikesin.

And kambana started his career as the son of a provincial ruler.when
entire south india was under slavery sangama dynasty which was just
a provincial rulers dared to dream of liberating it.Sangama dynasty
achieved it.Kambana established vijayanagar empire along with his
father and turned his eyes on TN.Finally his empire stretched from
kanyakumari to krishna-the same as RR and Rajendra(they did not
annex kadaram and ganges valley,just invaded)The lands above krishna
river to madyapradesh were ruled by his father bukka,So the sangama
dynasty virtually ruled entire south India.Kambana would have
inherited this had he lived,but he died at a young age.
I am not grading emperors based on the land which they ruled.If
thats the case chengizkhan is the greatest in the world.His empire
stretched from atlantic occean to pacific occean.RR's greatness is
his dream of a chola empire and rajendras greatness is furthering
his dream.They fulfilled the dream of vijayalaya and aditha.

Kambanas greatness is saving the culture.what no tamil king could
do,he did .He came to save the country when it was most needed.He
was the last great emperor in TN.After him there was none.

sampath
