PS As A COMIC OR CARTOON

by seshadrigokul on Sat Apr 12, 2003 11:47 pm

Sridhar - I've been thinking about this for quite some time.
With the help of latest graphics tools available - it is quite
possible to bring out PS as a comic. Atleast you need not draw the
pictures for each and every frame - all replication work can be done
easily and corrections are also simple. I've tried this with
something else (a project of mine called Tales from Indian
Mythology) - it did work. Though I did'nt bring it to the print
medium, I'm sure this can be done.
Inspite of this advantage, PS will still be a big challenge -
considering the background artwork involved. The above technique
helps characters - but not much on backgrounds. Tools like Bryce may
be useful.
One more thing. I feel this is not just for children - but for adults
too. You'll be surprised how popular "Lion Comics" and "Muthu Comics"
are - among the adults in TNadu ! ( I know this because I'm also a
comics fan!!)
Cheers
Gokul.S
