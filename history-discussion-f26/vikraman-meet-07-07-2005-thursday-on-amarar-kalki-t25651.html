by sps10142004 on Tue Jan 29, 2008 7:38 pm

REPOSTED FOR NEW MEMBERS ::

--- Inponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "Sivapathasekaran"
wrote 8.7.2005:

Dear members,

Senior Member, Retd Govt official Seethalakshmi, having so much
interest in group activities and good rapport with popular Tamil
Writers has come from the USA and organised a meet with Writer
Vikraman at the Ashoknagar residence of Retd Director / A I R Mrs.
Punithavathy Elangoven, whose Father-in-law was Dr. Rasamanickanar,
the Great Historian!

About ten of our members were present. The meeting took off exactly
at 1900 hrs. Introduction by Madam was too good. To the point.
"Dear Vikraman, please talk about Kalki since you have seen and
moved with him."

And Mr. Vikraman, a Senior Citizen and President of Tamil Writers'
Association, stuck to the guidelines and speak only about
Kalki - NOT about HIMSELF or about HIS Works!

What a Great Surprise! Mr. Vikraman himself was a Great Fan of
Kalki and started Kalki Readers Forum as early in 1943! Forerunner
PS e-Gp!

Kalki lived in Lake View Road, West Mambalam, near Kasi Viswanathar
temple and under the leadership of Vikraman's Ezhuthalar peravai,
it was resolved to RENAME THE ROAD AS 'AMARAR KALKI SALAI' and sent
to the Government a few years back!

The back ground for writing Parthiban Kanavu.

A forerunner before opening the magnum opus PS!

Kalki stayed with Rasikamani TKC in Maamallapuram for about 5 - 6
days and was thrilled with the unfinished marvels etc. He created
imaginary characters as Main characters and through them brought
out the back ground of real historical characters - his method of
story telling! Chettinad Raja, Annamalai (who later became Sinna
Annamalai of Kumudam) were some of his Admirers!

He avoided majority of his admirers because he was over working
and attending music critisim, political ariticles apart from
various literary works in writing. He hardly appeared in the
stage.

After leaving Anandavikatan in a rift with Dyon SS Vasan in film
making of Parthiban Kanavu, he was financially supported by
Renowned Musician MSSubbulakshmi. He joined Rajaji and started an
Ashram in Tiruchengodu and brought out a magazine. Vikraman has
written extensively on these things in Amudhasurabi.

His name Kalki is NOT named after his mother Kalyani as popularly
believed, but named after Thiru Vi Kalyana Sundaranar.

He avoided Astrologers, Religeous Heads and was always thinking of
the welfare of the society. He cared for Southern Politicians like
Sathiamurthy getting respect in the Northern India.

He travelled extensively because of his involvement in Freedom
movement. He flew to Ceylon with SS Vasan & Maali in a three
Seater Plane of Vikatan and made observations before writing on
Ceylon in the PS.

When PS was published by Kalki Weekly in during 50s, every Friday
evening, after 730 pm, there used to be long queue in front of
Higginbothoms Egmore and readers were given Kalki only if they
agreed to purchase Vikatan! Still there was long queue!

According to Mr. Vikraman, NO OTHER WRITER would have/had EVER
ENJOYED this kind of HERO STATUS in any Indian Language and
certainly NOT in Tamil Language before or after Kalki!

Those who attended the meet are suprised by the UNRESERVED
APPRECIATION OF KALKI'S WORKS by a fellow Writer like Vikraman -
who takes pride in calling himself an ardent fan of Kalki and has
undertaken to write sequels only to quench the curiosity of readers!

We can organise similar meet again in common place on a Week end
when members desire!

We do not know how to reciprocate the love and affection showered
by Member Seethalakshmi, Mrs. Punithavathi Elangovan, her son
Kumaravelu (who enacted PS drama in the stages about 6 yrs back!)
his wife who served food to us and sister and all the other members
of the family!

The entire Family is dedicated to History in General and Ponniyin
Selvan in Particular!

Re PONNIYINSELVAN DRAMA visual we have seen after the Dinner, I
leave it to the best hands of those present other than me to
present and bring out the impact it has created in us!

More than Superb!

Another mile stone .. may be another beginning .. let us wait and
hear all the views!

Anbudan / sps
