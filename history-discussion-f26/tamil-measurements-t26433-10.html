Dear thiru,

as per Wikipedia,

1 paagam = 4 Muzham = 8 jaann

1 jaann = 23.333334 cm

1 Kaadham = 6000 paagam = 6000 x 8 x 23.333334= 11.20 Kms

8 Km = 5 miles.

11.20 / 8 x 5 = 7 miles.

1 Kadham is 7 miles as per this calculation.

Even then 1.16 something is erroneous.

Muzham is not from palm but from middle finger tip to elbow! If you
measure in your hand you will measure two jaan from your elbow to middle
finger tip.
