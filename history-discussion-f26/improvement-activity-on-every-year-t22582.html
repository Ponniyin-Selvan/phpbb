Improvement/activity on every year

by vijay_v5 on Mon Mar 24, 2008 5:42 pm

Every sadhaya vizha we need a new activity in the name
of Emperor Rajaraja Cholan like these.

1. Running a monthly day tour of Chola Day Out
covering Uraiyur, Kallanai, Thanjavur, Kumabakonam,
Tiruvarur, Nagapattinam, Poompuhar, Gangaigonda
Cholapuram and return to Trichy.

Commercial activity wouldn't harm the integrity of our
group as long as trust maintained in our PSVP trust.

After successful year it may be promoted weekly. This
will bring us more activities. This might be promoted
to the schools and other education institutes. Very
large potential there.

2. Media entry. Covering our activities in youtube.com
and possibly TV.

3. Temple maintenance on task basis. eg. A temple
cleaning and day pooja/prasadam could be a task
undertaken by a remote thondar/bakthar.

These are all could lead us actively participating
rather seldom emailing in our group.

Further suggestions welcome.

Thanks
