Kambar and kalki-comparision.

by rojatraders on Thu Nov 25, 2004 3:16 am

Hi members.I am sampath,a new member to this group.First thing I did
after joining was to read all the previous mails and I am fascinated
by the amount of knowledge this group members possess.

In my first post I would give my views on what made PS great.I have
read many epics in English and Tamil and in my opinion Ponniyin
selvan can sit at par with any epic in any language in the world.The
closest competition to Ponniyin selvan will come from another tamil
epic kambaramayanam.

I have read most of shakespheres works and have always wondered what
made them great.Most of them were ordinary movie stories,for example
Romeo and juliet was nothing but a love story.Plain ordinary love
story.What made it great?

Its only the way in which the story was presented made it great.The
words and the play writing skills of shakesphere made it an epic.If
I can use a parallel from cinema,its not the story that makes a
movie succesful.Its the screenplay that makes the movie succesful.We
have seen great writers transforming ordinary stories into great
epics by just presenting it in a beautiful way.

Similiarly Kalki has made this ordinary fight for asendency to
throne between 3 heirs a mega epic by presenting it in a beautiful
way.But the only pity is that it can be understood only by people
who know Tamil.Even if we translate it into English the charm goes
away.If we picturize it also the charm goes away.

Another aspect I love in Ponniyin Selvan is characterization.The way
Kalki has created each characters has been magnificient.But in this
aspect I would place him a BIT lower than Kambar(Sorry hardcore
Kalki fans,but I'm sure Kalki will agree with me.If we say he is
next to kambar he will feel proud)

The reason I say is as follows.I would like to compare both on
characterization.Kalki's creation of characters like Vandiya thevan,
Ponniyin selvan,manimekalai was great.Even though he worshipped
arulmozhi in the novel it did not look artificial.Tamils have
worshipped MGR and know what hero worship is.Kalki also made the
Hero worship worthwhile.He made us believe that Arulmozhi deserved
the hero worship he had.

Kambar also had limits and he also had to hero worship.
But within this limits what a beautiful job he performed?What a
characterization he created for everyone?For example when Rama shot
vali hiding behind a tree valmigi writes as if vali abused Rama and
in that scence made rama appear as a villian and vali as a hero.But
kambar changes that.

Vali calls rama as "Why did you shoot me from behind?Kathir mathiyam
pol mugathoye.."--Is this abuse?Even when Vali is down in earth he
couldnt stop admiring rama,who killed him.So kambar hails rama in
valis words.Will anybody hail his killer as "kathir mathiyam pol
mugathoye.."(Im not sure about exact wordings here,im sorry)

In every abuse of vali Kambar adds a praise for Rama.There he
glorofies Rama better than even valmigi.

And Valmigi wrote as ravana lifted seetha by his hands.But Kambar
couldnt tolerate the thought that anyone can even touch seeta.So he
wrote as "Ravana lifted the hut along with the land and carried
seetha without touching her".He upholds tamil culture there.

The way he has characterized Bharatha makes us wonder whether Rama
is the hero of the epic or bharatha is.See the way in which he
builds the Bharatha character.

When Dasaratha tells Rama that Bharatha will be the king,Rama goes
and tells his mother kousalya that bharatha will be the new king.See
what words Kambar puts in kousalyas mouth.

"Bharatha is three times better than you to rule this kingdom".

See this.Even after hearing that her only son is being robbed of his
throne the words that come from kousalyas mouth are hailing
bharatha.See further how Kambar builds bharathas character.

When Bharatha goes to forest to meet ram he meets Guha.After hearing
why Bharatha has come to the forest Guha is fascinated.He hugs
Bharatha,cries and says "Even thousand Rama's are not equal to you".

See this.Now Bharatha is hailed as 1000 times better than Rama,the
hero of the epic.But the climax is the pinching one.

When Rama doesnt return after 14 years Bharatha is about to jump
into fire.Then kousalya prevents him and says "Dont do it my son.I
can live without Rama but not without you.PALAKODI ramnum unakku
iniyavano?"

Here Bharatha is portrayed as better than "Palakodi Raman",the hero
of the epic.This is how Kambar built each character with the limited
freedom he had.
