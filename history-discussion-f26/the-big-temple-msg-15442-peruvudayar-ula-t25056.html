The Ganapathy in the big temple was known as ARULMOZHI VINAYAGAR as
late till 1820 ! - atleast .. before any inscription was deciphered
in the 1890 ! and it was not known then that the main lingam was
named as Rajarajeeswaramudayar nor was it built by Arumozhi @ RRC !
====================

In continuation of msg 15442::
---------------------------------

"Aruludhavunkeerthi ARUMOZHI VEZHATHIN
Irumalarp potralirainjik kurumadhiyar
Senjadayar engal Sivanar PERUVUDAYAR
Thanjaiyula Selvendraga "

" LET ME COMMENCE WRITING ON THE ULA OF PERUVUDAYAR AFTER
WORSHIPPING THE ARUMOZHI VINAYAGAR " - commences Sri. Sivakkozhundu
Desikar of Kottaiyur (village next to Thiruvalanchuzhi /
Pazhayarai). His books on medicine are written during Sarfoji period
in 1826.

The Author did not have access to the INSCRIPTIONAL DECODING
tookplace during the British in the Last Decade of 19th Century.

He is mentioning that Karikala chozhan brought the stone from
Narmada and established Peruvudayar ! Means Rajarajan was known to
Such a learned Scholar, who has vividly quoted BRAHADEESWARA
MAHATHMIYAM !

When we go through the Archieves, we can find mails where Dr.
Sridhar has reacted to a Web-site quoting Karikalan built the Big
temple ! Our own Scholar/s believed so till the 19th Century !
Surprising !

Dr. U.Ve. Saaminatha Iyer has provided notes to this book later and
Thirupananthaal Kaasimadam has published this book in 1932.

Some Interesting Verses :

Verse 13 : Yervalarun Thanjai RAAJARAJESWARATHIL
Pervalara mannum Peruvudayan .. !

Means The name RAJARAJESWARAM was known before the inscriptions
could tell us !

Heading :: T H I R U M A N J A N A M ::

Verse 22 : Thann padintha sandhanamumcharntha SIVAGANGAI
Vannpunalum kondu magizhnthatti .. "

SIVAGANGAI was known in mid 19th Century !

Heading :: P O O J A I ::

Verse 23 : Aadhi Saivar Needu MAGUDAGAMATHILaaranathil
Othisaivirchey POOSAIYUTKONDU .. "

In the Foot notes : U. Ve. Sa adds : 23 : MAGUDAGAM is among the 28
Agamas. "Ihdhu Ithalthirkuriyathu". ( This is practised in this
Temple).

Heading : UDAN VARUVORGAL (THOSE WHO ACCOMPANY in PROCESSION)

In verses 38, .. 49, the list of Idols which preceded on procession
is given which include :: Appar - Gnanasambandar - Sundarar -
Maanickavasagar - VARAGUNA PANDIYAN - SERAMANPERUMAL NAYANAR -
Aalayameesarkizhaitha Athanum (tho" denoting Kulothunga - Rajrajan
Idol would have been more appropriate).

MEANS, Apart from NAALVAR, SERAMAN PERUMAL (CHERA) - VARAGUNA
(PANDYA) - and CHOZHA SIVA DEVOTEE IDOLS were also taken on
procession ATLEAST Till late 19th Century !
