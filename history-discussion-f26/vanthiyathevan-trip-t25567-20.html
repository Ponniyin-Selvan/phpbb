Hi Sathish
think this following itinery though jampacked would work out

friday night- take a rathi meena bus to kumbakonam. we can stay
there. (swetha and sps have immense clout in that town. so they can
arrange things over the phone too.)

saturday morning- travel north. see kadambur, veeranam eri and the
temple beside thath and travel back to kumbakonam for lunch. start
for tanjore see the big temple and if possible what temple kalki
meant as singala nachiar koil back to kumbakonam and sleep. those not
tired can see the artha jamam at kumbeshwarar. we could even have a
group discussion on the steps of the maha maham tank.

sunday morning. move thro peralam- thiruvarur- nagai- kodikarai and
back by the night.
we can leave pazhuvur and mazhapadi which are not directly visited by
the charecters.

the advantages for the trip are:
only 2 days.
can be fitted any weekend suitable to all of us.
costs will be slightly more than half of a 4 day trip

think about it.

=======================================================
Day 1 : Kumbakonam - Veeranam - Kumbakonam - Thanjavur Big temple -
Singala Naachiar.


saturday morning- travel north. see kadambur, veeranam eri and the
temple beside thath and travel back to kumbakonam for lunch. start
for tanjore see the big temple and if possible what temple kalki
meant as singala nachiar koil back to kumbakonam and sleep. those not
tired can see the artha jamam at kumbeshwarar. we could even have a
group discussion on the steps of the maha maham tank.

======================


Day 1 :

Assuming we reach Kumbakonam by 6 am and ready to leave our place by
8 am (in just 2 hrs) in a Tempo traveller / Qualis (depending on
no.of persons) and keep packed break-fast, we shall reach
Veeranarayana perumal temple 10 am / 1030 am and Udayarkudi @
Kaatumannargudi (Ravidasan / Parameswaran / Soman Sambavan name in
inscriptions avl here - related to Aditha karikalan murder) 1100/1130.
Mealakkadambur - Amirthagadeswarar temple 1200 hrs. (within 2 hrs
getting down / board at 3 places and go around 3 temples).
Lunch there itself. Gangai konda chozpuram 2 / 3 pm. Pahuvur (not
getting down) - Thirumalpadi (not deviating or getting down) -
Thiruvaiyaru (not getting down) - Thanjvur 5 pm. Not going to
Saandaram / upstairs, since arrangements made in South mandap itself.
Any how we need minumim 3 hrs in Thanjavur. 8 pm. dinner.
Arthajamam not possible KUMbeswarar. REST at THANJAVUR.

Day 2 :

sunday morning. move thro peralam- thiruvarur- nagai- kodikarai and
back by the night.

THANJAI - TIRUVARUR - (* Naagai - difficult ) VEDARANYAM / KODIKKARAI.

We can slightly rework here. Thanjavur - Thiruvarur - Vedaranyam -
Kodikkarai - Naagai* - Karaikkal - Thirukkadayur - Seerkazhi -
Chidambaram (Night Dinner). (we may miss NAAGAI )

LEAVE CHIDAMBARAM FOR CHENNAI : BY RATHI MEENA.
====================================


That gives us ALTERNATIVE PLAN :

We can Take a Vehicle at Chidambaram (for 2 days) after reaching by
Rathimeena. Shower / breakfast Chidambaram arrangements possible.

Chidambaram - Veeranam - Kadambur - GKC - Pazhaiyari @ Kumbakonam -
Thanjai (Big temple) Halt.

Thanjai - Thiruvarur - Kodikkarai - Naagai - Karaikkal - Thirukkadayar
- Seerkazhi - Chidambaram.
======================================

This appears to be workable.

Regards / sps

Day 1 : Kumbakonam - Veeranam - Kumbakonam - Thanjavur Big temple -
Singala Naachiar.

saturday morning- travel north. see kadambur, veeranam eri and the
temple beside thath and travel back to kumbakonam for lunch. start
for tanjore see the big temple and if possible what temple kalki
meant as singala nachiar koil back to kumbakonam and sleep. those not
tired can see the artha jamam at kumbeshwarar. we could even have a
group discussion on the steps of the maha maham tank.

======================

Day 1 :

Assuming we reach Kumbakonam by 6 am and ready to leave our place by
8 am (in just 2 hrs) in a Tempo traveller / Qualis (depending on
no.of persons) and keep packed break-fast, we shall reach
Veeranarayana perumal temple 10 am / 1030 am and Udayarkudi @
Kaatumannargudi (Ravidasan / Parameswaran / Soman Sambavan name in
inscriptions avl here - related to Aditha karikalan murder) 1100/1130.
Mealakkadambur - Amirthagadeswarar temple 1200 hrs. (within 2 hrs
getting down / board at 3 places and go around 3 temples).
Lunch there itself. Gangai konda chozpuram 2 / 3 pm. Pahuvur (not
getting down) - Thirumalpadi (not deviating or getting down) -
Thiruvaiyaru (not getting down) - Thanjvur 5 pm. Not going to
Saandaram / upstairs, since arrangements made in South mandap itself.
Any how we need minumim 3 hrs in Thanjavur. 8 pm. dinner.
Arthajamam not possible KUMbeswarar. REST at THANJAVUR.

Day 2 :

sunday morning. move thro peralam- thiruvarur- nagai- kodikarai and
back by the night.

THANJAI - TIRUVARUR - (* Naagai - difficult ) VEDARANYAM / KODIKKARAI.

We can slightly rework here. Thanjavur - Thiruvarur - Vedaranyam -
Kodikkarai - Naagai* - Karaikkal - Thirukkadayur - Seerkazhi -
Chidambaram (Night Dinner). (we may miss NAAGAI )

LEAVE CHIDAMBARAM FOR CHENNAI : BY RATHI MEENA.
====================================

That gives us ALTERNATIVE PLAN :

We can Take a Vehicle at Chidambaram (for 2 days) after reaching by
Rathimeena. Shower / breakfast Chidambaram arrangements possible.

Chidambaram - Veeranam - Kadambur - GKC - Pazhaiyari @ Kumbakonam -
Thanjai (Big temple) Halt.

Thanjai - Thiruvarur - Kodikkarai - Naagai - Karaikkal - Thirukkadayar
- Seerkazhi - Chidambaram.
======================================

This appears to be workable.

Regards / sps

Where u in the stadium during GRV's 96 against West Indies?
Hi rahul
I was.and it was the second match i was seeing. earlier one had been
a ranji final, bombay vs tamilnadu.
i nearly cried when everbody else got out and grv stood like a rock.
first innings both teams scored 200 and 210 i think.
venkat raghavan was dropped and was 12th man.
andy roberts was then the worlds fastest.
when india won somebody in B stand lit a fire and i still remember
the fire truck racing on the green field.

i think we lost the series 3-2 though we won the madras match.
i used to be a great lover of cricket but I quit playing when my
class mate in school started playing for india( when he was in school)
