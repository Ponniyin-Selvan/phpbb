Thisthought came to arulmozhi's mind because of kundavai,Periya velar etc. She makes the sabatham so thatarulmozhi can understand her true love for him. stubborn also.She pays a
deaf ear tokundavai when she wants to go to nagai for arulmozhi.She asks for VD's help but when he is not willing shedecides to go all alone. These incidents are examplesof how undeterred her mind is. Actually she provesher true love for arulmozhi over poonguzhali oranyone. Manimekalai fails to do this to VD.Even when arulmozhi teases her so many times she justremains quiet. She never gets angry , she neverargues, which means that she is sure of herself. Sheis undisturbed. She accomplishes her goals in a veryquiet and calm manner. One more point to be noted is that after knowing hermind fully, arulmozhi immediately agrees to marry her.Kundavai doesn't come in the picture at all. Vanathytells him that he should marry her before he goes tonagaifor political reasons for which he says okay.And finally he does that also. That's why they didn'twait for VD and others to come for
their
wedding.So arulmozhi and vanathy marry, influenced by theirownminds. Kundavai had nothing to do here.Arulmozhi's teasing of vanathy is because of his lovefor her. He never does this to any other person.We all know that when a person has no problems in hispersonal front, he can concentrate on his carrer fullyand reach greater heights. This is one reason whyarulmozhi chose vanathy to be his bride. He tells herthat he has so many ambitions and he feels afraid thatshe may become hinderous in achieving his goals. Vanathy clears that saying that she will never be astumbling block for him. And arulmozhi understandsthat vanathy knows what she wants and what arulmozhiwants. And so he decides to marry her. In case of poonkuzhali, her mind wavers. She is notsure of what she wants actually. She keeps changingher thoughts every now and then. Nandhini also doesthis but is totally
unpredictable.
Poonguzhali acceptsamudhan only when she comes to know fully that shecannot get arulmozhi. I like poonguzhali' s characteralso. There is a saying " nam kai 5 viralkalum onnairukaadhu".
