accepting it, you are cheating yourself. Only for money, only for
comfort, todays man is becoming an animal. Without all these gizmos,

no need for money, peace is sure to stay.

I think I am deviating to philosophy from religion.

the house, dont say that the House itself is wrong. The house will
surely contain flammable and non flammable items. Its our
responsibility to keep the fire away from the flammable items and
thats why I am requesting you. Dont go behind the misinterpreations
of fire. And I said only 30% of the news items are correct and if you
