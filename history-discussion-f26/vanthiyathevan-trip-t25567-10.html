vanthiyathevan trip

by abhivencat on Wed Apr 04, 2007 12:48 am

Hi

swetha seems to be getting more and more eager for the trip.

here's her itineray

Day 1 ( 28-04-07)
======
Leave Chennai at 7 am.
Breakfast at Arusuvai Arasu - ECR.
Reach Chidambaram Temple at 11.30 am.
Chidambaram Temple Darisanam.
Lunch at Saradharam.
Proceed to Veeranam Eri, Kattumannargudi and Kadambur.
Proceed to Thanjavur - overnight halt.


Day 2 ( 29- 4-07)
======
7 am Breakfast and proceed to Periya Koyil.
Lunch at Thanjavur.
Start at 3 pm and proceed to Thirumazhapadi temple.
Next to Thiruvaiyaru temple.
Back to Thanjavur - overnight halt.


Day 3 (30-4-07 monday)
======
7 am Breakfast and proceed to Thiruvarur temple.
Next to Nagappattinam temple.
Lunch at Nagappattinam.
Proceed to Kodikkarai.
Next to Vedaranyam temple.
Proceed to Karaikkal - overnight halt.


Day 4 ( 1-5-07)
======
7 am Breakfast and proceed to Tharangampadi.
Next to Thirukkadaiyur temple.
Next to Thiruvenkadu temple.
Lunch at Sirkazhi/Chidambaram.
Proceed to Chennai via ECR.


we are seeing some major chola temples which vanthiya thevan missed.

also a danish fort in tharangambadi.

venketesh
