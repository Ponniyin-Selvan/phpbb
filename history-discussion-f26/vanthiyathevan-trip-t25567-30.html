the GRV innings is always mentioned. They even remember the headlines in The

his team-mates. It was a quintessential Indian reaction to fame, shyness..."
I read these lines 6 years ago, i have forgotten the magazine i read it in
(it was Sportstar i think) but these lines are sort of burned into my memory
forever.
