Kaviri Mainthan in Chennai Book Fair 2007

by sps10142004 on Tue Jan 16, 2007 12:34 pm

--- Inponniyinselvan@yahoogroups.com, "SatishKumar Arunachalam"
wrote:

Hi Satish and Dear Friends,

Yesterday (15.1. Pongal) I visited the Chennai Book Fair on
Poonamalee High road - opposite Pachayappa's College, in the
evening.

Vaanathi - Paari - Thanjai Tamil University - Thimizh puthagalayam -
Saiva Siddhantha - Madras University - Annamalai University - Na
Paarthasarathy @ Vengugopalan - Kizhakku padippagam - Ainthinai -
Alliance etc., etc., have displayed their publications in Tamil.

Oriental Longmann, Oxford University Press, Macmillan etc.,
displayed their English Publications..

Sultan Chand - Asian and few others present from Northern regions..
Missed to see Banarsidas (Iconography - Indus velley books) this
year.

English - Tamil software was catching attention ..
Classic Literature + Periyapuranam - Thirumandhiram - Siddhar
Paadalgal were invariably displayed under several Publications by
various authors !

Several versions of Ponniyinselvan displayed .. including the one by
Saradha Publications.. Opposite to that stall sits our Venkat's son
and running stock of Kaviri Maindhan - with a single Banner -
PONNIYINSELVAN THODARKIRATHU ! ... WELLDONE DEAR VENKAT ! It is time
we mention and recognise the contributions of Venkat's wife (another
Thiruvengattu Nangai !) and his son ! Well-knitted Family. Best
Wishes on this happy occasion !

Apart from Vanathi & Saradha - Vardhaman Publications is also
displaying PS (which brought out economy editions first).

I purchased a set of PS + SS + PK + Thiagabhoomi for Rs. 400/- at
Saradha! One set of Aimperumkappiyangal (5 Great Tamil Epics) for
Rs. 300 at Vardhaman!
Gandhiji's Sathiyasodhanai & Pres.Abdul Kalam's Agni Siragugal for
Rs. 100/- (together!).
Narmada is selling our Vizag Dhivakar's Vamsadara + Thirumalai
Thirudan. 18 Puranas (including Vaayu as the 19th) 600/- Rig /
Yajur / Saama Adarvana Veda jist + 10 Upanishads - 600 (One Vol).

In Ananda Vikadan stall I promptly picked our dear friend - founder -
(Lalitha) Ram @ Ramahchandran Mahadevan's "Isaiyulaga Ilavarasar
GNB ". Very well written .. Gudalur (about 18 Kms East of
Mayavaram) Narayanaswamy (Triplicane Hindu High School Teacher +
Carnatic Music organiser)'s son Balasubramanian is abbrieved as GNB -
Carnatic music famous among other well known THREE LETTERers - MGR -
Sivaji - AVM - TVS etc... !

Has been invoking thoughts of Thi.Janakiraman's " Mogamull ".. a
Fiction (?) based on a Carnatic Musician on the banks of Cauvery
(Thanjai - Kummonam).

Will write separately on this again.. Hearty Congrats Dear Ram !

It is the Asian Publications which mezmerised me : Marcobolo's
Travel scripts .. Buddha Vihar to Hindu temple evolution etc...
Should visit the Book Fair again..

While leaving I was feeling Pride that such Writers like Venkat -
Ram - Dhivakar are among us and we are able to share our views
freely ! Really they are very TALL !

BEST WISHES AND SINCERE GREETINGS FOR MORE ACCOMPLISHMENTS !
