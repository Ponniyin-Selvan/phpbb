Let us consider the matters on hand :

1. Whether Temples give History ?

The inscriptions talk most of the grants made to the temple. SI
inscriptions, Epigraphia Indica, State volumes, Avanam, Valarau,
Temple, The Cholas, Pallavar Varalaru etc., are written based on
these data only. Copper plates are also furnish similar data.

Alternative is Literature.. which are considered to contain more
flattery.

Therefore, in the absense of recording of data in Temple, we will be
left with only Literature.

2. Temples do indicate EGO of Builder :

Instead of putting NO, I would consider NONE OF the Builders have
constructed their PALACEs at par with the Temple - including the one
indicated here.

Should we consider alternative diction to EGO :: Motive ..
Involvement .. Or if EGO is interpreted as fitting the gradeure of
the Builder with the combination of Motive and Involvement, then may
be EGO is not the wrong selection.

Then how are we going to explain the Monolithics in Srilanka (which
Kalki describes) and our Sravanabelaguda in neighbourhood state?

3. Single story / Multi-storied structure ::

A detailed study on Ancient Temples would provide us data that :

i) Apart from being place of worship, they were cultural centres;
ii) Provided employment to several
iii) inscriptions show that they served as Registrar office
iv) place of safeguarding food-produces
v) Sheltering the needy during calamities
vi) Served as Banks during Famine / Flood
vii) Presence of Government establishment (as MANDRAMs were
held under the shade of Trees only )- linked the Village / Panchyat
with the Central Establishment at the Throne level.
viii) Entertainment
ix) Associated with Educational Establishments


4. Place of worship is hardly multi-storied - except in certain
Vinnagarams .. Nindran - irundhan - Kidanthan type (like
Thiruveezhimizhalai, Thirukottiyur, Uthiramerur Sundraraja Perumal
etc.).. Re Big Temple, pls read concept of the temple in the
archieves.

Now the Vimanams on the top of main deity served are not that tall
and are defined for devine purpose. The Gopurams at the Entrance
also served as Check post - overseeing any invasion / intrusion.

This morning in JAYA TV a programme related to Siva-ottam (Siva run)
covering about 12 temples around Kanyakumari wherein Siva - Narayana
are the presiding deities and the devotees (more than 100 in
numbers) commence the run for 100 Kms distance chanting Siva &
Narayana on the previous day of Sivarathri day/nite and conclude
Sivarathri night!

Thanks for the opportunity to bring out this harmony.
