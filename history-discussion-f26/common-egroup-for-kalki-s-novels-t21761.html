Vanakkam,

Thinking of kalki, the first and foremost thing that comes to our mind
is PS, followed by SS, PK and so on. But we do at times forget that
Kalki had given some master pieces in current time based novels too,
among which 'Thyaga Bhoomi' is the only one i had read. The more
important fact is that most of our members, i suppose, wouldn't be
knowing the whole creations of Kalki. So it would be helpful if we
could have a separate thread or somthing in which reviews of his works
been submitted by those who read them and maintained, so that even new
joinees of the group could have a chance of knowing the creator more
widely than a narrow vision as a Historical writer or just a author of PS.
First and foremost, i would like to get to know about the number of
novels, written by our creator and then we can start posting our
reviews on them, which could form a good discussion room on his works.

Hope people will find this plausible.
