Recently I read in an article about the Curator of Egyptian Museum trying to
get back artefacts taken from Egypt. It was mentioned in that article, that
as per UN conventions articles taken before 1971 need not be returned to the
country. Any artefacts taken after 1971 should be returned and the country
in question can approach UN for the same.
