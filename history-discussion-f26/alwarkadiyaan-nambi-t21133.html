Dear Venkat
the discussion of Alwarkadiyans lingo came when someone suggested that they could do Iyengar accent so could play AN...

But as you rightly said most charectors spoke in a similar lingo...

Coming back to Alwarkadiyan

He is in his late thirties or fourties during the storyline..
Kalki claims hes vaishnavaite but his cousin brother Esanasivapattar is Saivate...

Sembiyan madhevi gives this family the young girl nandhini so he is young in his late teens and brings her up as his own sister....he does get impressed by her ability to sing andals songs and things shes andal herself.....and would offer the same impact on people like Andal...

He is dissapointed when she leaves them to go with Pazhuvettarayar and Ravidaasan...

He tries to bring her back...and make her realise that shes doing wrong...


He doesnt have any carnal desires towards her ( nandhini once says to VD oh he wants me to marry him and raise kids to spread vaishnavism)



AN also uses his Kalamugar getup so very frequently and mostly rescues our freind VD

this charector is also present in most of the crucial moments of the story

He lead AK to veerapandiyans hideout as the asiriri
he finds about Kadambur Sathiyalosanai
Pallipadai sathiyalosanai
Hes there in Elam when they find about Mandakini
Hes there when when Mandakini sacrifices herself for Sundara
Hes there when nandhini confesses her sins to PP and stops Punguzhali from going after them to seek revenge

All the while being a faithful spy to Brahmarayar and Sembiyan madhevi
thats kalkis greatness....

A charector who is not the main stay but is integral...

It is AN if I am not right who really puts the icing on the cake that is nandhini

He says Kundavai has the power to change hell to heaven however nandhini can make the man believe he is in heaven though he is in hell and rejoice in Hell

authorship.
