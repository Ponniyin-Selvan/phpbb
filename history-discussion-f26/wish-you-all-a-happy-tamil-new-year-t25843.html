Wish You all a Happy tamil New year

At a point of view it may look like a pulambal, which needs a "big
hearty laugh"to talk about the language apart from our profession.
But it is not.

If the big countries of the World namely United Kingdom& many more
can open many Centres all over the World in other Countries -
{speaking different languages and professing different cultures}-
with a view of introducing and spreading their own Language,
Literature, Culture and Fine Arts through their Centres named as
British Council, American Cultural Center, Russian Cutural Centre,
Gothe Institute, Alliance Francaise, then why not TamilNadu open
such Centres all over the World (named Tamil Cultural
Institute,tamil chair presently there are only 4 tamil chair in
india out of one in Bhubaneswar)and promote Tamil Language,
Literature, Culture and Fine Arts to the Tamils and Non-Tamils
living in various other countries of the World.

This will help the Tamil who have now spread worldwide not to loose
touch of their own language,culture,traditions, fine arts etc, and
not being absorbed and assimilated in the foreign societies and
their cultures, loosing their own identity as Tamils.

During the great days of Tamils under Rajaraja Chola - 1 & Rajendra
Chola - 1 and others thousand years back (A.D.985-A.D.1070) this
cultural spread did really take place,

Even they ruled kalinga through choda ganga dynasty .
the impact of which is very visible in the culture and language in
some countries of the World.

This fact is further evidenced by the presense of Tamil Stone
Inscriptions around 900/1000 years old, discovered in the recent
times by Archeological Departments of some countries, namely
Thailand, Burma, China, Sumatra (in Indonesia), Malaysia and Sri
Lanka.

If companies and corporate house of tamilnadu will mixe commerce
with tamil culture definitly tamil culture will improve like HDFC
bank using ratham (chariot)as their display card

Imagine Western Women gradually going crazy to wear Sarees and
Kanchipuram and other Sarees being exported to the Western
countries, like how the Westerners have made the young Tamil Girls
of TamilNadu crazy with Denim Trousers and Cotton Tee-Shirts.

There are very many items that could find export
to foreign countires from tamil nadu along with tamil cultureal
background.

Like how the west has made the youngsters in TamilNadu crazy with
Pizzas and western Foods

Why not make the tamil girls of the west who are used to dancing
Waltz and BallRoom Dance etc to dance Kummi & Koll Attams & Baratha
Natyam with long plats,Malligai poo & kunjams!!!! during their tamil
functions

If Indian Tamilians want to take on the task of spreading Tamil and
it's history to The Tamils and non - Tamils in the greater
Indian/South Asian diaspora or the world, the Tamilians in India
MUST CHANGE so that they can be easily approached by other non-
tamilians. The reason many communities in India,

I have noticed, look at Tamilians in a contemptuous manner because
of how we view ourselves and that pride we have within our own
culture. It is high time Tamilians take charge of their own affairs
and change their views on India, South India and Tamil's place in
India herself. , TN as a state will not be a place, other South
Indians and Indians as a whole will want to look up to.

Yes

Tamil is an ancient language of India and is a priceless jewel in
India. Tamils must preserve that jewel to the best of it's ability,
but not at the risk of demeaning and demonizing other linguisitc
communities (Hindi speakers/ North Indians) and castes
(Brahmins) ..change that and you will see change happen.

So my suggestions to all Indian Tamilians - Change your outlook and
perspective on your country

let us give importance to our language

at the same time respect other languages too

our country spilted due to administrative measures some 50 years ago

let us give importance to the mother language not to any other

your comments please

Endroom anbudan
