Suggesting a PS Group meet in Chennai in the First Week of Feb 2007
when Dr. Sridhar Rathnam will be visiting us from London.
On this occasion, we can consider :

1. A Group meet in Bhavan's hall Mylapore;
2. Press meet re points we want to raise for Public awareness;
3. Felicitation of Writers : Venkat - Pavithra - Gokul - Vizag
Dhiwakar - Rajni Ramki from our Group. Chennai Book Fair would have
ended by then.
4. Exhibition of PS Assets by Karthik mother + other possible
exhibits
5. Seminar which includes Writer members and participation of
members like Dr. Thiagasathiamurthy (ASI) - Bangalore
Gopalakrishnapillai - Sriraman - Kalingatamil - Mumbai Uma and other
New members who have been contributing regularly;
6. Quiz programme
7. Sale of Calender of PS Group / with T shirt / Cap etc.
8. Cultural programmes of PS Members and family : Dance + Drama (?)
9. SPECIAL INVITEES on Dias ( to be decided by a Committee)
Consider : Magic Lantern - who first staged PS Drama
Vikraman - who wrote sequels to PS and had direct
association with Amarar Kalki
Manian Selvan & some rep. from Kalki Family
10. Co-ordinators : Chennai :: Venkteswaran - Madan - Sivaram
Bangalore :: Muhil - Deepa Prakash
11. Public Relations (Media) : Chandra TKV Rajan
--------------------------
We can invite Historians and request them to speak on specific
topics with a Question / Answer Session.

Say about 3 pm to 8 pm with Dinner
or 9 am to 2 pm with Lunch.

Suggestions welcome.
