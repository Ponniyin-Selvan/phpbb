Location&Direction:This
Temple is located 9 Kilometres from Dindukal.As there is no lodging
facility at this place, it's better to stay at Dindukal and plan to
visit this place.


Current Name : Thaadikombu (Thaazhai vaanam)
MainDeity : SoundaraRajaPerumal
Godesses : Soundaravalli
Theertham : Thaazhai Theertham
Dharshan Timings: Morning 6-11.30 Evening 4-8.30pm

Legend
says that Thirumalirunzholai Nambi azhagar perumal (madurai
kallazhagar) himself came to this place to help saint Mandugar and
Killed demon Thaalasuran. As Madurai kallazhagar himself poises as
SoundaraRajaPerumal,Almost all the festivals celebrated at kallazhagar
temple is being celebrated here as well.Famous one being Lord
soundararaja perumal goes through kudaga River at Chitra Pournami for
Godess Annai Abhirami. This temple was renovated by Achutha Devar later
by konirama Devar.

I have visited this temple a couple of times. My sister lives in
Dindigul and we all made to this place two-three times earlier

(appa...first time Natarajan Prakash has posted a temple which I know

One of the beutiful temple I have visited. The munmandapam has around
12 (14?) amazing sculptures in each of the pillars. Each is around 8-10
feet tall and very lively and intricate sculptures.

What I feel bad is, all the time I went there, I reached there around 5
in the evening and before I can get satisfied seeing these beauties, it
will become dark. worth a visit.
