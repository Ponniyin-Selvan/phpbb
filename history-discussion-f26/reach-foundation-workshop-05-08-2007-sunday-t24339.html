REACH FOUNDATION WORKSHOP :: 05.08.2007 Sunday

by sps10142004 on Sun Aug 05, 2007 10:18 pm

Dear Friends,

Today in Eldams Road "Thathwaloka" Hall, our beloved plstics Chandra
invited UZHAVARAPPANI members from various parts of the State.

Dr. Thiaga Sathiamurthy welcomed "Silai meetta Semmal" Dr. R.Nagaswamy
who presided over the function. Chief Guest is non other T.S.Sridhar
IAS, who rexcently assumed Charges as State Tourism Secretary from
State Archeology Dept. Because of Saniperyarchi, HR&CE Board Jt. Secy
could not attend. The hall was given free for use by TN Ramachandran.

Dr. Nagaswmy's speech with Power point presentation on Tranqubar
Masilamaneeswarar temple was remarkable. TS Stidhar IAS highlighted
tourism potential in ancient places of worship.

Our beloved PS Sriraman's power point presentation on how to preserve
the temple, DOs and DONTs guidelines while undertaking renovation with
specific reference to Dharmasthala Temple Preservation Trust stole the
show.

Uzhavaranappani participants were represented in the dias by Komal
Sekar - whose speech was well attended and appreciated by one and all
and evidently HE WAS THE MAN OF THE WORKSHOP.

Komal Sekar - teamed with Coimbatore Kaali Steel Vasaantha Kumar
Industrialist - has unertaken several prominent projects in Thanjai
District such as Konerirajapuram, Karkadageswarar Thiruvisalur etc.
His friend Murali of Chadura published Panniru Thirumurai in single
volume and recognised by Chidambaram Dikshidars as reported early this
year in our Group.

Komal Sekar highlighted ::

1. Preserving Temple by weeding any plant growing in the gaps of the
walls / roofs so that major repair can be avoided in early stage
itself.

2. Necessity of keeping the Temple Water Tanks in usable condition -
obviously with rainwater harvesting facilities.

3. Necessity of a Nandavanam (Flower Garden)

4. Necessity of Ghosala.

From: Padhigams he quoted that well-preserved is bound keep away
disceases, keep those responsible for Power (in Democratic process
all the people themselves ) in good health and Monsoon without failure.

When Dr. Nagaswamy spoke he clarified that Thennadudaya Sivan (or
Thirumal) is ENNATTAVARKKUM IRAIVAN - citing NOT only the temples in
Cambodia, Indonesia or Northern India, but also referring to a 2000
yr old GREEK COIN which contain the image of Balaraman one side and
Krishna on the reverse with Tamil letters and Greek letters - that our
Gods are Universal!

The dias represented Dr. Nagaswamy from History, HR&CE / Komal Sekar -
Religeon, TS Sridhar IAS - Tourism and Dr. Thiaga Sathiamurthy as
REACH Co-Oridinator at APEX LEVEL !

The enthusiasm of Uzahvarappani members is remarkable .. and
definitely their potency need to be channalised.

Plastic Chandra and his team mates - our Madhan included - Latha, her
husband Suresh, Auditors Hariharan and Auditor Chandrasekar are
working with Zeal .. to initiate MOVEMENT under the able guidance of
our PS Sriraman and Dr. Thiaga Sathiyamurthy..


Best wishes to one and all.

sps
