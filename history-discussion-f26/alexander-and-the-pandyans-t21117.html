Dear RJC,

The first grand Alliance of Chera- Chola - Pandya appeared to have
taken place against Emperor Asoka! - 300 BC.

Pallavas controlled Thanjai (Trichy Rock Temple Inscription), 6th AD;

Vijayalaya conquered Mutharayas and controlled Thanjai.. 850 AD;

Rastrakuta Krishna III conquered upto Cauvery (tho" he boasts of
conquering Rameswaram as well) - 950 AD;

Parantaka I, Sundara, RRC and RJC - conquered Pandyas say 920 to
1120 stretch.

Yet none of them ever disturbed any place of worship !

On the contrary, Historians say Rajasimhan was the role model of
Rajarajan! He has also followed the life-pattern of Cheraman
Perumal - who lived about 150 years prior to him!

Sundara Pandya avenged the defeat of Pandyas after about 400 years
and conquered Rajaraja III !

His Meykeerthi is in our File Section. Detailing how he avenged.
Destroyed every single Pillar built by Chozhas - except the 16
Pillar Mandap from where Karikalan gave away grants to
Uruthirankannanar who sung Pattinappalai !

And he gave grants to the Big Temple !
He provide Gold-roof to Srirangam !
He did not touch a single stone in any Temple in Chozha land !

Earlier, when Chalukya Vikramadithan reached Kanchi - to avenge the
defeat and setting fire to their Capital Vaatapi by Narasimha I, on
the outskirts upon seeing Kailasanaatha Temple built by Rajasimhan,
all his fire to set ablase Kanchi was extinguished and was thunder-
stuck by the Temple's grandeur. He made grants and ordered his army
to retreat!

Our Kings / Emperors Greatness is Unique !

united front against a future invader.

it was a miserable flop. till the invader came upto srirangam news
did not reach the king at madurai. there were no vassals to stop the
invader or atleast delay him till the emperor got his battle fatigues
ready.



whereas on the contrary if a pallava had stopped malik kafur in
thindivanam, chola stopped him in srirangam the madurai war might
