Dear Cheenu, Nice to read the link (used auto select) to read Tamil
fonts.

Thinulallam @ Konerirajapuram is HISTORICALLY very important
temple. The points brought out Author Krishnamurthy are worth
pondering :

1. The temple was constructed in 973-974. Kandraditha Thevar - Great
Sivanadiar - lived through this. The diety is named after him;
2. RRC named a diety in Thenneri after Uthama.
3. Moovenda velalan accorded grants in memory of Kandraditha.
4. Sembiayanmadevi was Superpower during Uthama period and completed
Tiruvakkarai in ( 987 - wrong) completed in 1000. Died in 1001.

This paves way for detailed discussion on this topic. Let the
inputs lead to assess the Coordial or otherwise relationship between
Uthama and RRC.

But, it is established fact that KONERIRAJAPURAM is Sembiyanmadevi's
DREAM project. If one goes through the grants described in the
inscriptions, she has accorded and the meticuluous planning to
maintain the temple, one is likely to think that it is the
forerunner for RRC to plan the Big temple management.

Re the famous QUOTE of SEMIBIAN MADEVI THIRUVAYIRUDITHA UT HAMA
CHOZHA, I have earlier mailed - now repeated - quote from PAPANASAM
(Kudanthai - Thanjai) temple inscriptions ::

RRC in his 7th (992) year has reviewed all the grants - tax free
lands etc and upon finding several irregularities only ordered NEW
SURVEY in 1000 - with famous "Ulagalandhan kole". In 992, in
Papapanasam he checked all the grants and REINSCRIBED THEM in
various Panels. Panel 1 to 8 contained the inscriptions of Uthama
duly reinscribed which read " Uthama chozhar is yaandu ... in 1st
panel and ends in the 8th panel as "Sembiyanmadevi thiruvayiruditha
Uthama chozhar //" and RRC continues " in 7th Regnl yr of RRC ..."
and orders continuation of earlier grants and gives additional
grants also..

Therefore, RRC adopted what was being said earlier by Uthama and
these are not the terms coined by Rajaraja Chozha himself! One
cannot also rule out a sarcasm!

Yet, it is interesting to note that this year also ARUDHRA is being
conducted in Good Scale from 04.01.2006 to 13.01.2006 in Thirunallam
ARULMIGU DHEGA SUNDARI SAMETHA UMAMAHESWARAR TEMPLE located 3 Km
South of S.Pudhur ( Kudanthai - Karaikkal route). Abhishekam at 4 am
on 31.01.2006 Friday - to the Biggest Nataraja 9 feet tall - in the
World! Gurukkal phone : 0435 2449830 Gnanaskandhan.
