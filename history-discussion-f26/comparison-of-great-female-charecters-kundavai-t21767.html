Comparison of Great Female Charecters:Kundavai

by sridhar_rathinam on Wed Sep 13, 2006 5:36 pm

Dear Suganya
Kundavai was possibly the only female charector who had a stable mind to the end...so we all accuse her of playing politics etc...

lets face it how many of you have hated your freinds or your brothers just because your brother is freindly with your female freind....hands on the heart....thats female nature...

thats what happens between Kundavai and Nandhini...moreover Kundavai was Sembiam Madhevi's pet as well so she influences her decisions to shun nandhini as well

second she was ambitious but she was a women so she wants a pure progeny for the chola heir..so she promates AK and AMV and groomes Vanadhi

She also knows the pains her father went through because of his love for a commoner and she doesnt want the same for amv

When she knows Nadhini's story she does try to make amendments...

Poonguzhali changes from one extreme to another
Vanadhi changes
Manimegalai changes
even Nandhini changes
but
Kundavai stands steadfast....in her love for her Kingdom..

regards

Sri

Suganya wrote:
Hi Sri and Venkatesh,

How abt Kundavai...

From: my point of view, I see Kundavai as a good "Raja Thandri".

To justify this point, I can say abt how she was side-lining
Poonguzhali from Arumozhi. She was afraid that Arulmozhi might get
impressed by the practical and dynamic Poonguzhali rather than timid
and shy, Vanathi.

She must have also thought that Arulmozhi should also not get into the
same swirl like Aditha Karikalan. AK fell in love with Nandini, a
non-royal blood which led to lot of confusion.

She does everything for the betterment of the Royal clan than doing
justice to everyone's individual feelings. She is so aristocratic and
made her wishes happen. I dont think she would have ever thought about
Vandhiyathevan, had him being a non-royal blood.

Is this nature good or bad ? Or this superiority complex is expected
out of Kundavai, a born queen....This nature of Kundavai only kindled
the ego of Nandini to take revenge against her in either way.

Anbudan,
Suganya

Comparison of Great Female Charecters:Kundavai

by suganya_ml on Thu Sep 14, 2006 6:51 pm

Exactly Sri....She is the one stable-minded. But, never
changed/adopted herself for circumstances. That's my point.

She made up her mind that Vanathi is AMV's bride even before she came
to know abt Oomai Rani's story. Isn't that not influencing over one's
individual feelings....She never even gave choice to him to decide.
She concluded everything on her own....

For her, only Chola Kingdom is indispensable and not the members of
the Kingdom. Being ambitious, she made others to sacrifice for her
ambitions to come true.

In assumption that Vandiyathevan not from a royal clan, this Kundavai
character would not have even fell in love with him. The same reason
why she removed Poonguzhali from the queue.

FYI, the female character I like the most in PS is Kundavai only. But,
still I am looking from the opposite side also. )
