sps wrote in 16344 / 2007 : SEMBIAN MADEVI

Nice summing up on Uthama Dear Bala. missed to read earlier.

From Nageswaran temple (Kumbknm) we find a Meenavan Madevi (Pandya
clan Queen of Uthama) who also visited Tiruvottiyur along with him!

Re Konerirajapuram, ( about 8 Km east of Kumbakonam) the observation
of ASI that the beared-man was Kandrathithar leads to the speculation
that he lived during Uthama period and was witnessing the
consecration
of this Thirunallam temple (Bhoomiswaramudayar) which was done in the
3rd Regnl yr of Uthama, possibly 972 AD.. Means he did not die as per
storyline of PS..

The pattern of grants and arrangements for implementation by
Sembianmadevi is definitely a fore-runner for Rajaraja to take queue
in organising such things in Rajarajeswaram !

The chief Engineer of Konerirajapuram was HARA SARANA SEKARAN ! In
Thanjai Big temple, it was Thennavan Moovenda velalan @ adithan
suriyan @ Rajaraja Perunthachan!

Sembian madevi (natal star Kettai / Chithirai month) festival is
celebrated every year in Nagai Dt Sembian madevi village ! In one go,
we will get all the names of Uthama's queens from the inscriptions
over here .. lavish grants !

Undoubtedly she has transformed several brick-temples to Katrali
(Stone temples ) during Sundara - Uthama and RRC period ! But we
should also draw our attention as to the source of such funds !

When I raised this with some of the contemporary Scholars, they did
not over rule the possibility of a Financial Pact between Sundara
Chozha family and Kandraditha family - with Sembian madevi at the
helm!

In the 16th year of Uthama, she gave grants in Thiruvarur and
Thirupurambiyam. She reportedly passed away in 1001 after completing
consecreation of Chandramouleeswarar in Thiruvakkarai!

In Mukkoodal(near Chingleput) , there appears to be memorial mandap
buit for her by RRC !

As rightly depicted by Kalki, Sembian madevi is the first of the
royal
Queens to have projected herself so prominently .. and Kundavai -
sister of RRC followed suit. Olgamadevi, Crown Queen of RRC and
Panchavanmadevi Queen of RRC also played their roles very well ..

Thiruvarur Paravai, Udayar Rajendra Chozha devari Anuukiar Paravayar
Nagayar .. was equally prominent in his tenure.. for whose sake RJC
gold sheeted the vimanam of Thiruvarur temple !
