by vijay_v5 on Thu Apr 07, 2005 9:19 pm

Hello Friends

I have a thought that if people from this group have a
dream of making Ponniyin Selvan serial why don't we do
our selves.

Nothing is impossible.

There are 2 approach we needed.

1] Excecutive structure
2] Marketing structure

1] We need to have sponsorships on episodes per week
one basis. I am ready to sponsor 1 episode.

2] We have to generate money by allowing
people(arranging tourists programs) to visit live
shooting and visit Chola places. That generates some
money regularly and gives word-to-mouth advertisement.

3] Getting abroad TV pre commitments, pre commited
business advertisements.

If we do this great work, ours become great as
Ponniyin Selvan's historic thiyagam and Kalkis great
work.

I just put here my idea, please share your ideas.
People in India or abroad can do their best to find
people sponsoring episodes.

If things goes well we can start something this year
ponniyin selvan's birth day.

Thanks
Vijay

by css_rajan on Fri Apr 08, 2005 9:26 pm

--- Venkateswaran Kasirajan
wrote:
Over the years, people have built-up an image about
RR, Paluvettaraiyar, Nandhini etc., It is very
difficult to meet the expectations with a TV
serial...

What happend to the movie "Parthiban Kanavu." I
guess
it was never a hit...
HI
Parthiban Kanavu was not a bad movie. I dont know
whether it was a hit or not but it went along with the
main theme of Kalki's story.
By the way there is no harm in giving a genuine try by
any capable person in making PS TV serial. Even when
Mahabaratha was started, people were sceptical about
it. There were doubts whether the director would be
able to bring in all the important side stories of the
epic. Characters of Krishna, Yudhishtira, Bheema,
Arjuna, Duruyodhan.... were etched in the common
people's mind for centuries when you compare the
impact of the characters of PS. But still with obscure
actors, the serial became the biggest hit in the
Indian TV history and the ators also became idetified
with the characters( almost none of them were able to
make it big in any other serial or movies except
Mukesh Khanna who played Bhishma). The problem with us
is that we are trying to fit PS characters with the
existing movie artists....if some good director can
unearth new talents and new faces to mould them to PS
characters it will be worth a try.
Sundar
