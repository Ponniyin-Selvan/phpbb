confirmed attendees

by abhivencat on Sun Aug 06, 2006 1:10 pm

Hi all

the meet was attended by 8 of us.
and a great time we had

it was more oriented to the literary aspects of our group and we had
an opportunity to go indepth in PS again.

that is something we dont have much of an opportunity to do in the
group these days where people talk more of of epigraphy and
iconography.

we discussed ponniyin selvan, the scene of aaditya karikalans murder
and took a vote on who could held the hilt of the sword that killed
him.
we also talked about the greatness of the chalukyas .
sankaran briefed us on the three chola heirs to the throne who were
murdered, and how kulothungan needled his way to the throne. he seemed
rather angry that kuluthungan had been compared to RRC in these
columns.
sivaram, seenu,madan along with chandra, muruganandam, sathish,
sankaran and myself attended.

the menu was sundal, sukku cofee, cauliflower, vaalakkai and chilly
bajji.

it was a wonderful time guys after quite some time, and I hope the
others enjoyed it as much as I did

venketesh
