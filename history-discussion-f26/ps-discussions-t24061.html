by satish_arun on Fri Apr 15, 2005 7:10 pm

aahahahaaa........rendu moonu naal mail pakkalana evvalavu
matrangalaaa? I am very much held up with my work these days and thats
the reason for my prolonged silence. Once in a couple of days I just
check PS mails alone...when I was going through the mails today, I got
confused reading Sri's mail about the topic. I was totally confused
... even thought "loosu ayitena...onnume puriyaliye" then only I
checked the messgae numbers and found that they jump about 8-9 mails.
I read the mails directly from the yahoogroup site and hence lost the
flow. Probably I should check my mail box for the lost mails to see
whether the topic is interesting or not.

So we should not discuss anything other than PS or Kalki. Then we have
to form a different group for Parthiban kanavu, a different group for
Sivagamiyin selvan, a different group for English Literature etc. I
cant manage with just one or two groups itself. If I have 100 groups,
better I dont want a group at all. I will leave all the group and be
at peace. why shoudl we discuss about Mahabalipuram ASI findings in
this group. That is also not relevant to PS groups ideology?

I never got a chance to read English literature and even never liked
to read it. TO be frank after reading PS 5 years back, I stopped
reading any english novel altogether. I kept quite when Priya, Sri and
others were discussing about Foresyth or any other english author/
novels. Its matter of choice I make whether to take it or not. If I
leave the group because in one mail Sri and Priya were discussing
English novels and feel that this group is PS only by nakesake, whose
fault it is?

If we should stick to the purpose, we should not even say hi to our
collegues, becuase in office we are supposed to only work. We should
not talk to our next seat friend, or our team mate, we should not
discuss movies cricket etc. at office, should not check personal mails
in office, should not use office resource for personal use (PC,
printer etc). At office we should work work and only work for 8-12
hours. Are we doing that?

If discussing only PS is the sole purpose of this group, then it will
be monotonous. PS kindled historical thirst amoungst us. What happened
yesterday is also history. People should be free to discuss things and
some xyz, leaving the group for such things should not be taken
seriously. If people dont like no one is stopping them to publicly
condemn such debates. Only kindergarten kids complain to the teacher
secretly. I was under the assumption that PS group has grown up
people, but I am surprised to hear that there are still some "kids at
heart" characters here. No offence meant, I am trying to say that
people should be bold enough to say things openly. If in person, I can
be afraid to say something, due to fear of getting beaten up, but
through mails, ah....come on give me a break.

Brushing aside facts is easy, facing it is quite difficult. Groups
such us PS, should build up that kind of strengh to the people so that
they can feel stronger. Why we talk about History, why we talk about
RR, only because we feel a sense of strenght in us thinking about RR.
If the society is filled with cheap politicians and people who take
comman man for a ride, its every individuals responsibility to make
others feel strong and face the fact. Without this underlying essense,
talking only history is of no use.

Why did Kalki wrote Parthiban Kanavu? only to kindle the patriatic
feelign among the people. Why should he talk history, when talking
about freedom struggle? If freedom struggle is Kalkis motto, then as
per this groups norms, he is a fool to talk about Parthiban and his
kanavu in a form of novel. Becuase he should have talked only about
freedom and not history.

But things change. We can always learn. I am learning a lot these
days. Talking facts is fanatism. Kalki is a fool to have talked about
history when his motto was freedom struggle.

New dimension......new thoughts............great............I am
learning a lot. quite interesting.

Satish

NB : People who wished the group for tamil puthandu, this is also not
the motto of PS group. We should only discuss about PS and should not
send personal wishes to others in this public forum. So better change
yourselves and dont repeat it again..hahahaaaa...hhhhhh........Just
kidding??? Am I? I leave it to you all to guess....

PS on namesake and leaves....
Thats why I request an alternative platform may be
Thamilvalarchi...maybe thamilchamrajiam.....

Thats all I have to say

Regards
