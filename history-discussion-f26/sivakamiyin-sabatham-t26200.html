by abhivencat on Mon May 12, 2008 6:14 pm

Hi
one of the best parts of sivakamiyin sabatham were the seeds kalki
sows in the story for happenings beyond the book's timeline.
the best example was the romance between nedumaran and
mangayarkarasi. though they are very important people they come as
side charecters in sivakamiyin sabatham.

it was a great visualisation of the early years of a couple we know
little about. infact if there are a 100 tamils in history who have
changed the way we live today thiis couple would find a place right
on top. it was nice that kalki recognized the fact in his own way.

also sivakamiyin sabatham has so many charecters who had actually
lived. anybody in history at that time kalki pulled in
starting from huen tsang the chinese traveller.
the nayanmars in the book include paranjothi, appar,mangayarkarasi,
nedumaran and kulachiraiyar.

--bhuvaneshwari sundaram :

ungalakku SS mela an evalo kovam?????()
Ondraaaaaaa... Rendaa kobangal... Ellaam sollavey, oru mail podhuma...

Sivagaamiyi sabadham maadhiri oru paththu volme pogum. Irundhaalum solla muyarchikkarane:

1) No inspirational characters
2) No characters that reflected me
3) Orey thegattara romance.... Mills & Boons thoththudhu
4) Kashtta padara heroine, kashtta padara hero
5) Sogam, sogam, melum sogam
6) Irundha orey oru nalla jovial character (Paranjothi)rum first chapterodaclose (uyir irukkum, aanaa character close)
7) Bikshu oru ponnai virumbaradhu, adhukku plan pannaradhu, etc. Etc. Karmam karmam.
8) Vaanathi maadhiri oru heroine - dream girl-kku badhilaa dreaming girl: maan mayil kuyil nu suththara heroine.
9) most of the characters are emotional than rational in SS, barring a few.PS-la villains mudharkondu ellaarumey budhdhisaaligal. SS pazhi vaanga sandai podara goshtti, Tamil film heroism of the 80s. All thoththaangulees.
10) SS padikka naraya maturity venum (read: vayasaagi irukkanum, atleast manasalavula).


Innum naraya irukku, blackberryla type panna mudiyaadhadhaala idhoda neruththikkarane.

Sari sari, oru samaadhaana udanpadikkai. Maybe SS is not my cup of pattai saaraayam.... vandhu, I mean coffee. Ungalai maadhiri enakkum nara vayasaanapparam, irunga irunga...kochchukkaadheenga. Unga ellaarayum maadhiri enakkum maturity vandhapparam, I might have enough appreciation for SS also. Adhuvaraikkum Kalkiyai mannikkaradhaa illai, ippadi oru novel ezhudhinadhukku.
