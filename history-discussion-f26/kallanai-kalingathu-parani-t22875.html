Dear RJC,

No doubt Karikalan built kallanai. Sevi vazhi cheydhi is that he
gave Kalappali in Vallam - Ekaveeri - before commencing Dam.

But is it the same dam, which we call today as Kallanai?

Scholars say no .. They say the dam located in the East of Trichy
called as Kallanai was built by the Nayaks !

That is why the subject assumes significance !!

Then which was the dam ? It is said Karikalan reinforced the bunds
of Cauvery using stones .. A coin of about 1500 yr old in Madras
Musuem shows a tamil inscription and a river flowing through two
small mounds ..

The scene resembles exactly with the two small mounts near Nemam
(near Tirukkandiyur !). This is a view of another Scholar !

Kallanai was a Natural dam, where the flow of cauvery was restricted
and its fury was further subdued by reinforcing the bunds with
Stones !
