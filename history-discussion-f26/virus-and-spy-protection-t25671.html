I just thought it will be useful for the group to be protected against viruses and spyware. Fundamentally, you need three things:

a) A good anti virus
b) A good firewall
c) A good anti spyware

If you are happy to pay, the best one to install is Norton Internet Security Suite - which offers you all the three.

In terms of freeware, I would recommend the following for all our members -all of them free for download

1) For good anti virus - AVG free version is good
2) For firewalls - I would recommend Zone Alarm
3) For Anti Spyware - Spybot is good. I use Ewido, which is really good.

It is essential that you have ALL the three on your computer. As Sudharshanji said, things are getting very sophisticated, but you dont need to throw your PCs away - installing the above should be able to do the trick.

In addition, a lot of the viruses come because of Internet Explorer. You might like to install Mozilla Firefox, an alternative browser - it does appear that most of the viruses are written for a the Internet Explorer environment and not yet for the Mozilla environment - hence there is a degree of protection.
