Re LOVE, there are five pairs highlighted in PS:

Arumozhi - Poonguzhali :: Ficticious

Adithakarikalan - Nandhini :: Ficticious

Nandhini :: Vandhiyathevan :: Fictictious - debatable

Manimegalai :: Vandhiyathevan :: - do -

Vandiyathevan :: Kundavai :: Real - matured into marraige


By this, Vandhiyathevan is highlighted as more desirable by the Princesses,
yet his heart longed for Kudavai - the greatest & glamarous bride of their
times - and finally they got married. Rajendra's son and Kundavi's
daughter got married and Kundavi's daughter's heirs paved way for Telugu
Chozhars.


In Chozha History we are reading of 1. Veeman Kundavai 2. Vaidumbai Kalyani
3. veeranarayani 4. Vananavan Madevi 5. Sembian Madevi 6. Kundavai 7.
Danthisakthividangi @ Olgamadevi (RR's Pattamahishi), 8. Thribuvanamdevi @
Vanathi (RJC's mother. Inscriptions speak of RR's other beloved wife (1)
Panchavanmadevi / her maid servant (2) Kundavi (who fell in love with
Vimaladithan and married him. Thiruvaiyaru inscriptions speak of him.
Vimadthan crowned as Chalukya King by RJC).

I do not know whether we remember our own aunts' names so well...

This is because Chozhas gave prominence to their womenfolk - they had the
right and liberty in bidding Auctions, permitting grants and recording
their inscriptions!

Kalki was prudent to pick the best pair from the inscriptions and
projected their love beautifully.

That is how Vandhiyathevan - Kundavi are being discussed as one of the best
lovers in History!

In comparison to this, Nandhi's attention towards Vdthvn appears to be
opportunistic and lusty.

Characterisation of Nandhini is being frequently discussed in our group ..
she is a class by herself.. She is an anti-heroine and amolgamation of
several real characters...

It occured to me that King George's love for a common person would have
kindled Kalki to introduce Poonguzhali as lover of Arumozhi!

Sri (Sir) Sridhar - pls opine.

Regards, sps

Dear Sri,

Even last week I visited Thiruvalanchuzhi (Dr. Kalaikkovan & Dr.Nalini were
also there giving finishing touches to their new invents ) and could not
resist visiting Pazhayarai - which is just 0.5 Km South. When Crossing
Kudanthai - Thanjai Main Road (Raja Paattai) I was visualising the scenerio
10 centuries back .. Royal Palaces - particularly those of the RR's wives
and Kundavai - should have been Guarded well .. Even looking at them from a
far away point itself should have been prohibitive .. Imagine
Vandhiyathevan meeting Kundavai - talking to her - impressing her - falling
in love with her - her getting impressed - falling in love with him and
MARRYING HIM at last!

She has given grants to several temples including Nallur - near Sundara
Perumal koil, Thiruvaiyaru etc. Constructed Jinalayam, Rajarajeswaram near
Esalam ... Has purchased lands in Public Auction - imagine NOT LIKE PRESENT
DAY GOVERNMENT ACQUISITION as wrongly conceived by some of our members -
along with CROWN QUEEN (OF RAJARAJA) DANTHISAKTHI VIDANGI - reclaimed from
Cauvery River-bed and donated them to Mahadevar Temple! She had the
Authority - Money Power and Will to do so!

Queen Panchavan Madevi's maid Servant was granted persmission to donate 6
Golden flowers to Thiruppugalur Thirunavukkarasar and recorded in
inscriptions!

Kundavi made golden idols of Sundara and Vanamadevi and arranged worship in
Thanjai Big Temple !

In Real life, Vandhiyathevan, should have been very much powerful in
proposing and marrying Kundavai!


Regards, sps
