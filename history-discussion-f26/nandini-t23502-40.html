4.May I add another point. nandhini was created just to add support to Kundavai character. In a plot where there is real no hero/heroine Vs Villain, when a plot focus on more around situations rather than characters, it imperative to create pairs of characters. VVD-Alwarkadiyan, Aditya-Rajaraja, Kundavai & her shadow Vanadi- Nandhini, Aniruddha-Paluvetta raiyar, Pathibendran- Kandamaran, many opposing minor characters. If you write a story with just Kundavai and her shadow Vanadi, it will be a drab Kundavai pampering RRC, teasing Vanadi, consulting the Kudanthai astrolgers, discussing religion with Sembiyan madevi. Kundavai character will lose its importance. So Nandhini was created by Kalki to highlight Kundavai.
