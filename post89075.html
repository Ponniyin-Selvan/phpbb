Dear Ram, In continuation of my earlier message, I would like to add
from Aagamic sources that:

Yoga and Tantra are complementary and included in the all embracing
experience of the Reality. God (Siva) revealed the pure knowledge
through His famous five faces - UPPER - Easana, EASTERN -
Tatpurusha,
SOUTHERN - Aghora, NORTHERN - Vamadeva and WESTERN - Satyajata.

Incidentally in Brahadesvara temple, the Sikara & Stupi on top
represent Easan, and other four sides the other four faces, the
whole
structure being the gross form (Stula) of Sadasiva. The Badra
openings
in all the four directions serve as Mukhas. RRC has established a
metal Panchadeha murthy in this Temple.

The said pure knowledge of transcendental Siva is primarily
manifested
as Naada, the first expression of the pure consciousnes in the form
of
vibration (Spanda). That vibration assumed the form of scriptural
knowledge after ascending to the sphere of Sadasiva. From: each of
the
five faces respectively Laukik (empirical), Vaidik (Scriptural),
Adhyatmika (spiritual), Atimarga (transcendental) and Mantratmak
(hymns) have emanated. Thus total number of sources come to
Twentyfive
(which are shown in the inner side of Nallur temple Gopuram).

By the Grace of Siva, a Saadhaka (disciple) on obtaining the three
kinds of DIKSHAS namely Vedha - Manu and Kryia from the Guru,
getting
rid of three impurities - Maaya - Karma - Anava, achieving the
conditions of Malaparipaaka and Karma-samya becomes free from the
cycle of Birth and Death and consequently attains the state of
Liberation.
