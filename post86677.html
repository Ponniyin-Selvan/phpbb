A few months ago, I read the thread about the PS trip by Ms. Pavithra
(http://historicaljourneys.blogspot.com/

about how to reach the places etc., But as luck would have it my search

include my name as well in the list. It would be really great to travel
these places with people who much travel with the same idea as I would,
in the process ending up with a couple of friends,may be.,

Awaiting response,

Chennai.
