I have to confess I know pretty little about the alwars.
sure andal came from pandi nadu and peyalvar from thondai nadu.
can some body list where each of the alwars came from?

venketesh
======================================
Our Venkatesh is an expert in this Anthakshi ..
Will try to put some basics related to Azhwars :

POYGAIYAR - BOODHATHAR - PERYAZHWAR :: believed to have been born
(without physical contact between human) out of the Grace of Lord
Vishnu . Azhwars' Vishnu Amsas are also denoted.

Poygai: Kanchi(Poygai)- Aippasi Thioruvonam -Ist Thiruvandhadhi/SANGU
Boodathar: Maamallai - Neelorpam flower- Aip.Avittam - 2nd-do- DANDU
Peyazhwar: Mylapore - Sevvalli-Aipaasi SADHAYAM (!) - 3RD -DO- SWORD

Thirumzahisai (near Chennai)- Thai Magam - Thiruchandavirutham-CHAKRA
NAMMAZHWAR- Thirukkurugur/ Tnveli- Vaigasi Visakam - 1102 - NITYASURI
MADURAIKAVI-Thirukkolur- Chithirai/chit- Kanninun siruthaambu-KUMUDAN

Thondaradipodi- Trvaiyaru-Kum: Margazhi Kettai - Thirumalai / THULASI
Thiruppanazhwar-Uraiyr - Kaarthigai/rohini* - Amalanathipiran / MARU
Thirumangai - Thirunagari - Kaarthigai/Kaarthigai -1134+ 226/SARNGAM

Kulasekara-Tiruvannchikalam-Masi/Punarvsu-PerumalThirumozhi-KOUSTHUBM
Periyazhwar-Srivlpthur-Aani/Swathi- Pallandu/Per.Thirumozhi-473GARUDN
AANDAALL:- Srvpthr-Aadi/pooram-Thirupavai/Nachyr.Thrmzhi-173-BOODEVI

4000 Divyaprabantham includes :

First 1000 :: 947 by Periyazhwar/Andal/Kulskr/Trhiumzhi/Thondrdi/
Thirupanazh & Madurakavi

Second 1000 :: 1132 of Thirumangaiyazhwar

Third 1000 :: 817 by Poygai/Boodathar/Peyazhwar/Thirumazh/
Nammazhwar/Thriumangai

Fourth 1000 :: 1102 by Nammazhwar.

Their Period ranging from (Pre) Sangam to 8th Century AD.

Hearsay that when Madurakavi was probogating Nammazhwar's
Thirumozhi - Tamil Sangam wanted to accord recognition by putting
the verses on Sangapalagai and Madurakavi agreed to the
same. "Sirumuriyil (in small palm leaf) siruvaarthai (wrote KANNAN
KAZHALINIAI - ONLY 2 WORDS)- Ezhudiyittadhum " Sangapalagai
accepted the palm leaf !

Azhwars are equated with Lord Vishnu Himself !

Pranams to their Lotus Feet!
