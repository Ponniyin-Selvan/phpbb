Dear All,

Very nice to hear many interested people looking for Artist Silpi's
Paintings.

The Artist Silpi Blog is indirectly supported by the artist son
Mr.Mahalingam. All the paintings posted in the blog are taken from him
and approved by the Artist Direct Family.

Artist Son Mr Mahalingam is also promoting the work of his father for
more than 15 years now. He has taken this work so that the works of
the artist are preserved for the years to come. The works are right
now being promoted in the form of Calenders, Greeting Cards, Packet
Pictures, wall hanging, Paneling (of various size ) , collection of
different gods and goddess for Poja room etc., and in many other forms
as far as i know. The work is done with a sole intention of making
the gods ,goddess and the line drawings to reach as many homes as
possible as per the wish of Kanchi Sankaracharya Sri Chandrasekarendra
swamigal and also by the artist himself.

I too agree that the copyright needs to be protected. The email id of
Artist Silpi's son issilpi.mahalingam@...
