Hello All

Today Perarasar Rajaraja Cholan's sadhaya peruvizha started and
arranged for 2 days. In previous years it happened 1 day and local
holiday announced in the district. In my schooling days the local
holiday made us remembering the festival.
=========
Hello Vijay,

Do you also hail from Thanjavur like our Sri? Nice.
I had the Blessings of RR to attend this year's Sadhaya Vizha on Sunday,
the 24th Oct, in Big temple:

The whole Thanjavur wears a festive look. Pandal specialist "Siva" has done
up two, one at Eastern entrance like Gopuram and another inside the SW
corner to accommodate invitees. Kalasa poojas conducted in North East
mandap from there brought to the Sanctum on the back of elephant by the
chief priest. Rajaraja bronze idol - which Sri wrote about sometime back -
was kept inside the sanctum. A commentator narrated the proceedings over
megaphones.. Crown Prince HH Bonsle, Kumbabisheka chakravarthy "Ayyappa
Swamingal" Revenue & Police Officials were among the VIPs. State Tourism
had a stall.

Since morning Thevaram hyms etc. were sung. Then Sahasra Dara was conducted
and sacred waters collected in Kalasams. Poojas performed in Yagasala till
noon. Around 1230 noon, the Abhishekam peformed for Peruvudayar (The Hindu
has written already about the grandeur of Pradosham in Big temple). It
lasted for about half an hour. Unforgettable visual treat.

A truck was converted and a POP Big temple model was erected. Ethinic
musical troups, dancers came around the important places in Thanjavur on
this truck. About 50 sets of instrumental-musicians (Thaarai, Ekkalam etc.),
dancers took part. A fitting tribute to the Emperor who created Thalichery
for promoting Art and Religion in Thanjai.

Kanchi Acharya has donated a Golden Crown to the Brahnnayaki which was also
on display. Prominant establishments of Thnjai displyed posters through out
the city. Night illumination was also superb. About 20000 people would have
attended the Abhiskekam alone - from entrance to sanctum it was sea of
human heads. Some foreign Nationals were also given prominance.

If one closes his eyes and listen around, he might have transcended back
1000 years. Incidentally it is 1019th Sadhaya Vizha. The First one taken
as in 985 - the year RR ascended to throne along with Uthama. Sice year of
birth of RR is not known, his ascendancy is taken cognizance of. He
ascended to the Chozha Throne on Karkadaga (Aadi ) Purvasu - Sri Rama's
birth star - in 985. However we chose to celebrate his Birth Star only.
Hence 1019th Sadhayam!

It was cloudy thoughout. Immediately after the Abhishekam, it dizzled
outside. Cauvery is flowing around in full form ... Blessings showered ...
True to the title Ponniyinselvan !

fondly, sps
