Certainly the project is going to be benificial in many respects..
1. Lots of time saved (for ships).
2. As a result, costs of goods arriving to Tamil Nadu or India will be saved.
3. The costal districts of Tamil Nadu will be benifitted (Lots of chance for many harbour expansions. For example one in Tutucorin and Kanya Kumari Dist). People will have a change earn their livings (eg setting up warehouse etc). These advantages are going to certainly out weigh the disadvantages (example fishing). And the fisherman is going to get good life. (Note: Some people doesn't like the welfare of poor people. They want them to be alwayspoor.. VERY SAD)
4. Transportation will increase
5. Also for India's own security reasons, this should be done. We (Tamil Nadu) people's will get entrapped especially the southern part if any one start attacking using Sri Lanka as a base. China and Pakistan may be among them. Reason: Very difficult for the Navy to patrol the southern parts.


Above all these, this is prestegious project for Tamils. Its Tamil Vs others. Being a Tamil, I felt very insulted when SOMEONE issues fatwa against our CM. Dont you guys feel ashamed of all these. First Tamils should UNITE.

Thanks,
Jaga
