by priya_vaishnava on Mon Aug 23, 2004 8:06 am

Anyone reading ponniyin selvan would have wondered about the future
of the chola dynasty.It seems impossible that the cholas lost out to
pandya's in the end.It is indeed ironical that the last chola king
was named as rajarajan.

But close scrutiny of history reveals a fact.Kings who built great
temples share a boon.Their dynasties never lose.Till the last heir
their dynasties retain glory and valor.Rajaraja built the largest
known temple to shiva.His dynasty never last a war again.

Rajaraja cholans dynasty had glorious heirs like
Rajendra,rajathiraja,veera rajendra and athi rajendra.In Fact
Rajathi raja cholan,son of rajendra cholan was very valiant.His
problem was that his father did not leave him any place to
conquer.The then known world was conquered by his father.From: Ganges
to kanyakumari,from indianesia to kambodia his father conquered.So
there wasnt anyplace for rajathiraja to win.But he defeated chalukya
king parameshwara in a massive way.Parameshwara ran away from the
battlefield.

The last heir of that dynasty was athirajendra.After it the famous
vijayalaya chola's dynasty ended.Even Kulothunga who was not a
direct descendent was valiant since he had the blood of rajaraja in
him.But after his death again the dynasty changed.History would have
been different had the dynasty of vijayalaya not ended.But till the
last king,the dyansty was full of warriors and brave kings.

It is the same with Narashimavarma's dyansty.He built huge temples
in mahabalipura.His grandson rajashima pallava built huge temple for
vishnu.Their dyansty never losta war then.The dyansty changed with
abarajitha pallava.Then he lost out to adhitha cholan.

isnt this intereting?

In General it is interesting.. However, it is Kulothunga who
improved the structures of Chidambaram & Srirangam. After him the
Chozha innings was short. There is Sundarapandya meykeerthi posted
in the files section. It speaks volumes of Pandya vengence.. taken
after 400 yrs of suppression. Can anyone imagine.. the spark never
died in Pandyas for 400 yrs... There was a series of mails on
Whether Medeival Chozhas outsiders - means mix of Aryans unlike
Karikalan & predecessors.. ruled upto 6th AD. Chozha race came back
to lime light in 250 yrs, but Pandyas waited for 400 long years -
but achieved the innings.

However, most of our South Indian Kings are orthodox and God
fearing - including Chalukyas..

It is something like two sides of coin.. If a person lived long, he
was blessed by God.. If he dies early, he was relieved of the world
soon to join God early .. again Blessed!

We have hardly discussed about Mahendravarma Pallava... It appears
he is the trend settor in several accounts to many of our Kings.. He
was good administrator - warrior - and above all himself AN
ARTIST.Directly contributed so much to Sanskrit literature and
Drama - which even RR did not do..

His famous inscription "here is the temple which is not built by
wood, metal or sand .." on cave temple speaks volumes of his
vision.. and may be you are right .. of his dynasty!
