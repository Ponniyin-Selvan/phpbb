Chennai Book Fair - 2008

by abhivencat on Thu Jan 03, 2008 9:19 pm

Hi all

Chennai Book Fair starts on 4th the friday.
Cm is inaugurating it.
the location is St. Georges school opposite pAchaiyappaas college on
PH road.
on holidays timings are 11 am to 8. 30 pm
on other working days its 2. am to 8.30 pm.
true book lovers should visit the fair during the starting hours,
and can get some wonderful browsing time, free from crowds.dont worry
about the lunch for the canteen is excellent.
evenings especially during holidays would be extremely crowded though
'Bapasi' has taken some crowd control measures with an unprecedented
6 entrances.

regards

Venketesh

this year too...

by abhivencat on Fri Jan 04, 2008 9:35 pm

Hi

just back from the book fair. cM did not come because of the rains.
but the stalls are open.

10% discount is mandatory. but many give a little more because of the
change problem.

I am repeating, there are 6 entrances and 6 exits. duruing the big
crowd time you may be able to see only half the stalls.
so make it a point to visit it in the mornings on holidays, and
afternoons on working days
