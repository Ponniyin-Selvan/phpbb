wrote:

Hi All,

Please go through the contents in the web sites:

http://www.tnpsc.org/archaeology.htm
http://www.asi.nic.in/index2.asp?sublinkid=51

Looks like a full archeology course! Some Kanchi kind
of universities can think of offering such courses!!
===============================================
Our Friends' associated only conducted the said course.

DISCUSSED the Syllabus in detail yesterday with Epigraphist
Ramachandran (http://www.varalaru.com), Dr. Sthiamurthy and Dr. Rajavelu:

Introduction to Indian Epigraphy :: 2 hrs

Brahmi - 4 hrs theory + 2 hrs practical :: 6 hrs

Vattezhuthu (Pandya) etc., Copper plates reading :: 5 + 3 = 8 hrs

Tamil script :: Pallava chozha/ ltr pandya Vijayanagara - 3 hrs

Grantha - 3 hrs

Prasasthis & historical values - Pallava - Ch - Pnd-VN- 6 hrs

Indian Calender system Eras / Rgnl yrs etc., - 2 hrs

History of Tamilnadu with background Epigraphy : 4 hrs

Important inscriptions like Mahendra Caves - Uthiramerur- Bigtemple -
Tvlngud CP etc., & Historical values :: 6 hrs

Practical training for taking estempages etc., on tour : one day.

--------------------------------------

Fees is to be decided based on no. of participants.

If between 20 & 40 enrolements : Rs. 2000 per head.

(Above 40 say upto 80) : Rs. 1500 each

If we can show 100 enrolement, we can look at Rs. 1200 per head.

Below 20, no Classes.

We may have to interact with other groups and bring in other
participants.

40 hrs needed. 20 sittings of 2 hrs each. Absentees need to be
covered with a special sitting. Details to be worked out.

But this is exclusively tailor-made for our Group.

anbudan / sps
