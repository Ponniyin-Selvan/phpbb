Posted by skamalakkannan using id skamalakkannan and user unsubscribed later

Hi Friends,

I think everyone one is busy with their work. Try this puzzle in your
leisure time.

Rules:
------

1. Read and fill in Thamizh.
2. Find out the hidden word from the given phrase.
3. Select the letter in the position of question mark.
4. You have to find only one suitable letter on your own without any
clue phrase (second letter of the second word).
5. Combine all the selected letters. You will get the name of an
important character in PS. The name contains 3 words (4,5,4).
6. For example, the numbers 4(7) represents that the hidden word has
7 letters and you have to select the 4th letter. If the number is
3(5,3), then the hidden word is having 2 words with 5 and 3 letters
respectively and you have to select the 3rd letter from the beginning.

Here are the phrases.

(1). Veera paandiyanin adiyatkalin maru peyar
_ ? _ _ _ _ _ _ 2(8)

(2). Paadhukaappu tharum irattaiyargal
_ ? _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ 2(10)

(3). PS il varum oru mukkiyamaana idam
_ _ _ ? _ 4(5)

(4). Yuthathukku maru peyar
_ ? 2(2)


(5). Kadambooril irunthu vandhirukkum sathikaaran
? _ _ _ _ _ _ 1(5,2)

(6). Find this letter on your own without any phrase.
?

(7). Ponniyin selvanin paattanaar
_ _ ? _ _ 3(5)

(8). Budha madhathin kovilgalin maru peyar
_ _ ? _ 3(4)

(9). Oomai thai
_ ? _ _ _ 2(5)


(10). Kodumbalur ilavarasi
_ ? _ 2(3)

(11). Pen kulathin pon vilakku
_ ? _ _ 2(4)

(12). Veeram miguntha pattathu ilavarasan
_ ? _ _ _ _ _ _ _ 2(4,5)

(13). Sundara chozharin valathu kai
_ ? _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ 2(5,7)


Clue: 3vathu vaarthai mattumthaan ivarathu peyar. Mudhal 2
vaarthaigalum ivarin patta peyar.

Have nice time.

Thanks
Kamal
