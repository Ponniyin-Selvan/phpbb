Dear All
The recent unpleasant mails regading one of our members were something we are not usually accostomed to....A a group we have maintained decorum in spite of our differences and arguements etc....

But this is a wake up call...to all of us for our future endaevours...

A Anything we publish should have a link as the source and the source acknowledged( Balu has always done that)
B Clearly define the various defined structures associated with the group

Yahoo group: A group interested in PS,Tamil heritgae

Varalaaru: A online magazine of tamil history and heritage....independent of PS group

REACH foundation: An independent charity organisation devoted to rural education and restoration of heritage

AASAI a arm to reach foundation

Ponniyin Selvan Varalaaru Peravai: A proactive organisation which was going to provide a platform for the charitable deeds of the group....SPS proposed a working group/council but we are yet to officially take off

thebigtemple.com A site started by PS group to provide authentic info about the Bigtemple still in progress...

We have had other organisations associated with us like Sudarshanam,Kalinga tamil but they are all individual organisations in themselves...

These things become importnat now as all our messages are available on the http://www.....which I am still not comfortable personally I think it should be archived and vetted before being avaialble in the http://www...veiws welcome

Thanks
