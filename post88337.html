by snandaakumar on Wed Sep 13, 2006 1:03 am

Hi,

I was just wondering, isnt Poonguzhali the confident, strong, practical, adpatable and being the most down to earth of all. or is this all weak traits.

I had a feeling that Kalki was playing to the masses by portraying female characters as idealistic, chavunistic and manipulative. From: my point of view vanathi, kundavai & nandhini have these characteristics in them, in varying degrees, while Poonguzhali was infact loyal and with pure love to the charming prince.

She was not sure where her actual feelings were in the first couple of chapters and hence her ramblings were portrayed. Also the way she is portrayed, we may feel that she has some screws uncorked on the top side.. but the underlying point was her feeling of finding security & causing insecurity in the minds of unknown individuals.

Just another point of view, trying to turn the things around.. oops its my weakness to find unargueable points to argue against..

Regards
Nanda
