Dear Sps
so you visit thiruvengadu in aadi month

some trivia about the temple

called aadi chidambaram
puthan sthalam
mentioned in valmiki ramayana( so you can guess its age)
3 adjacent ponds with different temparatures.
chandra agni and surya kulam
plenty of inscriptions

when sambandar ( as a 4 year old) comes into the temple he visualises
the town as sivalokam and land full of sivalingkam)
uurellaam sivalokam mannellaam sivalingam

he refuses to step into the templeso the goddess comes out and
carries the child
there is a rare statue of a mother goddes carrying a child behind
the amman temple( pillai edukki nayaki)
fantastic thevarams for this place.
very close tokaviri's confluence witht the sea.

paranjothi marries a girl from this place ( in sivakamiyin sabatham)

regards

Being Aadi month - particularly on Velli (Fri - nites) I visited
some Kaalis / Durgais in the 4 Fri's:

1. Mahisasuna marthini - Pallava - Mamallapuram (near Tiger - to be
corrected as Yaali) cave. One of the best few.

2. Tiruvakkarai - Vakra Kaali - Sembiyan madevi

3. Madurakaali (Supposed to be Kannagi) Tiruvachur

4. Tirvengadu Kotravai - dancing Goddes - placed opposite to
Nataraja. This supposed to be the ancient Chozha temple. If early
Chozhas' puhar is submerged, then this is nearest to the
Kvpmpattinam.

5. Tiruvarur - Erisinakkotravai - Rajendra Chozha

6. Nisumbasoodhani - Vijayalayan (Disagreement exists) - Thanjai.
I
personally feel this as Nisumbasoodhani.

7. EKA VEERI of TIRUVALANCHUZHI.! Scholars suggest this as the
BEST
in Tamilnadu.

But re Urthuvathandava, let us do some more homework - since this
subject is Historical & Religeous.
