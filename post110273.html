Hello everyone

I saw a program on MGR in Kalaignar TV. In which, they narrated after Nadodi Mannan, MGR planned to made a film ponniyinselvan.

Even he selected the artists for almost all important characters.

Vandiathevan - MGR
Kundavi - Vaijainthi mala
Arulmozhi Varman - Gemeni Ganeshan
Vanathi - Padmini
Poonguzhali - Savithri
Manimegalai - Sarojoa Devi
Aditha Karikalar - OAK Devar
Azhvarkadiyan - Balaiah
Chinna Pazhuvettarayar - MN Nambiar
Nandini - MN Rajam
Sundara Chozhan - Nagaiah

I would like to change/include artists for some charachters.

Periya Pazhuvettarayar - PS Veerappa
Nandini - TR Rajakumari
Sendan Amudan - Muthuraman
Aniruthar - Major Sundararajan
Sundara Chozhan - Subbiah

Share ur comments

R. Srinivasa Varadharajan
